# Review Discipline

Technical writing review should check accuracy, usefulness, accessibility, and consistency.

## Review checklist

- Is the audience clear?
- Is the purpose clear?
- Are terms defined and used consistently?
- Is the document scannable?
- Do the examples match the task?
- Does the error text tell the reader how to fix the problem?

## Good signals

- The document is easy to navigate.
- The reader can recover from failure.
- The wording is concise without being cryptic.
