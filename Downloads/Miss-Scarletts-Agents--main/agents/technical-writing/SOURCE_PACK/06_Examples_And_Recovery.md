# Examples and Recovery

Examples and recovery steps turn an explanation into something the reader can act on.

## Core facts

- An example can show the reader the expected form or result.
- Recovery steps should make the next action obvious.
- Links to more documentation are useful when an explanation would otherwise become too long.

## Practical guidance

- Use examples to show the correct shape or usage.
- Provide a recovery path after describing a failure.
- Keep examples tied to the task.

## Common mistakes

- Example material that does not match the real requirement.
- Recovery instructions that are too vague to follow.
- Overloading a message with too much explanation instead of linking out.
