# Review Discipline

Analytics review should check whether the measurement actually answers the question.

## Review checklist

- Is the business question clear?
- Does the metric actually answer it?
- Are event names and properties stable?
- Are the relevant cohorts or funnels well-defined?
- Are internal users or noisy sources filtered out?
- Is the dashboard answering one question or many?

## Good signals

- The metric definition is explicit.
- The dashboard is easy to interpret.
- The analysis points to a decision.
