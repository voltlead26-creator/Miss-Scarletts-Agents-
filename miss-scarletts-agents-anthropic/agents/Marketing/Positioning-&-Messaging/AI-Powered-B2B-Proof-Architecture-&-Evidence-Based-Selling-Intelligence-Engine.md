## Overview
This engine systematically builds, curates, and deploys a structured proof architecture — mapping customer ROI data, competitive win evidence, analyst validation, and industry benchmarks to every persona, objection, and buying stage. Use it when your pipeline is stalling because buyers don't believe your claims, when sales reps are improvising proof points inconsistently, or when you're entering a new market segment and need credibility fast.

## Integration Tips
- **Salesforce / HubSpot CRM**: Tag proof assets with persona, objection, and deal stage metadata. Build a proof asset field on opportunity records to track which evidence was deployed in each deal. Correlate proof usage to win rate in reporting.
- **Highspot / Seismic / Showpad**: Structure your sales content hub using the Tier 1/2/3 proof pyramid as your content taxonomy. Set up proof "kits" that auto-surface based on opportunity stage, persona, and competitor detected in CRM.
- **Notion / Confluence**: Build a living Proof Inventory database with freshness dates, usage rights, approval status, and linked source documents. Assign quarterly proof audit as a recurring PMM task.
- **Gong / Chorus**: Mine recorded calls for spontaneous customer proof moments — phrases like "we used to spend X hours," "before Meridian," or "our old vendor" are gold. Auto-tag and route to PMM for proof capture.
- **G2 / Capterra**: Set up automated review request sequences triggered by NPS promoter score or 90-day milestone in customer success platform. Filter review asks by persona to ensure you capture technical evaluator and economic buyer voices.
- **Zapier / Make**: Automate: NPS score ≥ 9 → trigger CS manager email to ask for case study → log response in Proof Inventory Airtable → if yes, schedule PMM interview.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Customers agree to be case studies but never approve the final draft.
**Solution:** Remove the word "case study" — call it a "customer spotlight" or "success brief." Send a 3-bullet summary for approval instead of a full document. Offer to publish under their logo only, or anonymize with "a leading [industry] company." Add a "review within 5 business days or we publish the anonymized version" policy.

**Problem:** Your proof exists but sales reps don't use it — they default to product demos and feature lists.
**Solution:** Proof must be frictionless to deploy. Build a 1-page "Proof Cheat Sheet" by competitor and objection, laminated if you're in a field sales environment, pinned in your Slack sales channel. Run a weekly "proof of the week" Slack post with exact language to use. Add proof asset deployment as a mandatory field in CRM opportunity updates.

**Problem:** Existing case studies are old (2+ years) and customers have churned or been acquired.
**Solution:** Don't delete — anonymize and add temporal context: "In 2022, a mid-market manufacturing company achieved X..." Then fast-track 3 new interviews from your current top NPS promoters. In the interim, use aggregated outcome data ("Across our 47 customers...") which has no expiration issue and is often more persuasive than individual stories.
