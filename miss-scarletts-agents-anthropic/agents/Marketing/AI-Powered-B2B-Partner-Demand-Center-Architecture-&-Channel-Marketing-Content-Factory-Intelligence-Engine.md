## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot Partner Portal + CMS Hub:** Use HubSpot's CMS for partner-gated content pages. Map partner contact records to a custom "Partner Tier" property so content access automatically gates by tier. Use Lists to segment partner pipeline touches from direct pipeline.
- **Impartner or Allbound (PRM):** Sync your campaign-in-a-box program library to your PRM. Set up automated notifications when new campaigns drop so partners get push alerts, not emails they ignore. Track asset downloads and campaign activations as PRM activity scores.
- **Salesforce Partner Community:** Create a Partner Marketing Workspace with Flows that trigger content recommendations based on partner opportunity stage. When a partner creates a new Opportunity, auto-suggest the relevant campaign-in-a-box based on ICP match.
- **Canva for Teams (Brand Kit):** Lock brand colors, fonts, and logo placement. Partners can customize approved zones (their logo, contact info, local case study swap) without touching protected brand elements. Export templates as Canva links — zero design software required.
- **Zapier Automation:** Trigger: partner accesses campaign-in-a-box in portal → auto-creates a task in your channel manager's CRM → partner receives a "Your campaign is live — here's what to do in week 2" email sequence → 30 days later, trigger partner pipeline check-in.
- **Google Looker Studio:** Build a Partner Demand Center performance dashboard. Connect to HubSpot/Salesforce for pipeline data, PRM for content adoption data, and Google Analytics for landing page performance. Share with partner managers and regional channel leads weekly.
- **Seismic for Channels (LiveSend):** Use LiveSend links so you can see exactly which slides, assets, or emails a partner's prospect engaged with — feeding this back to the partner as "your buyer looked at slide 4 for 3 minutes" coaching moments.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Partners download campaign assets but never activate — content adoption rate stays below 30%.
**Solution:** The barrier is almost never motivation — it's activation energy. Reduce setup to under 15 minutes by pre-filling as many variables as possible and removing customization steps that don't move the needle. Add a "1-click activate" option for Always-On Tier 3 content where the partner does nothing except give you their email list access. Run a 60-minute "Demand Center Office Hour" webinar once per quarter and replay it as an async video — adoption spikes 2-3x within 2 weeks of live sessions.

**Problem:** AI-generated content sounds generic and partners complain it doesn't fit their voice or their clients' industry.
**Solution:** Build a "Partner Voice Calibration" questionnaire (5 questions: 3 words your clients use to describe their pain, 1 success story you're proud of, 1 phrase you never want to see in your marketing). Feed these answers into your content generation prompts as a mandatory context block. Create industry-specific content variants (financial services, healthcare, manufacturing) rather than one-size-fits-all copy — partners in regulated industries specifically need compliance-safe language that generic content never provides.

**Problem:** MDF budget goes unspent because partners don't know how to submit claims or what qualifies.
**Solution:** Redesign MDF as an "investment pre-approval" model rather than a reimbursement model. Partner selects a campaign-in-a-box, submits a 3-field pre-approval form (expected reach, target account list, timeline), and gets approved within 24 hours. Funds are committed before the campaign runs, removing the reimbursement risk that kills partner participation. Track pre-approved commitments vs. actual utilization and report this in your monthly channel marketing dashboard as a leading indicator of pipeline health.
