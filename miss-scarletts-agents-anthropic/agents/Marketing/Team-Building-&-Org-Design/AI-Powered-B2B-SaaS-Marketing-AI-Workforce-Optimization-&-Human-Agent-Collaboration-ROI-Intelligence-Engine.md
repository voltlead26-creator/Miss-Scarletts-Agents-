## Troubleshooting

**Problem: CFO rejects the AI investment case because "you can't guarantee the savings."**
Solution: Reframe from "cost savings" to "capacity creation." Instead of projecting headcount reduction (which requires a hiring freeze or attrition), project the additional pipeline value of deploying current FTEs on strategic work freed up by AI. At $145K/FTE, if AI frees 30% of a demand gen manager's time, that's $43,500 of human capital reallocated to higher-ROI activities. Present ROI as "value created" not "cost cut" — CFOs in growth-stage SaaS respond better to pipeline math than headcount math.

**Problem: Top performer (senior content writer) threatens to quit when AI deployment is announced.**
Solution: The announcement sequence matters more than the decision itself. Brief your top performers 1:1 before any team announcement — give them the context, show them their new AI-augmented role description, and frame it as "you now have AI doing the grunt work so you can focus on strategy." Offer a formal "AI Champion" title and the opportunity to lead Phase 1 tool selection. Senior creatives who become internal AI advocates stay; senior creatives who feel blindsided leave. Budget 2 weeks for 1:1 change management conversations before any all-hands announcement.

**Problem: AI-generated content quality is inconsistent — some outputs are great, others require near-complete rewrites.**
Solution: Inconsistency almost always traces back to unclear brand voice documentation and AI system prompt quality, not AI capability limitations. Before scaling AI content production, invest 2-3 days in creating a comprehensive Brand Voice & AI Style Guide that includes: 5 writing samples with editorial commentary explaining *why* each sample is on-brand, a glossary of 20-30 brand-specific phrases and their AI-unfriendly alternatives, and explicit instructions for tone across content types (thought leadership vs. sales vs. customer success). Feed this guide into every AI agent system prompt. You'll typically see consistent output quality within 2 weeks of implementation.
