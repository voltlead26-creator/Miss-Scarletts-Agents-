## Integration Tips

- **LinkedIn Talent Insights:** Use LinkedIn's Talent Insights tool to validate candidate persona sizing — confirm there are enough people matching your ICP before building a full campaign targeting that segment
- **Greenhouse / Lever / Ashby (ATS):** Add EVP language to every automated email touchpoint in your ATS — offer emails, confirmation emails, rejection emails all shape employer brand perception
- **Glassdoor Employer Center:** Set up real-time alerts for new reviews; use the response templates generated here to respond within 48 hours — response rate is a public signal of culture
- **LinkedIn Career Pages:** Upload employee testimonial videos (30-60 sec, shot on iPhone is fine) directly to LinkedIn Career Pages — organic reach on these is 3-5x higher than text posts
- **Notion / Confluence:** Store your EVP framework as a "Brand Bible for Recruiting" and share with every hiring manager — ensures consistent messaging across 50+ interview conversations per month
- **Zapier automation:** Connect new Glassdoor reviews → Slack channel → assign owner for 48-hour response SLA
- **HubSpot CRM (Talent Community):** Build a talent nurture sequence in HubSpot for silver-medal candidates (strong candidates who weren't hired) — re-engage with content every 60 days; these are your warmest future applicants

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The EVP generated sounds great but current employees don't recognize it as authentic.
**Solution:** Before finalizing EVP, run a 5-question internal survey ("What made you join?" "What makes you stay?" "What would make you leave?") — use verbatim employee language in the EVP, not marketing language. The best EVPs quote real employees directly. If there's a gap between EVP aspiration and reality, fix the reality first (even one meaningful change) before amplifying the message.

**Problem:** LinkedIn posts get engagement from current employees but no traction with target candidates.
**Solution:** Stop posting from the company page and start posting from the CEO/CTO/VP Eng LinkedIn profiles. Algorithmic reach on personal profiles is 5-10x higher than company pages for the same content. Train 3 executives with the content toolkit; ghostwrite for them if needed. Post at 7-9am Tuesday–Thursday for max B2B candidate reach.

**Problem:** Offer acceptance rate is still below 75% even after implementing the EVP and marketing funnel.
**Solution:** Run a 5-question survey with every candidate who declined an offer in the past 6 months (via LinkedIn or email — most will respond). The data almost always reveals 1-2 systemic issues (compensation gap, specific competitor perception, process friction). Fix the top issue before optimizing marketing. Employer brand cannot overcome a structural comp or culture problem — it can only amplify reality.
