## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Managers resist using the IDP framework, citing time constraints. Development plans exist on paper but aren't followed.**
Solution: The failure point is usually that IDPs are treated as HR compliance rather than management tools. Reframe them as "how I help my best people stay." Reduce the IDP to a single page. Make the manager's commitments section mandatory (what YOU will do, not just what they will do). Tie IDP completion and follow-through to the manager's own performance review — if their direct reports don't have active IDPs, it counts against the manager's leadership score. When IDPs become visible in performance reviews, completion rates typically jump from 40% to 85% within one quarter.

**Problem: Career ladders are built but high performers still leave, citing "no path forward" despite documented levels.**
Solution: The issue is usually that promotion criteria exist but advancement is still perceived as political or unclear. Fix this by making the criteria observable and reviewable: each quarter, a marketer should be able to self-assess against the promotion criteria checklist and show their manager exactly where they stand. If someone meets all criteria and isn't promoted within 60 days, there must be a documented reason (headcount freeze, budget constraint) and a clear alternate timeline. Ambiguity kills trust — clarity retains talent even when the answer is "not yet."

**Problem: The AI fluency framework creates anxiety — some team members feel they're being evaluated on whether AI will replace them.**
Solution: Reframe explicitly and repeatedly: AI fluency requirements are about making their jobs easier and their output more impressive, not about replaceable vs. irreplaceable. Show concrete examples where AI fluency led to promotions and salary increases (not layoffs). The framing that works: "We're investing in you becoming an AI operator — someone who can manage AI systems that used to require multiple FTEs. That makes you more valuable, not less." Pair every AI fluency milestone with a visible reward (conference, stretch assignment, comp adjustment) to reinforce the positive association.
