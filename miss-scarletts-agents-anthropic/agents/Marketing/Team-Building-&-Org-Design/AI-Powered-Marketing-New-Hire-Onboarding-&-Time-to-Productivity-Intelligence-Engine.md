## Integration Tips

- **BambooHR / Workday:** Trigger the onboarding plan template on "Offer Accepted" status. Auto-assign 30-day tasks to the hiring manager and new hire on their first day. Set automated reminders for each checkpoint.
- **HubSpot:** Create a new hire contact record and enroll them in an internal "Marketing Team Onboarding" email sequence — auto-sending daily tips, resources, and check-in prompts for the first 30 days.
- **Slack:** Build a `/onboarding-bot` workflow that DMs the new hire each Monday morning with their weekly priorities, reminds them of scheduled stakeholder meetings, and pings their manager at Day 28/58/88 to initiate retrospective scheduling.
- **Salesforce:** Create a custom "First Pipeline Contribution" field on opportunity records — tracking which opps the new hire touched as a leading indicator of ramp velocity.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The new hire completes the 30-day plan tasks but still feels unclear on strategic priorities.**
*Solution:* The Day 5 "First Impressions" memo and Day 30 "State of [Function]" report are the diagnostic tools. If either deliverable is vague or lacks specific recommendations, it signals the manager hasn't provided enough strategic context. Run an emergency 90-minute "Strategy Alignment" session where the CMO presents the 3-year vision, 12-month plan, and the new hire's specific contribution to each.

**Problem: The stakeholder map generates superficial relationship advice (e.g., "meet with Sales VP").**
*Solution:* Add specificity to the Advanced prompt: name the actual people, their known concerns, their current perception of marketing, and any political sensitivities. The AI produces dramatically more targeted relationship-building language when given real names and context rather than titles.

**Problem: The onboarding plan is too generic for a highly specialized role (e.g., a Marketing Data Scientist or a Lifecycle Marketing Engineer).**
*Solution:* In the Advanced prompt, replace the functional specialization field with 5–7 specific technical skills (e.g., "Python for marketing analytics, Segment CDP data architecture, SQL for cohort analysis, Amplitude product analytics"). The more technical the role, the more specific the inputs must be. Also specify the first technical deliverable expected (e.g., "audit current attribution model and recommend improvements") so the AI can reverse-engineer the learning path needed to get there.
