## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High MQL volume but low CMO/executive deal sponsor involvement — deals stuck at Marketing Ops level.**
Fix: Implement the Champion Development Kit from Phase 3 immediately. The root cause is usually: (1) your demo and trial experience only satisfies a practitioner, not an executive; (2) you have no CMO-to-CMO social proof content; (3) your ROI model isn't CFO-ready. Create a "CMO Briefing Package" — a 5-minute video + 1-page executive summary your Marketing Ops champion can forward to their CMO today. Offer an executive briefing call with your CEO or CMO for any deal with $50K+ ACV potential.

**Problem: Benchmark report gets downloads but low conversion to demo — practitioners are taking the research without converting.**
Fix: The report is creating awareness but not enough urgency. Add a personalized benchmark score — after download, run the prospect through a 5-question interactive benchmark that shows them how their performance compares to peers. Personalized data creates urgency that generic reports don't. Also add a "Your Personalized Action Plan" as a gated upgrade to the benchmark score — this earns the demo request in context.

**Problem: Losing deals to "we'll build it ourselves" or "we'll use our existing HubSpot/Salesforce reporting."**
Fix: This is a positioning problem, not a sales problem. You need a "build vs. buy" calculator that makes DIY look expensive when the true cost of engineering time, maintenance, and accuracy is included. Also create a "Weeks to Deploy vs. Months to Build" comparison — MarTech buyers respond to speed-to-value. Finally, offer a free 30-day proof-of-concept with a specific, measurable outcome defined upfront: "In 30 days, we'll show you $X in unattributed pipeline — or we'll give you your money back."
