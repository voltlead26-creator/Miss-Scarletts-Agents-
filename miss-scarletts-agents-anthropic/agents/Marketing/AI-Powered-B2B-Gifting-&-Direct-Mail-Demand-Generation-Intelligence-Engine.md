Use Postal's "Send on Signal" automation to fire Tier 1 gifts when a CRM field (e.g., "ICP Score > 80") is updated. Set up Sendoso campaigns to auto-enroll contacts into Outreach/Salesloft sequences when tracking confirms delivery.

- **HubSpot:** Use Workflows to trigger gift sends via Postal's HubSpot app when Deal Stage = "Demo Scheduled" and Activity Date is > 5 days ago (no-show indicator). Tag contacts with "Gifting Program" list for suppression from standard email sequences.
- **Outreach / Salesloft:** Build a dedicated gifting cadence that starts with the pre-ship email template. Use delivery webhook from Postal/Sendoso to auto-advance contacts to Step 2 (Day 0 email) without manual intervention.
- **Zapier:** If not using a gifting platform, use Zapier to connect Crunchbase/LinkedIn Sales Navigator → Slack alert → Google Sheets gift tracker → manual fulfillment queue. Set up a Zap to log Amazon tracking numbers to Salesforce Activity records.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Low email open rates on post-gift sequences (< 25%)**
Solution: Your subject lines are too transactional. The best-performing gifting email subjects are curiosity-based and don't reference the gift explicitly in the subject line. Test "Heads up, [First Name]" and "Something's headed your way" vs. longer descriptive subjects. Also ensure your SDR's email domain has proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC setup — gifting emails sometimes trigger spam filters due to shipment tracking URLs in the body.

**Problem: High gift undeliverable rate (> 15% returned)**
Solution: Implement address pre-confirmation for gifts over $50. Send a brief email 3 days before shipment: "Quick question before I send something — is [LinkedIn company address] still your office, or are you remote? Want to make sure it lands in the right place." This also adds a touchpoint and increases open rates on subsequent emails.

**Problem: SDRs not following up consistently after gifts are delivered**
Solution: Remove manual follow-up entirely — automate it. Use delivery webhooks from your gifting platform to auto-trigger email sequences in Outreach/Salesloft the moment tracking confirms delivery. Build a Salesforce dashboard that shows "Gifts Delivered — No Follow-up in 48 Hours" and make it part of the weekly SDR pipeline review.
