**Output Example (Excerpt):**

- BANNED: "AI-powered"
- Why it's hurting: In 2026, this phrase triggers immediate skepticism. Buyers hear it as "we added a ChatGPT wrapper."
- Replace with: "trained on 847M B2B sales conversations" — specific, credible, not easily claimed by newcomers.

## Troubleshooting
**Problem:** The prompt outputs differentiation claims I don't have proof for yet.
**Solution:** This is intentional — the Credibility Ladder deliverable surfaces the proof gap. Treat unproven claims as a content roadmap, not a messaging roadmap. Only activate claims you can back with evidence today; put the rest into the 60-90 day plan.

**Problem:** All six differentiation vectors score 2-3 — I'm in true parity with competitors.
**Solution:** Add the "Category Escape" parameter to the Advanced Version: ask the AI to help you reframe the category entirely so your parity features become the category definition. This is the April Dunford "repositioning" play — instead of competing on feature dimensions everyone has, define a new frame where you're the reference point. Also use the Category Design Market Creation Engine prompt.

**Problem:** My sales team ignores the new messaging and reverts to old talking points.
**Solution:** Run the Advanced Version again with sales call transcripts as input and ask specifically for "sales-friendly versions" of each differentiation claim — one sentence, objection-handling ready, with a specific proof story attached. Messaging that sales can tell as a story will be used; messaging that reads like copy will be ignored.
