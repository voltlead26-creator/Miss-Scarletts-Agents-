## Troubleshooting

**Problem: TL intent scores are accumulating but no pipeline is being created; nurture sequences are not converting to meetings**
Solution: The most common cause is misaligned content-to-CTA mapping — TL audiences respond to educational content but the sequences are pushing hard demos too early. Audit Track B emails: if you're asking for a demo before delivering 3+ value-add content touches, restructure sequences to follow a "3 gives, 1 ask" rule. Add a soft CTA option (attend a live event, read a related piece) alongside the primary demo CTA for contacts scoring 26-55.

**Problem: Dark funnel de-anonymization rates are below 15% (not worth the pipeline effort)**
Solution: Check that RB2B/Clearbit pixels are firing correctly on *all* TL-linked pages — specifically verify that LinkedIn-shared links redirect through your tracked domain rather than directly to a PDF. If the pixel can't fire on PDF views, create HTML landing pages for all white papers with the PDF embedded (not downloadable directly). Also audit your ICP definition: if your audience is predominantly outside the US or at small companies (<50 employees), RB2B coverage rates drop significantly.

**Problem: Executive doesn't have time to engage with post-comment ICP engagers; opportunities are going cold**
Solution: Implement a "comment-to-task" workflow — use a tool like Shield Analytics or Taplio to monitor executive LinkedIn posts; when a comment from an ICP-matched profile is detected, auto-create a Salesforce task for the executive's EA or a designated SDR with the pre-written response template. The executive approves with a single click rather than drafting. This reduces active time from 15 min/post to 2 min/post.
