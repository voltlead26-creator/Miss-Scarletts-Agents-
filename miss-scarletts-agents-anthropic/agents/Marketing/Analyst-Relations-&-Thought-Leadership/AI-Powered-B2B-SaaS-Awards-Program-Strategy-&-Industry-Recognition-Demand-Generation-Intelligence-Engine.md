## Troubleshooting

**Issue: We keep losing award submissions — strong product, weak submissions**
Fix: Most submissions fail because they lead with features instead of customer outcomes. Restructure your submission opening: instead of "Our platform uses AI to analyze pipeline data," write "NorthStar HCM's customers reduced time-to-close by 31% and improved forecast accuracy from 62% to 89% — here's how." Judges read hundreds of submissions; lead with the number that makes them stop scrolling. Add this constraint to your submission-writing prompt: "Every claim must be followed by a specific metric within one sentence. No claim is allowed without evidence."

**Issue: Our G2 review generation campaigns feel spammy and get ignored**
Fix: The highest-converting G2 review requests come from CSMs at the moment of a customer success milestone — not from mass email blasts. Instrument 3 success signals in your product: (1) first time a customer hits their defined success metric, (2) completion of a QBR with documented outcomes, (3) NPS response of 9-10. Trigger a single personalized email from the CSM within 24 hours of each signal. "Hey [Name], I saw you just hit 3x pipeline coverage for the first time — that's huge. Would you share that win on G2? Here's a direct link." Response rate is 4-8x higher than batch-and-blast.

**Issue: We won an award but got no pipeline impact — it just sat on our website**
Fix: An award on your website is a passive signal. Awards accelerate pipeline when they appear in the moment of buyer decision-making: in retargeting ads when a prospect is in evaluation, in the sales one-pager during a competitive deal, in the outbound email to a cold account as a credibility opener. Build a 72-hour activation checklist: update 3 sales assets (deck, one-pager, email template), launch a LinkedIn retargeting campaign, send a targeted email to active pipeline contacts, update the homepage hero, and post from the CEO's personal LinkedIn. If you do nothing beyond the press release, you capture 5% of the award's value. The activation is where the other 95% lives.
