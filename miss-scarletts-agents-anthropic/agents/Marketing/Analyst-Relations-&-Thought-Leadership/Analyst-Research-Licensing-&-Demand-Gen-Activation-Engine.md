## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Our position in the report isn't flattering — we're a Niche Player or Contender, not a Leader.**
Fix: Reframe the narrative around criteria where you scored highest, not the quadrant position. Buyers rarely read reports end-to-end — they scan for their priority criteria. Pull the specific criteria scores that favor you (e.g., "highest score in Ease of Implementation" or "top score in Customer Satisfaction") and build campaigns around those data points. You can also position the report as "the market is validating our category" without making your quadrant placement the hero — use the report to establish category urgency, then use your own case studies and ROI data to establish vendor preference.

**Issue: Legal/compliance flagged our use of analyst content in paid ads — our license doesn't cover it.**
Fix: Contact the analyst firm's licensing team immediately (not your research contact — the licensing team). Paid media licenses typically cost $5K–$25K extra per quarter depending on firm and impression cap. Calculate your expected CPL and pipeline influence — if a $10K license upgrade generates 50 MQLs at a $15K/opportunity pipeline value, the math almost always works. In the meantime, run ads to your own landing page copy that doesn't quote the report directly — use your own positioning and offer the report as the asset, so you can still drive downloads without violating the license.

**Issue: Sales team isn't using the assets — the report has been shared but AEs are ignoring it.**
Fix: Run a mandatory 20-minute Sales Enablement session within 5 days of launch. Show 2-3 recorded examples of how to use the report in a call (role-play or real Gong snippet). Load the talk track and email templates directly into their sequence tool (Outreach/Salesloft) so there's zero friction to use them. Create a Slack channel that posts "analyst report success stories" — when an AE wins a deal where the report was used, celebrate it publicly. Sales teams adopt tools that help them close deals; make it frictionless and show proof that it works.
