## Overview
Deploys an AI agent that designs and executes end-to-end B2B tech PR campaigns — from building a personalized journalist target list and crafting embargo-ready press releases to generating individual pitch angles for each reporter and sequencing follow-up outreach. Use this when you're preparing a major announcement (funding round, product launch, partnership, executive hire) and need to maximize earned coverage, or when you want to build a sustained media relations program that generates compounding press authority and attributable pipeline.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Journalist opened email but didn't respond or cover the story.**
Solution: Wait 48 hours, then send a single follow-up that adds one new data point not in the original pitch — a specific customer quote, a new stat, or a tie to a news event that happened since you sent the original. Never send a "just following up" with no new value. If still no response after two attempts, move them to a "warm but not ready" list and engage with their content on social over the next 90 days before pitching again.

**Problem: Exclusive journalist received the brief but is asking for more time than the embargo allows.**
Solution: This is a negotiation. You can offer to push the embargo 24 hours if the journalist commits in writing to a publish date. If they won't commit, release the Tier 2 list on the original schedule — never hold a full announcement hostage for an uncertain exclusive. A Tier 2 placement is better than a delayed announcement with no coverage.

**Problem: Press release went out but coverage is minimal — only wire pickups, no original reporting.**
Solution: Wire distribution creates SEO-indexed content but rarely drives original journalist coverage. Analyze which tier 2 pitches didn't get responses and conduct a post-mortem on the pitch angles. The most common cause is that the announcement angle was too company-centric. Identify the most provocative market stat or customer insight from the announcement and pitch a standalone data story to 5-8 journalists 2 weeks after the announcement — "The announcement was just the beginning; here's the data behind why this market is exploding."
