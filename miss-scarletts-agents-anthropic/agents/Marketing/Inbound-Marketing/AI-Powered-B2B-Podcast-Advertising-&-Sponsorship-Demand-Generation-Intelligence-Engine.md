## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Promo code redemption rates are consistently below 0.2% across all shows**
*Solution:* This almost always indicates an offer problem, not a show selection problem. Audit the CTA offer: Is there a compelling incentive beyond a standard trial? Add tangible value (extended trial, free onboarding call, custom benchmark report). Also verify the URL is short and memorable — listeners can't click, so complex URLs kill conversions. Test a simpler 4-character promo code.

**Problem: High promo code redemptions but very few qualified demo requests**
*Solution:* The show audience is not matching your ICP despite the surface-level category fit. Pull the Podtrac data and ask the host for audience survey data. You may be reaching adjacent roles (e.g., accounting staff instead of CFOs) or company sizes outside your sweet spot. Tighten your scoring criteria for new shows and add an explicit company size question to your trial signup form to filter unqualified leads.

**Problem: Unable to get verified download data from a show you want to sponsor**
*Solution:* This is a hard dealbreaker for any significant budget commitment. For micro shows ($2,000–$3,000/quarter commitment), you can accept Spotify for Podcasters screenshots as proof. For any commitment >$5,000, require Podtrac or Chartable certified data. If a host refuses, offer a small one-episode test at a heavily discounted rate rather than a full commitment. No reputable show with real downloads will refuse to share verified data.
