- Export keyword difficulty and volume data to validate AI estimates before publishing
- Use Site Explorer to confirm competitor content gaps identified in Deliverable 4

**Google Search Console:**
- Connect to track impressions, clicks, and average position for each published piece

- Create a HubSpot workflow: if contact's first touch = organic blog → enroll in SEO nurture sequence

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI-generated keyword volumes feel inaccurate or too broad**
Fix: Use the AI output as a directional framework only. Validate every keyword in Ahrefs, Semrush, or Google Keyword Planner before committing to the roadmap. The cluster architecture and prioritization logic are more valuable than the volume estimates — treat volumes as relative comparisons, not absolute targets.

**Problem: Pillar page not ranking after 90 days**
Fix: Check three things in order: (1) Is the page indexed? Check GSC for crawl errors or noindex tags. (2) Is the content comprehensively covering the topic? Compare word count and subtopics vs. current top 3 rankings using a tool like Clearscope. (3) Does the page have enough internal links? The pillar should receive links from every cluster piece plus homepage navigation or resource hub. Add 10+ internal links pointing to the pillar before assuming authority issues.

**Problem: Organic traffic is growing but leads are not converting**
Fix: The CTA-to-funnel-stage alignment is likely off. Run a quick audit: (a) Are TOFU posts offering low-friction CTAs like newsletter or content download, or are they asking for a demo? (b) Are BOFU comparison/pricing pages driving to a demo request, not a generic contact form? (c) Is the lead capture mechanism (form, landing page) loading correctly on mobile? Start by adding a content upgrade (downloadable template or checklist) to your top 3 traffic-driving posts and measure the lift within 30 days.
