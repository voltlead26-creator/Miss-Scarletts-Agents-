## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Homepage conversion rate is below 1.5% despite ICP traffic**
Solution: Run a 5-second test with your ICP persona using UsabilityHub — if they can't describe your product and primary CTA after 5 seconds, your hero message needs to be simpler. Focus only on the above-the-fold section before touching anything else. The hero accounts for 60–70% of homepage CRO impact.

**Problem: Can't get statistically significant A/B test results because of low traffic**
Solution: If you have fewer than 5,000 monthly homepage visitors, skip A/B testing and use qualitative methods first: 5 user interviews with ICP prospects, Hotjar heatmaps for 2 weeks, and a Wynter message testing survey. Use these insights to make a single high-confidence page redesign rather than incremental A/B tests.

**Problem: Demo requests are high but SQL rate from homepage is poor (<30%)**
Solution: Your homepage is converting broad traffic, not ICP-fit traffic. Add friction to your demo request flow: include one qualifying question ("How large is your sales team?") to increase ICP specificity. Also add a logo grid and case study above the fold to self-filter non-ICP visitors before they submit.
