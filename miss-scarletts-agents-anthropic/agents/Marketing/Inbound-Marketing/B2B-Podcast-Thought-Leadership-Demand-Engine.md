## Troubleshooting
- **Problem:** Downloads are growing but zero pipeline attribution — no one from the podcast is requesting demos.
  **Solution:** The conversion architecture is likely missing or misaligned. Audit three things: (1) Are you capturing email addresses, or only tracking anonymous RSS listeners? Without email capture, you cannot attribute pipeline. Implement at least one lead magnet opt-in per episode with a post-download nurture sequence. (2) Is your primary CTA relevant to where listeners are in their journey? If they are awareness-stage listeners and you are pitching a demo, conversion will be near zero. Add a mid-funnel offer (assessment tool, template, research report) as your primary CTA for the first 90 days. (3) Are UTM parameters being passed correctly through your funnel? Test every UTM link end-to-end monthly to confirm your CRM is capturing source data.

- **Problem:** Guest booking rate is below 20% — most outreach goes unanswered.
  **Solution:** Three root causes: wrong guest tier, wrong message, wrong channel. Start with channel — LinkedIn DMs outperform cold email 3:1 for podcast booking if you have a shared connection or have recently engaged with their content. Fix the message by removing all self-promotional language from the first sentence — lead with something specific to them (a post, a talk, a recent company milestone). Fix the tier by starting with Tier 3 guests (emerging practitioners, easier to book) and using those published episodes as social proof to land Tier 1 guests in months 3-6. Also consider offering a Calendly with one-click booking — reducing friction increases response rates by 40%.

- **Problem:** Content repurposing takes too long — the team publishes the episode but never creates the social content.
  **Solution:** The bottleneck is almost always the blank-page problem, not time. Solve it by building AI-assisted templates using your actual episode structure. Immediately after recording, use Riverside or Otter to generate the transcript, then run it through a pre-built Claude/ChatGPT prompt (your repurposing chain from Deliverable 5) that outputs all seven content formats in one session. The entire repurposing chain for one episode should take under 45 minutes of human review time when AI produces the first draft. Batch all repurposing work into a single 3-hour weekly block rather than spreading it across the week — context switching is the real time killer.
