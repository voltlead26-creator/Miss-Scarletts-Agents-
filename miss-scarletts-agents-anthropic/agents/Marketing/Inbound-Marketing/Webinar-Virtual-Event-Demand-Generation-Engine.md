## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Low show rate (below 30%)**
Fix: Show rate is primarily a function of email frequency and day-of communication. Add a T-2 hours "we're going live soon" text/SMS if your MAP supports it. Ensure your calendar ICS file is included in the T-7 confirmation email, not just the registration confirmation. Test webinar time — Thursday 11am ET and Tuesday 12pm ET consistently outperform Monday or Friday slots for B2B audiences.

**Problem: High registration, low MQL conversion from hot leads**
Fix: Your hot lead definition may be too broad, or the BDR follow-up is too generic. Segment by engagement score (answered poll + stayed 75%+ = true hot lead). Rewrite the BDR email to reference a specific moment from the webinar ("You stayed for the part where Jordan showed the procurement audit framework — that's exactly what I'd want to walk you through for [COMPANY]."). Generic "thanks for attending" emails convert at 2-3%; personalized reference-the-content emails convert at 12-18%.

**Problem: Content repurposing takes too long and assets go stale**
Fix: Record a 15-minute "content debrief" immediately after the webinar where the speaker pulls out 3 key moments. Feed the transcript and debrief to your AI assistant to draft all 8 assets the same day. Assign a single owner with a 5-business-day SLA. The replay blog post alone drives 40-60% of post-event registrations for the replay — it should publish within 48 hours.
