## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Creators have large followings but generating zero MQLs**
Solution: The creator's audience doesn't match your ICP. Before activating, use SparkToro or Shield Analytics to validate that 40%+ of their engaged audience holds your target job titles. Pivot to smaller creators with tighter ICP concentration rather than chasing vanity follower counts.

**Problem: Creator-sourced leads are low quality (poor MQL-to-opportunity conversion)**
Solution: The offer isn't aligned with where creator audiences are in the buyer journey. Most creator audiences are in awareness/consideration stage — not decision stage. Replace bottom-funnel offers (book a demo) with top-funnel value offers (benchmark report, ROI calculator, free tool) and nurture creator leads with a dedicated 6-email sequence before SDR outreach.

**Problem: Creators are asking for editorial control and refusing to follow the content brief**
Solution: This is actually a feature, not a bug — audiences trust creators because they speak authentically. Shift from scripted briefs to "talking point menus" (3-5 points they can weave in naturally) and only require mandatory elements (disclosure language, UTM link). Give creators creative freedom on format, hook, and framing. The best creator partnerships feel native, not advertorial.
