## Troubleshooting

**Problem: TikTok CPLs are 2x LinkedIn CPLs in the first 30 days.**
Solution: This is normal — TikTok's algorithm needs 50+ conversion events per ad group to exit the learning phase. Consolidate ad groups to concentrate budget, lower your bid to Lowest Cost (no cap), and focus first 30 days on video view campaigns to build pixel data before optimizing for lead gen conversions. CPLs typically drop 35-50% after the learning phase completes.

**Problem: Lead quality from TikTok Instant Forms is poor — low sales qualification rate.**
Solution: Add 1-2 qualification questions to your Instant Form (company size, primary pain point), switch from "Volume" to "Higher Intent" form type, and tighten your retargeting audience to website visitors and video viewers only. Also brief your SDR team: TikTok leads often need 2-3 more touchpoints than LinkedIn leads before converting — update the follow-up sequence to 7 touches over 14 days.

**Problem: Video creatives are getting views but no click-through to the website.**
Solution: Your hook is working but your CTA is unclear or too soft. Add a visible text overlay at 15 seconds and 25 seconds ("Link in bio ↑") and end the video with a direct verbal CTA ("Go to the link in bio right now — 2-minute setup, no credit card"). Also test replacing "Learn More" CTA button with "Try Free" or "Get Started" — action-oriented CTAs outperform curiosity CTAs for BOFU-intent ads by 20-40%.
