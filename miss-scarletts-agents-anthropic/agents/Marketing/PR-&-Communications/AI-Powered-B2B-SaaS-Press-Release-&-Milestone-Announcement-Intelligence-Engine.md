## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Press release goes live on wire but gets zero media pickup.**
Solution: The lede likely buried the news. Journalists read first sentence only — if that sentence doesn't answer "why does this matter today?" they move on. Rewrite using the "inverted pyramid": put the most surprising or largest-scale fact first. Also check your wire distribution categories — if you checked "general business" instead of your specific vertical, you missed your target audience. Activate the follow-up pitch sequence (Deliverable 6) within 4 hours of wire live with a new angle offer.

**Problem: CEO quote sounds like every other press release.**
Solution: Record a 10-minute audio or video interview with the CEO answering: "What would you tell a journalist who asks why this announcement matters to someone outside your industry?" Transcribe and extract the 2-3 most surprising or counterintuitive statements. Those are your quotes. Corporate press releases fail because quotes are written by PR teams, not pulled from authentic executive voice. Use the AI to transcribe → extract → tighten to 50 words.

**Problem: Embargo broke early on social media by a conference attendee or employee.**
Solution: Implement the embargo break protocol immediately: (1) Contact your exclusive outlet journalist within 15 minutes with an apology and offer to move their publish time up immediately; (2) Email all embargoed journalists that the news is now live and their story can publish; (3) Push wire release live immediately rather than waiting for scheduled time; (4) Post CEO social content within 30 minutes. Speed is now your only asset — the exclusive relationship damage is done, but you can minimize pickup loss by getting the official narrative live fast.
