## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Show rates below 30%**
Causes: Too much time between registration and event (>14 days), generic reminder emails, no pre-event value drip, wrong day/time for audience.
Fix: Shorten registration window to 7–10 days. Send a "pre-read" or insight teaser 48 hours before that creates a genuine reason to attend live. Test Tuesday–Thursday 11am ET or 2pm ET. Add SMS reminder for Day-of if permitted.

**Problem: Attendees don't convert to pipeline after the event**
Causes: Wrong audience (too educational, wrong seniority), weak CTA during the event, follow-up sequence too generic, SDR outreach delayed >48 hours.
Fix: Redesign 1 webinar per quarter specifically for BOFU intent (competitive displacement, ROI proof, live demo). Implement engagement scoring immediately — SDRs must follow up Hot attendees within 24 hours. Personalize follow-up emails to reference specific content from the session (not just "thanks for attending").

**Problem: Topics running dry after 3–4 episodes**
Causes: Content strategy not tied to ICP's evolving JTBD, no systematic input from sales/CS, topics chosen based on what marketing wants to talk about vs. what buyers want to learn.
Fix: Set up a monthly "topic harvesting" session with 3 sales reps and 1 CSM. Pull top 10 questions from Gong/Chorus/Clari from the last 30 days. Use community listening (LinkedIn comments, Slack communities, Reddit) to surface emergent buyer conversations. Build a 12-month editorial calendar with built-in flexibility for 2 reactive topics per quarter.
