## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Listeners aren't clicking companion content CTAs even when mentioned in episodes.
**Solution:** CTA placement and language are the most common culprits. Test moving the CTA to the 60-70% mark of the episode (not just the end — most listeners don't finish). Rewrite CTA copy using the "selfish gift" framework: make it about their problem, not your asset. Example — instead of "Download our template," try "Here's the exact framework I wish someone had handed me in my first demand gen role." Also ensure the CTA is in the episode description on every platform with a clickable link — many listeners find companion content by reading show notes, not from the audio CTA.

**Problem:** Companion content downloads aren't converting to pipeline — lots of MQLs that sales rejects.
**Solution:** Your Tier 2 CTAs may not be ICP-signal content. Apply the "specificity test" — ask: would a random person outside your ICP find this valuable? If yes, it's a Tier 1 asset (too broad). Redesign Tier 2 assets around problems only your buyer has. For example, "5 Email Templates" is Tier 1. "The Marketing Attribution Audit for B2B SaaS CMOs Reporting to a CFO Who Only Believes Last-Click" is Tier 2 — only your buyer wants that. Also review the lead scoring model: ensure the MQL threshold requires Firmographic match (job title + company size) in addition to behavioral points — behavioral signals alone will inflate MQL volume with non-buyers.

**Problem:** Can't track which podcast episodes are driving pipeline because listeners aren't identified until they fill out a form weeks later.
**Solution:** Implement a "progressive identification" strategy. First, create a free listener community (Slack, Circle, or LinkedIn Group) as a Tier 1 CTA — this identifies listeners at scale for minimal friction. Second, add a "How did you hear about us?" field to your demo request and trial forms with "Podcast" as an option, and a free-text field for episode name. Third, train your sales team to ask "How did you first hear about us?" in every discovery call and log it in CRM. These three tactics will capture podcast attribution retroactively for high-intent buyers who never engaged with companion content before requesting a demo.
