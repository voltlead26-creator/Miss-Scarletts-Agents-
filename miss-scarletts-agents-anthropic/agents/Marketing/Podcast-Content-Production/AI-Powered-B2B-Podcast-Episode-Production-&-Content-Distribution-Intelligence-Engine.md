## PRODUCTION REQUIREMENTS

### 1. EPISODE TITLE SUITE (5 Options)
Write one title per formula:
- **Formula A — Specific Outcome**: "[Number] Ways [Target Persona] Are [Achieving Specific Result] Without [Common Painful Method]"
- **Formula B — Provocative Claim**: "Why [Widely Accepted Belief] Is Wrong (And What Actually Works)"
- **Formula C — Story Hook**: "How [Guest Name / Archetype] [Achieved Specific Result] in [Timeframe] — The Behind-the-Scenes"
- **Formula D — Question That Creates Urgency**: "Is Your [Process/Tool/Strategy] Actually Costing You [Specific Business Pain]?"
- **Formula E — Trend Framing**: "[Emerging Trend]: What It Means for [Target Persona] in [Current Year]"

For each title, include:
- SEO keyword (primary search term this title targets)
- Emotional trigger (curiosity / fear / aspiration / validation / FOMO)
- Predicted click-through score 1-10 with rationale

### 2. GUEST RESEARCH BRIEF
Structure as a pre-call one-pager the host reads 30 minutes before recording:

**Guest Background Summary** (3 sentences max): Career arc, current role scope, most relevant credential for this episode topic

**Content Intelligence**: 3 specific posts, articles, or talks from the last 90 days — summarize each in 1 sentence and flag which could generate the best episode moment

**Unique Angle This Guest Brings**: What counterintuitive insight or lived experience can ONLY this person provide? What would make this episode impossible to replicate with any other guest?

**Rapport Builders**: 2-3 personal or professional details to reference naturally in the intro (shared connection, recent win to congratulate, topic they're visibly passionate about online)

**Landmines to Avoid**: Competitors, past employer sensitivities, controversial takes they've walked back

**Predicted Best Sound Bite**: Based on their known positions, predict the one quote most likely to become a viral audiogram clip

## Integration Tips

- **Descript**: Paste the Episode Outline as a script document. Descript's AI can auto-generate a studio-quality transcript after recording, which you then clean against the outline. Use the Soundbite Alert timestamps to find and export audiogram clips directly from the timeline.
- **Riverside.fm / Squadcast**: Before recording, share the Pre-Interview Prep Kit with your guest via the session notes. After recording, use the AI speaker detection to isolate individual tracks — the Guest Intelligence Package's predicted soundbite helps you know where to look first.
- **Notion / Airtable Podcast CRM**: Build a database with one row per episode. Fields: Title, Guest, Record Date, Publish Date, Download Count (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30), Top Audiogram Clip, Pipeline Attributed, Guest Follow-Up Status. Link each row to the show notes document and the 90-Day Distribution Calendar.
- **HubSpot / Salesforce**: Create a "Podcast Listener" smart list from anyone who clicks episode links in emails or visits show notes pages. Route VP+ job titles into a dedicated ABM sequence that references the specific episode topic. Episode CTAs should use UTM parameters (utm_source=podcast&utm_medium=episode&utm_campaign=[episode-slug]) to enable pipeline attribution.
- **Zapier + Spotify for Podcasters API**: Trigger a Zapier workflow when a new episode hits 100 plays → auto-send a Slack notification to the marketing team with download stats → auto-publish the Day 3 LinkedIn clip post from the Social Distribution Plan.
- **Clay.com (for guest outreach)**: Use the Guest Research Brief structure as a Clay enrichment template. Pull guest's recent LinkedIn posts, company funding data, and conference speaking history automatically before each recording to populate the research brief at scale.
- **Capsho / Podcastle AI**: Feed the episode transcript into Capsho to auto-generate show notes first draft, then use the SEO-optimized Show Notes template in this prompt to upgrade the output with the structured timestamps, keyword targeting, and CTA optimization.
- **Beehiiv / ConvertKit**: Segment subscribers who opened the episode launch email and clicked the listen link as "high-intent podcast listeners." Create a separate nurture sequence for this segment that promotes your next 3 episodes automatically, increasing per-subscriber episode exposure and reducing churn.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The guest gives short, surface-level answers and the episode lacks depth.
**Solution:** Use the "Deep Dive Extensions" from the Prep Kit and deploy the follow-up probe: "That's interesting — can you give me a specific example of a time that played out in practice? What company, what happened, what was the number?" The phrase "tell me more about that specific moment" almost always unlocks the story beneath the talking point. If the guest is still thin, pivot to the "Lightning Round" early and plan a Part 2 episode as a guest loyalty mechanism.

**Problem:** The episode show notes aren't ranking on Google for the target keyword.
**Solution:** The primary keyword must appear in the episode title, the first sentence of the description, at least one H2-equivalent subheading in the show notes, and in the alt text of the episode thumbnail image. Also ensure the podcast RSS feed is linked from the show notes page (Google crawls RSS for podcast content signals). Run the published page through a free SEO audit tool (Ahrefs Webmaster Tools or Google Search Console) 2 weeks post-publish to check indexing status and add internal links from 2-3 existing blog posts to the new show notes page.

**Problem:** Guest hasn't shared the episode on their own channels despite receiving the co-promotion kit.
**Solution:** Send the co-promotion kit 24 hours before launch (not day-of). Follow up with a personalized voice note or short Loom video on launch day saying: "Episode is live! Here's the direct link and your pre-written post — tap to post takes 30 seconds." Guests are 4x more likely to share when they receive a single-tap post draft versus being asked to write their own. For high-value guests, offer to feature them in a compiled "best of" clip post 30 days later as an additional incentive.
