## Overview

This engine designs and deploys AI-orchestrated multi-channel marketing intervention campaigns for B2B SaaS companies to rescue at-risk accounts before churn occurs. Use it when health score thresholds are breached, renewal dates approach with low engagement, or behavioral signals (usage drops, support ticket spikes, champion departure) indicate churn risk — transforming CS data into automated marketing rescue sequences.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The AI generates generic "we miss you" retention emails instead of archetype-specific campaigns.**  
Solution: Include specific data signals in your prompt (exact health score numbers, which features are unused, what the champion's LinkedIn title change was). The more specific the input, the more differentiated the output. Use the archetype definitions in Section 3 verbatim as context.

**Problem: CS team ignores the marketing-generated handoff briefs.**  
Solution: Run the prompt specifically for the CS Brief section (Section 5) and have CSMs review 3 example briefs before launch. Frame briefs as "Intel you'd research anyway, pre-built" — not as marketing automation output. The recommended opening line is typically the highest-value element; lead with that in the brief header.

**Problem: Rescue campaign save rates are significantly below benchmarks.**  
Solution: First check archetype detection accuracy — if accounts are mis-classified (e.g., a budget-pressure account getting low-adoption messaging), open rates and CTAs will misfire. Run the archetype classification section again with corrected signal data for the underperforming cohort. Second, review offer ladder — Level 1 and 2 offers must have genuine perceived value to the buyer persona, not just to the CS team.
