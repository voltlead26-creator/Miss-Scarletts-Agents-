# The AI CMO: The Automated Strategic Brain

**Your next CMO won't be a person. It will be a system.**

This module is the strategic brain of the Autonomous Marketing Engine.

It automates the C-suite marketing function, focusing on the strategic trifecta of analytical, creative, and interpersonal tasks. We are building AI agents to handle high-level planning, forecasting, and proving the value of marketing in concrete, financial terms.

## Digital Marketing Agent

The first operational agent is now defined in:

- `AGENTS.md`
- `agents/digital-marketing-agent.md`

The agent is designed as an Australian-first digital marketing strategist and compliance-aware AI CMO assistant, with US marketing requirements treated as a secondary localisation layer.

It covers:

- campaign strategy,
- audience and positioning,
- channel planning,
- brand governance,
- campaign copy review,
- ROI and KPI planning,
- Australian-first marketing compliance checks,
- US secondary compliance checks,
- campaign risk verdicts: `PASS`, `REVISE`, or `BLOCK`.
