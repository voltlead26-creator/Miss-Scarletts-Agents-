## Success Metrics

**Content Performance:**
- Reels: Target >3% engagement rate (saves + comments + shares / views); benchmark is 1-2% average
- Carousels: Swipe-through rate >60%, save rate >2% of reach
- Stories: Retention rate >70% frame-to-frame; tap-forward rate <15%
- Overall account: Engagement rate >3% (strong), >5% (exceptional)

**Growth Metrics:**
- Follower growth rate: Target 10-20% month-over-month for accounts under 10K
- Profile visit-to-follow rate: >8% indicates strong first impression
- Reach-to-impression ratio: >60% means algorithm is distributing to non-followers

**Business Impact Metrics:**
- Link-in-bio click rate: >1.5% of reach
- DM volume from content: Track weekly
- UTM-attributed traffic from Instagram to website
- For lead gen: Instagram-sourced demo requests or form fills per month

**Content Quality Signals:**
- Save rate: Saves / reach > 2% = highly valuable content
- Share rate: Shares / reach > 1% = viral potential
- Comment depth: Are comments substantive (>5 words) or just emojis?

## Integration Tips

**Content Planning & Publishing:**
- **Later / Buffer / Sprout Social**: Export the 30-day calendar directly into scheduling tools. Add UTM parameters (`?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=organic&utm_content=[post-type]`) to every link-in-bio URL
- **Notion**: Use the pillar framework as a Notion database with fields for Format, Pillar, Status, Post Date, and Performance — tag each published post to track pillar performance over time
- **Google Sheets**: Build a simple content tracker with columns: Date | Format | Pillar | Hook | Reach | Saves | Shares | Comments | Profile Visits | Link Clicks — review weekly

**Repurposing Workflow:**
- **Zapier automation**: When you publish a Reel that exceeds your save-rate threshold (set in Zapier), auto-add it to a "Top Performers" Google Sheet for repurposing
- **TikTok cross-posting**: Use CapCut's publish to both platforms feature; adjust caption for TikTok (shorter, more native tone)
- **Email integration (Klaviyo / HubSpot)**: When a follower clicks your link-in-bio to your email opt-in, tag them as `instagram_organic` in your CRM for segmented nurture sequences

**Analytics:**
- **Meta Business Suite**: Pull weekly reach, follower demographics, and top content reports — feed data into your Notion content tracker
- **Google Analytics 4**: Set up Instagram as a traffic source; create an Instagram-specific acquisition report to track website sessions, goal completions, and revenue attributed to organic Instagram

**DM Automation:**
- **ManyChat**: Set up keyword triggers for DM CTAs used in Reels (e.g., "type MARGIN to get the calculator") — connects to HubSpot or your CRM via Zapier to log leads instantly

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Reels are getting views but no profile visits or follows.**
Solution: Your hook is working but your content isn't establishing brand value. Add a clear "why follow" signal in the final 5 seconds of every Reel — tell viewers exactly what they'll get by following (e.g., "Follow for a weekly breakdown of what's actually working in DTC right now"). Also audit your profile: if the bio doesn't immediately communicate who you help and how, visitors bounce without following.

**Problem: Carousels get low swipe-through rates (under 40%).**
Solution: Your slide 1 hook isn't creating enough curiosity gap to pull viewers to slide 2. Test this formula: Slide 1 = make a bold, specific claim. Slide 2 = "Here's what most [your audience] gets wrong about this..." — this creates an open loop that forces a swipe. Also check slide pacing: if slides are text-heavy, readers drop off. Aim for one idea per slide, max 15 words of copy.

**Problem: Posting consistently but growth has plateaued below 2,000 followers.**
Solution: At sub-2K followers, algorithm distribution is limited — you need external signals to break through. Run a targeted Collab post campaign: identify 3-5 complementary accounts in your niche with 5K-50K followers and propose a collaborative post (both accounts are tagged as creators, content appears on both feeds). Also invest 15 minutes per day in genuine engagement — comment substantively on posts from your ICP's accounts before posting your own content (this signals the algorithm to show your content to similar audiences).
