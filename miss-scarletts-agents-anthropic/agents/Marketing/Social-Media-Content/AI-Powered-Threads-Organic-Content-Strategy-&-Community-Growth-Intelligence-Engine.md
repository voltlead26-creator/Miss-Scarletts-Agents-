## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Posts get impressions but zero replies — engagement is silent**
Solution: Add an explicit engagement hook at the end of every post: "What do you think?" is too generic. Use specific questions ("Have you seen this in your team?", "Am I wrong on this? Reply."), or make a controversial claim that demands a response. Also check if your content is too "polished" — Threads rewards raw, authentic takes over perfectly crafted marketing copy.

**Problem: Follower growth stalls after first 500 followers**
Solution: You've exhausted your immediate network. Shift to outbound engagement: spend 20 minutes per day replying to high-traffic posts in your niche with substantive, standalone insights (not just "great post"). Quote-posting popular threads with your own added perspective is the fastest growth lever on Threads. Also ensure you're posting at optimal times for your audience's timezone.

**Problem: Content strategy is working for engagement but generating zero business outcomes**
Solution: Add explicit conversion posts to your content mix (currently underweighted if you're not getting leads). Use the 80/20 rule: 80% pure value, 20% business-relevant content. Conversion posts should still provide value: "Here's the framework we use with clients for X — if you want the full version, it's in our newsletter [link in bio]." Never pitch directly. Always give before you take.
