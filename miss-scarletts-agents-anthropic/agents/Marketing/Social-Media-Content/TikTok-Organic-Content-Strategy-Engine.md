- Link-in-bio CTR above 1.5% indicating TikTok viewers are actively converting to website visitors

## Integration Tips

Content Management in Notion or Airtable:
- Build a content tracker database with fields for Pillar, Hook Type, Format, Status, Post Date, Views, Completion Rate, and Comments received
- Use AI to pre-score new video concepts against the hook formula library before committing to filming
- Maintain a running swipe file of top-performing competitor TikToks tagged by hook type and video format for ongoing creative inspiration

Scheduling with Later, Buffer, or TikTok Native Scheduler:
- Schedule a maximum of 24-48 hours ahead. TikTok's algorithm rewards recency and penalizes content held in a queue for weeks.
- Use TikTok's native scheduler when possible as it produces the strongest algorithmic signals
- For the first 30 days, post natively without any scheduling tool to fully capture the early engagement window

Analytics with TikTok Analytics and Looker Studio:
- Export TikTok Analytics weekly as CSV and pull into a Looker Studio dashboard to visualize performance trends over time
- Tag all link-in-bio traffic in GA4 using UTM parameters: source tiktok, medium organic, campaign tiktok-organic
- Run a focused 30-minute weekly analytics review covering the top performing video, the lowest performing video, and one specific hypothesis to test the following week

CRM Integration with HubSpot or Klaviyo:
- Apply a TikTok source tag to all leads arriving from TikTok-attributed landing pages and enroll them in nurture sequences written in creator-style conversational copy
- Use ManyChat to automate DM-based email capture at scale by prompting viewers to comment a keyword to receive gated content
- Import TikTok audience data into Meta Ads Manager as a seed audience for lookalike targeting to compound organic momentum with paid reach

Zapier Workflow Automations:
- Connect a TikTok publish webhook to Zapier to auto-log every posted video into the Airtable content tracker with title, pillar, and post date
- Set a Slack notification trigger for any video that exceeds 2x the trailing 30-day average view count as a signal to engage comments immediately while velocity is building
- Route AI-generated video script drafts directly to a Google Doc in the content queue folder with status set to Ready to Film

## Troubleshooting

Problem: Videos consistently reach high view counts but produce almost no new followers.
Fix: The content is entertaining to watch but not compelling enough to follow. Every video requires a concrete follow-worthy reason stated explicitly in the CTA, for example: follow for one honest money reality check every single week. Audit the profile page directly: a generic bio and the absence of pinned videos kills follower conversion even when the underlying content is strong. Pin the three best-performing videos immediately. The industry benchmark for profile visit-to-follow rate is 10% or higher. A rate consistently below 5% means the profile page itself is the conversion bottleneck, not the content quality.

Problem: Only existing followers are watching and the algorithm consistently refuses to distribute content to the For You Page.
Fix: The hooks are assuming prior brand familiarity that cold TikTok audiences simply do not have. The first two seconds of every video must generate genuine curiosity for a complete stranger who has never encountered the brand before. Run an immediate experiment: produce the next five videos using pure entertainment or relatable-problem hooks and withhold any brand mention or product reference until after the eight-second mark. Simultaneously audit the hashtag strategy: using too many narrow niche-specific hashtags tells the algorithm to show the content only to a small existing interest cluster. Broaden the hashtag mix to include mid-tier tags in the 500K to 5M view range. Posting during peak audience activity windows in the primary viewer timezone also measurably improves FYP distribution probability.

Problem: Videos show strong completion rates but generate almost no comments or shares, creating the appearance of passive dead engagement.
Fix: The content needs explicit participation triggers built directly into the script structure. Close every video with a specific, accessible question that any viewer can answer without specialized knowledge. Publish the first comment on every video within 60 seconds of going live because TikTok's distribution algorithm uses early engagement velocity as a key ranking signal. Test the mild controversy technique: state a non-offensive but genuinely provocative opinion designed to invite both agreement and pushback, for example: unpopular opinion, traditional budgeting spreadsheets are the single biggest reason most people give up on managing their money entirely within the first two weeks of trying.
