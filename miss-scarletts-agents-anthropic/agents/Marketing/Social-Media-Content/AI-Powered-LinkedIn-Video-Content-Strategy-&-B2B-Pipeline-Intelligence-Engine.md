## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Videos getting <200 views despite good content**
Solution: The first 60 minutes after posting are critical. Have 5–10 team members like and comment immediately after posting — this signals to LinkedIn's algorithm to boost distribution. Also check: are you posting natively to LinkedIn (not sharing a YouTube link)? Native video gets 3x more reach. Ensure captions are enabled and the thumbnail/first frame contains text.

**Problem: Getting views but no pipeline activity (demos, DMs)**
Solution: Your CTAs are likely too vague or too high-friction. Replace "let me know if you have questions" with "Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you [specific asset]" — this creates a clear exchange of value. Also ensure your SDR team is actively monitoring commenters and ICP profile visits. Views don't become pipeline automatically; you need the human follow-up layer.

**Problem: Comments are from peers/competitors, not ICPs**
Solution: Your content is positioned too broad or too tactical for practitioners. Shift to speaking directly to executives — use language like "for teams managing $10M+ in pipeline" or "for RevOps leaders at Series B+" to filter your audience. Also, mention specific tools your ICP uses (Salesforce, Outreach, Clari) — this attracts the right practitioners through search and algorithm affinity signals.
