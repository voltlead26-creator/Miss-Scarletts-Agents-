## Success Metrics

- Engagement rate meets or exceeds tier benchmarks (nano >5%, micro >3%, macro >1.5%)
- Story/video click-through rate exceeds 2% average
- CPA from influencer channel is at least 30% lower than equivalent paid social CPA
- UGC library yields 10+ reusable assets for paid amplification
- At least 40% of creators agree to be re-engaged for next campaign (relationship quality signal)
- Brand mention sentiment is positive in comment analysis (>85% positive/neutral)

## Integration Tips

- **Grin / Aspire / Modash**: Feed creator brief directly into platform campaign workspace; use UTM builder inside tool for automated tracking
- **HubSpot**: Create influencer-specific UTM source to segment trial signups in CRM; build workflow to tag and score inbound leads from influencer traffic
- **Google Sheets**: Use the KPI dashboard template as a shared sheet with column-level formulas for EMV, CPE, and CPA auto-calculation; share view-only link with creators so they can see their own performance
- **Zapier**: Trigger a Slack alert to your marketing channel when any influencer post goes live (monitor via social listening webhook + Zapier); auto-log to tracking sheet
- **Notion**: Store the creator brief, outreach templates, and contract terms as a Notion database — one page per creator with status tracking (Outreach > Negotiating > Contracted > Brief Sent > Content Review > Live > Reporting)
- **Canva**: Share brand asset kit (logo pack, brand colors, product screenshots) via Canva shared workspace so creators have on-brand visual resources without emailing assets
- **Later / Publer**: Coordinate your brand's owned social reposts of creator content to align with creator go-live schedule for maximum algorithm momentum

## Troubleshooting

- **Problem: Creators ghost after initial interest.** Solution: Move to the Day 5 follow-up script. If still no response after Day 10, move on — creator responsiveness is a quality signal. Always maintain a 3x creator pipeline vs. your target count to absorb drop-off.

- **Problem: Creator posts content that violates the brief (missing disclosure, wrong CTA, off-brand claim).** Solution: Include in your contract a 24-hour takedown/edit clause for non-compliant posts. Provide the correction request template from Section 6. For FTC violations specifically, request immediate deletion and repost — do not negotiate on compliance.

- **Problem: Campaign tracking shows traffic but zero conversions.** Solution: Audit the landing page experience for mobile (most influencer traffic is mobile-first). Check that UTM parameters are firing correctly in GA4. Verify the offer is compelling enough for a cold audience — influencer traffic is warm but not intent-driven like search; add a lower-commitment CTA (free guide, webinar, quiz) as a micro-conversion step.
