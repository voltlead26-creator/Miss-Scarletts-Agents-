## Success Metrics

**Signal metrics (pipeline-predictive):**
- Content save rate > 2.5% of impressions (saves = high-intent readers bookmarking for action)
- Profile visits from posts > 150/week (measures brand curiosity driving deeper research)
- Follower quality: % of new followers matching ICP title + company size > 40%
- DM volume from LinkedIn: track in CRM, target 10+ qualified DMs/month by Month 3
- LinkedIn-sourced contacts in CRM: set UTM parameters on link-in-bio destination, target 30+ new contacts/month

**Ignore (vanity):**
- Raw impression count without engagement rate
- Total follower count without follower quality segmentation
- Likes (weakest engagement signal)

**Pipeline attribution checkpoint (Month 2):**
- Pull HubSpot/Salesforce contacts created in the past 30 days where "Lead Source = LinkedIn"
- Cross-reference with LinkedIn Page Analytics > Follower demographics to validate ICP match
- Calculate: LinkedIn content spend (hours × hourly rate) ÷ pipeline sourced = Content CAC

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Posts are getting impressions but zero DMs or CTA clicks**
Solution: The CTA is asking for too much too soon. LinkedIn cold audiences need 3–5 value-delivering touchpoints before they'll DM. Audit your last 10 posts — if fewer than 3 delivered standalone value with no product mention, rebuild the content mix. Increase "benchmark/education" posts to 60% and reduce "product" posts to under 15% for the next 30 days.

**Problem: Follower growth is flat even with consistent posting**
Solution: Follower growth on company pages requires reach beyond your current followers. Activate the employee advocacy brief: ask 10–15 employees to reshare top posts with a single authentic sentence added. One employee reshare to 500 connections generates more reach than 5,000 organic company-page impressions. Also test posting at 7:00–7:30 AM local time — early engagement velocity in the first 60 minutes is the #1 signal LinkedIn's algorithm uses to distribute posts to non-followers.

**Problem: ICP engages but never converts to a demo or pipeline**
Solution: The conversion layer is missing or too passive. Add a "pinned first comment" to every post with a specific, low-friction CTA: "If this resonates, I publish a weekly breakdown on [topic] — DM me 'weekly' and I'll add you to the list." This converts comment-readers who never click links. Also audit your LinkedIn page bio: it should include a crystal-clear value prop and a direct CTA link (not your homepage — a specific landing page).
