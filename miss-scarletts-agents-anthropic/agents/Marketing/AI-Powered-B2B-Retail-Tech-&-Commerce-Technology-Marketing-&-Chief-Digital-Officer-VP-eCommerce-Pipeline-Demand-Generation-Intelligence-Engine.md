## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High demo volume from IT Directors, not CDOs — deals stall at budget committee**
Solution: Add CDO persona filter to all paid campaigns immediately — exclude "IT Director," "Systems Administrator," "IT Manager" from LinkedIn targeting. Create CDO-specific landing pages with board-level language (GMV impact, digital transformation ROI, competitive differentiation) — not technical specs. Build a "CDO Fast Track" offer: skip the IT evaluation, get a direct 45-minute CDO briefing with a current CDO customer reference — offer this only to CDO-tier inbound.

**Problem: Prospects engage with content but freeze at Q4 peak — no buying until January**
Solution: Expect and plan for the retail freeze. Shift Q4 spend to relationship-building activities only (executive roundtables, no-ask touchpoints, holiday gifting to top-of-funnel CDO prospects). Pre-schedule January outreach for December planning — "Book your Q4 debrief + 2026 planning session" framed as an advisory conversation, not a sales call. Track all Q4 freeze contacts as "January Pipeline" and activate with urgency messaging on January 2.

**Problem: ROI claims not resonating — prospects say "our situation is too unique" to apply your case studies**
Solution: Build a configurable ROI calculator that inputs prospect-specific parameters (their GMV, their stockout rate, their markdown rate, their category mix) — eliminate the "that's not us" objection by making the model specific to their business. Pair with a "Free Data Diagnostic": offer to ingest 6 months of their own sales and inventory data and produce a customized baseline analysis before any sales conversation — this creates a data relationship before the commercial relationship, dramatically increasing close rate.
