## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** AI search tools consistently surface competitors but not your brand in vendor comparison queries, even after 60 days of content optimization.
**Solution:** The issue is almost always one of three things: (1) insufficient third-party validation — AI tools weight external authority (G2, analyst reports, press coverage) far more heavily than self-published content; (2) content is not genuinely answering the query — compare your content to what AI tools do surface and identify the specific information gap; (3) technical accessibility — verify your key pages are crawlable by AI agents and not blocked by login walls, aggressive bot detection, or JavaScript rendering issues. Audit with the Cloudflare AI bot logs if available.

**Problem:** Sales team reports that AI-referred prospects are arriving with inaccurate competitive comparisons or wrong pricing assumptions "from what the AI told them."
**Solution:** This is a content accuracy problem, not a sales problem. The AI agent is citing outdated or competitor-favorable content. Immediate action: publish clearly dated pricing transparency pages, create a "Common AI Misconceptions About [Your Company]" FAQ page, and submit corrections to any inaccurate third-party content you can identify. For recurring inaccuracies, contact the source (G2 review with false claim, industry publication with outdated article) and request correction. In the near-term, brief your AEs to open every AI-referred discovery call with: "Our AI search presence is something we're actively improving — before we dive in, is there anything you found in your research that you'd like to validate directly with us?"

**Problem:** The agentic buyer demand generation program is producing pipeline but executive leadership questions whether AI-mediation is genuinely adding value or is just a relabeling of existing inbound.
**Solution:** Run a 30-day cohort analysis comparing: (a) self-reported AI-assisted prospects vs. non-AI prospects on deal velocity, win rate, ACV, and first-touch source; (b) accounts where your G2 profile, comparison pages, or AI-cited content was visited before a demo request vs. accounts where it was not. If AI-mediated prospects close faster or at higher rates, you have ROI evidence. If they don't, you likely have a sales motion gap — the AI is generating shortlist inclusion but your AI-native buyer experience isn't converting on the compressed timeline these buyers expect.
