## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The founder says "I don't have time to review 28 pieces of content every month"**
Solution: Restructure the workflow. Use this engine to generate all content in one 2-hour monthly session. The founder records 30 minutes of raw thoughts via Loom or voice memo, you convert it to copy via this engine, then the founder does a single 90-minute review + approval session. After Month 2, the engine should know the voice well enough that only 15-20% of copy needs significant editing.

**Problem: Posts perform well on vanity metrics (likes/comments) but generate zero pipeline**
Solution: The content is resonating emotionally but not with the right audience. Run a follower audit — if new followers are mostly peers (other founders, investors) rather than ICP (target buyers), shift themes from "founder journey" content to "practitioner insight" content. ICP buyers respond to posts about their specific job problems, not founder inspiration narratives. Add more operational specificity: instead of "culture eats strategy," write "here's exactly how I'd restructure a sales team to reduce churn in the first 30 days of a new AE's ramp."

**Problem: The founder's POVs feel like opinions, not insights — they're not landing**
Solution: Every POV needs a "proof anchor" — a specific number, customer story, or before/after comparison that makes the opinion testable. "AI SDRs are overhyped" is an opinion. "We tested 3 AI SDR platforms across 12 accounts for 90 days and booked 2% fewer meetings than human SDRs, at 4x the cost per meeting" is an insight. Return to the Advanced Version's Phase 1 (Theme & Angle Matrix) and force a data point or specific example into every POV before writing content.
