- **HubSpot App Marketplace:** Install HubSpot's Partner Tracking module to capture install events. Use HubSpot workflows to auto-enroll new marketplace installs into a 5-step integration onboarding sequence. Tag marketplace installs in HubSpot CRM as "Partner Ecosystem — HubSpot Marketplace" for attribution.
- **Crossbeam / Reveal:** Connect your CRM to Crossbeam or Reveal to run account mapping with co-sell partners without sharing raw customer data. Set up automated Slack alerts when a prospect in your pipeline shows up as an active customer of a co-sell partner — trigger co-sell outreach immediately.
- **HG Insights / ZoomInfo:** Pull weekly lists of companies that recently adopted your top integration partners but don't appear in your CRM. Auto-enroll in tech-stack outbound sequences via Outreach or Salesloft using HG Insights → Salesforce → Outreach native integration.
- **Zapier:** Create a Zap that triggers when a new integration install is detected → enriches the account in Clearbit → scores it against your ICP model → routes to the appropriate SDR queue in Salesforce.
- **Google Search Console:** Monitor impressions for "[Your Product] + [Partner]" search queries. Any integration query generating 500+ monthly impressions with no dedicated landing page = immediate content opportunity. Build integration-specific pages within 30 days.

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Integration partners won't engage in co-sell or co-marketing despite a formal partnership agreement.**  
*Solution:* The problem is usually misaligned incentives, not relationship quality. Calculate the revenue impact on the partner's business from shared customers (include expansion revenue, not just initial deal). Present a "joint business case" to the partner's VP of Partnerships showing: how many shared customers both companies have, estimated pipeline from mapping those accounts, and specific AE-level commission opportunity from co-sell deals. Partners engage when their AEs see personal income opportunity, not when their partnerships team sees pipeline targets.

**Issue: Tech-stack outbound sequences get low response rates despite good signals.**  
*Solution:* The most common failure is leading with product ("We integrate with Salesforce") rather than the shared workflow problem ("Most CROs using Outreach + Salesforce tell us the gap between activity data and forecast accuracy costs them 2-3 points of close rate"). Rewrite your email openers to name the specific pain that exists in the joint workflow — not the integration. Also verify signal freshness: HG Insights data can be 60-90 days stale. Prioritize accounts where multiple signals confirm tool usage (technographic + review site + job posting mentions + LinkedIn skills).

**Issue: Marketplace reviews aren't converting into inbound leads despite high review volume.**  
*Solution:* Review quantity is not the driver — review relevance is. G2 and AppExchange algorithms surface reviews that match the searcher's use case. Audit your reviews for specific outcome language: reviews that say "improved forecast accuracy by 18%" outperform reviews that say "great tool, easy to use." Run a review coaching program: when you request reviews, provide customers with a simple framework ("Briefly describe the problem you had before Strata, what you tried that didn't work, and one specific metric that changed after you started using us"). This dramatically increases conversion-quality reviews without coaching the sentiment itself.
