## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Post-show follow-up emails get low open rates (below 25%)
**Solution:** Send within 24 hours while memory of the conversation is fresh. Use highly specific subject lines referencing the exact conversation ("Following up on your comment about rep ramp time"). Avoid generic "Great meeting you at [Event]" — it signals mass email. Include a specific next step, not a vague "let's stay in touch."

**Problem:** Hot leads go cold after the event; scheduled demos are no-shows
**Solution:** Over-communicate the meeting value in the 48 hours before the scheduled call. Send a "here's exactly what we'll cover" email with a specific agenda. Include a 30-second Loom video from the AE recapping the booth conversation. Have a VP or CEO send a brief "looking forward to connecting your team with ours" email. If they cancel, immediately offer 2 alternative slots within the same week.

**Problem:** Pipeline attribution is disputed by sales ("that deal was already in process")
**Solution:** Use "event influenced" as a separate attribution category alongside "event sourced." Track opportunity stage changes that happen within 21 days of the event — if a stalled deal advances after the event, it's event-influenced. Pre-negotiate the attribution model with sales leadership before the event so everyone agrees on the rules.
