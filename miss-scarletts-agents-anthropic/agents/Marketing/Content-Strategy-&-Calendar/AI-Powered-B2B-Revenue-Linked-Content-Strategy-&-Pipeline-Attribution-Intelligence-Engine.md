## Overview

This engine helps B2B marketing leaders build a content strategy anchored directly to revenue goals — mapping every content investment to pipeline stages, attribution data, and revenue outcomes rather than vanity metrics. Use it when you need to justify content investment to the CFO, rebuild content strategy around pipeline impact, or design a content flywheel that generates compounding pipeline growth quarter over quarter.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI generates generic content recommendations without specificity (e.g., "write thought leadership blog posts")**
Fix: Add 3-5 actual content assets you've already published (with titles) and tell the AI which performed best. Include 2-3 verbatim quotes from customer discovery calls or win/loss interviews. Specify the exact job title of your buyer and the specific decision they're trying to make. The more specific your input context, the more specific the output titles and frameworks.

**Problem: Pipeline projections feel unrealistically high or disconnected from your actual funnel**
Fix: Input your actual funnel conversion rates at each stage. If you don't have them, start with B2B SaaS benchmarks: MQL→SQL 12-18%, SQL→Opportunity 55-65%, Opportunity→Close 20-28%. Then ask the AI to recalculate all projections using your specific rates. Also specify your current pipeline coverage ratio (pipeline ÷ quarterly revenue target) — if it's below 3×, the strategy needs to prioritize volume over quality.

**Problem: Strategy recommends content your team cannot produce with current headcount and budget**
Fix: Add this constraint explicitly: "The content team is [X people]. Maximum content production capacity is [X assets per month]. The entire quarterly budget is [$X] including agency and tools. Prioritize quality over volume — recommend the fewest possible assets that generate the highest pipeline impact. Include make vs. buy recommendations (which assets to outsource vs. produce in-house)."
