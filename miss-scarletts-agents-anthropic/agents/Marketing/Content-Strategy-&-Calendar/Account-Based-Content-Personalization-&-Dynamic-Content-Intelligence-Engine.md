## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Output feels semi-generic — could apply to multiple companies in the same industry**
*Fix:* Your account intelligence input is too thin. Add 2-3 of: recent earnings call quote from their CEO, specific regulatory change affecting their sub-segment, a named internal initiative (e.g., "Project Phoenix digital transformation"), recent acquisition, or public job posting signals. The more specific the input, the more specific the output. Re-run the prompt with at least one account-specific fact that only applies to them.

**Problem: AE says "this doesn't match how our customers actually talk" for a given industry**
*Fix:* Add an industry language calibration step: paste 3-5 paragraphs from their industry trade press (e.g., American Banker for banking, HIMSS publications for healthcare) into the prompt as context before the personalization request. Ask the model to "use the vocabulary and framing from these reference articles throughout." This anchors output to authentic industry voice rather than generic B2B language.

**Problem: Personalized content package takes too long to produce at scale for Tier 2/3 accounts**
*Fix:* Build a "segment library" — run this prompt once per industry segment (not per named account) to create 8-10 reusable building blocks per vertical. Then mix and match blocks for Tier 2 accounts using simple company-name substitution. Reserve full custom runs for Tier 1 only. This 80/20 approach captures 70% of the conversion lift at 20% of the production cost.
