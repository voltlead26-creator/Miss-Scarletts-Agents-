## Troubleshooting

**Issue: LinkedIn posts get low reach despite following the framework**
*Fix:* The first 60 minutes after posting are critical — reply to every comment immediately to boost algorithmic distribution. Ensure the post does not include an external link in the body (put it in the first comment instead). Also check posting time: B2B LinkedIn peaks Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10 AM in the audience's primary timezone. If reach is still low, boost with $50–100 of Sponsored Content to seed initial engagement.

**Issue: Community posts get removed or generate negative responses**
*Fix:* Over-promotion is the #1 community violation. Re-read the community rules before posting. Lead with a genuine question or insight and let the link be secondary — or skip the link entirely and mention you'll DM it to anyone interested. Build credibility by engaging in 3–5 community threads before posting your own content in a new community.

**Issue: Sales team ignores the enablement snippets and doesn't amplify**
*Fix:* Distribution fails when sales adoption is zero. Run a 10-minute "content live" Slack standup call on launch day: show the post, explain the insight, and demonstrate how to use the snippet in an active deal. Track AE social shares in your CRM and make it a monthly team metric. The highest-performing teams gamify it (leaderboard, gift card for most pipeline influenced via content sharing).
