## Integration Tips

**HubSpot:**
- Build trigger workflows using deal stage + last activity date + contact engagement score
- Use Sequences for rep-triggered touches, Workflows for marketing automation
- Create a "Mid-Funnel Health" deal property with 1–10 scoring based on EB engagement + champion activity + days since demo
- Connect to Slack for real-time rep alerts when trigger conditions fire

**Salesforce + Pardot/Marketing Cloud:**
- Use Opportunity fields (Last Activity Date, Stage Duration, Contact Roles populated) as automation triggers
- Build Einstein Lead Scoring equivalent for open opportunities using engagement data
- Use Engagement Studio in Pardot for multi-path nurture based on stakeholder role

**Outreach/Salesloft:**
- Create role-based sequences for Champion, Economic Buyer, and Technical Buyer as separate playbooks
- Tag sequences with deal stage so reps can deploy with one click
- Use sequence "booked meeting" exit condition to prevent over-sequencing

**Content Operations:**
- Store all consideration-stage assets in a Deal Room tool (Aligned, Notion, GetAccept, Highspot)
- Track asset engagement at the account level — not just individual opens
- Set up automatic content recommendations based on industry and deal stage in Highspot or Showpad

**Gong/Chorus:**
- Use conversation intelligence to identify which objections appear most in stalled deals
- Create auto-alerts when competitor names are mentioned to trigger competitive intervention sequence
- Pull "moment of stall" transcripts to write more accurate champion email templates

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Sequences are sending but deals still stalling at the same rate.**
Fix: The content is wrong, not the automation. Pull the 10 most recent "no decision" post-mortems and identify the ONE objection that killed each deal. Rebuild the sequence around directly addressing that objection — not around sending more content.

**Problem: Champions are engaging with content but can't get economic buyer meeting.**
Fix: The champion needs internal selling ammunition, not more external marketing content. Build a "CFO Briefing Deck" the champion can present in their next leadership meeting — slide 1 is the problem statement in the CFO's language, slide 2 is the ROI model, slide 3 is the risk of inaction. Make the champion the hero internally.

**Problem: Triggers are firing but reps are ignoring the alerts.**
Fix: Reduce alert volume and increase alert specificity. Instead of "Champion hasn't responded," send "Deal X has $65K at risk — champion gone dark for 12 days. Here's what to say." Attach the exact email template and call talk track. Remove friction between alert and action.
