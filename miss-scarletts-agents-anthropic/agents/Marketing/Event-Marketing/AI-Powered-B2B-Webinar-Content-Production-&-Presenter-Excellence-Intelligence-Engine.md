## Integration Tips

**Zoom Webinars / ON24 / Demio:**
- Export poll data post-event → sync to HubSpot/Salesforce via Zapier (field: `webinar_poll_response`) for segmented follow-up
- Use attendance duration data (available in all platforms) to score leads: watched 80%+ = hot, 40–79% = warm, <40% = cold
- Tag registrants who asked Q&A questions as higher-intent signals for SDR outreach

**HubSpot:**
- Create a Webinar Engagement workflow: trigger on `attended_webinar = true`, branch by attendance duration and CTA click → route to appropriate nurture sequence or AE notification
- Use webinar poll answers to update Contact properties (e.g., `primary_challenge = volume_bottleneck`) for long-term segmentation

**Salesforce:**
- Create a Campaign for each webinar; add all registrants as Campaign Members with status: Registered / Attended / No-Show / Replay Viewed
- Set campaign influence attribution: $X influenced per Attended contact based on your historical conversion rates
- Use Task auto-creation for AEs when a target account contact attends and completes CTA

**Notion / Google Docs:**
- Store all scripts and slide briefs in a shared workspace with presenter 72 hours before event
- Create a run-of-show document: column 1 = minute, column 2 = presenter action, column 3 = producer action, column 4 = chat prompt to drop

**Zapier Automation:**
- Trigger: Webinar registration confirmed → Action: Add to pre-webinar email sequence in ActiveCampaign/HubSpot
- Trigger: Attendee clicks CTA resource link during webinar → Action: Create task for SDR in Salesforce within 2 hours
- Trigger: No-show detected post-event → Action: Enroll in replay + reactivation nurture sequence

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Attendee engagement drops sharply after the first 15 minutes**
Solution: This typically signals that Act 1 (the problem) ran too long or felt too abstract. Cut problem framing to 10 minutes maximum. Add a live poll at minute 7 to re-activate attention. Introduce a specific customer story (not a generic "companies like yours") within the first 12 minutes to create emotional investment before presenting the framework.

**Problem: High CTA click rate but low demo booking conversion (>10% clicks, <2% books)**
Solution: The offer and the audience are mismatched in stage or friction. Audit the landing page the CTA sends to — ensure it requires <3 form fields for TOFU/MOFU audiences. For BOFU webinars where the audience is evaluation-stage, replace "Book a demo" with "See it applied to your use case" or "Get a custom ROI analysis" — specificity reduces friction. Also check that the CTA landing page matches the language used in the CTA script exactly.

**Problem: Solo presenter appears flat or low-energy on camera — audience engagement signals drop**
Solution: Have the presenter record a 3-minute practice segment and review playback specifically for pace and eye contact. Flat delivery is almost always caused by reading from a script verbatim — convert the word-for-word script to bullet-point speaker notes for natural delivery. Schedule a 20-minute tech + energy rehearsal 48 hours before the live event, and have the presenter stand during the webinar (standing physiologically increases vocal energy and pace variation).
