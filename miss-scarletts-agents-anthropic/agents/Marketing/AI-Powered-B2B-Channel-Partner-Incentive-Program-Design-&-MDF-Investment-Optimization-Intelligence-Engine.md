## Integration Tips

- **Impartner / Allbound PRM:** Load the MDF Activity Catalog directly as a "Requestable Activity" catalog with max amounts pre-configured. Set auto-approval rules for activities under $1,000 with standard POP requirements to reduce manual review volume.
- **Salesforce:** Create a custom "MDF Opportunity" object linked to Partner Account records. Map SPIFF payments to Opportunity Close Date to track SPIFF program ROI by cohort. Use Salesforce Reports to build a real-time MDF utilization dashboard visible to PAMs.
- **HubSpot:** For companies using HubSpot as CRM, create a Partner Deal Pipeline with a "MDF Activity" association field. Use HubSpot Sequences to automate the 8-week partner communication launch plan.
- **DocuSign / Adobe Sign:** Automate co-branding approval sign-offs with digital signature workflows. Use conditional logic to route complex asset approvals (e.g., webinar decks with competitive claims) to legal review automatically.
- **Stripe / Bill.com:** Connect your PRM to Stripe for automated SPIFF payouts to partner rep email addresses on a Net-30 basis. Flag payments >$600 to trigger 1099 generation for US-based partners (IRS requirement).
- **Looker / Tableau:** Build a Channel Marketing Analytics dashboard combining PRM MDF data with Salesforce pipeline data. Key views: MDF spend by activity type, pipeline generated by activity type, SPIFF payments by partner, QBR score trend by tier.
- **Slack:** Create a #channel-partner-wins channel integrated with Salesforce to auto-post when a partner-sourced deal closes — builds internal visibility and motivates the channel team.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Partners are claiming MDF but pipeline generation is low (poor ROI).**
Solution: Shift reimbursement from activity-completion to outcome-based. Require proof of pipeline creation (e.g., 3 qualified opportunities created) within 60 days of activity as a condition for full reimbursement. Offer 50% reimbursement upfront and 50% upon pipeline milestone.

**Problem: High-performing partners use all MDF quickly in Q1, leaving nothing for Q2–Q4.**
Solution: Implement quarterly release cadence (25% per quarter per partner) with a Q3 rollover window where unused Q1/Q2 funds can be applied to Q3 activities. This prevents Q1 budget dumps while rewarding planning discipline.

**Problem: Small and mid-tier partners don't submit MDF claims — low program utilization.**
Solution: Simplify the POP process for sub-$2,000 activities: accept photo evidence + LinkedIn/email campaign screenshot as sufficient proof. Assign a Partner Marketing Associate to make proactive outreach calls in Month 2 of each quarter to Silver/Gold partners with unused MDF balances. Offer "plug-and-play" MDF packages (pre-designed campaigns with creative, copy, and media plan) that require only $500 partner contribution — eliminating the planning burden that deters smaller partners.
