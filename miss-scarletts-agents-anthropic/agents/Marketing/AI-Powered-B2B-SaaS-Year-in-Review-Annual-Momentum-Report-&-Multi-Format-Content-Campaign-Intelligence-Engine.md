## Overview

Converts raw company metrics, product milestones, customer wins, and team achievements into a complete Year-in-Review content campaign — producing 15+ coordinated assets across blog, email, social, PR, customer communications, and investor narrative in a single AI-powered production run. Use it every Q4/Q1 when your company needs to publish its annual momentum report, drive brand credibility with active prospects, deepen loyalty with existing customers, and sustain a full press news cycle heading into the new year.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: We don't have impressive enough metrics to publish externally.**
Solution: Reframe the campaign around momentum, not magnitude. "We tripled our NPS score" or "Every customer who renewed expanded their contract" can be more compelling than raw ARR numbers. Focus the narrative on product quality and customer love metrics (NPS, CSAT, expansion rate) if growth metrics are sensitive. Alternatively, use the campaign as an internal-only all-hands document rather than external publishing.

**Problem: Legal won't approve naming customer logos or specific ROI numbers.**
Solution: Use vertical/role-based anonymization: "A Series C logistics SaaS reduced X by Y%" works nearly as well as named logos in most contexts. For the press release, ask customers for a generic quote rather than a specific data point — "We've relied on [Company] as a critical part of our data infrastructure" requires no sensitive disclosure.

**Problem: The AI output feels generic or interchangeable with any SaaS company.**
Solution: The key input is specificity in your raw data. Vague inputs ("we grew a lot") produce vague outputs. Go back and add exact numbers, specific product feature names, real customer anecdotes (even anonymized), and your exact brand voice description. The more granular the input, the more distinctive the output. Run the advanced version and fill in every field — it's worth the extra 10 minutes.
