## Integration Tips

- **G2 API**: Use G2's Buyer Intent API (paid tier) to trigger review requests when an account's employees browse your G2 profile — highest-intent review candidates
- **HubSpot**: Create a custom "Review Requested" contact property + "Review Submitted" property; use these as enrollment triggers for post-review nurture flows and to suppress from sales sequences
- **Gainsight / ChurnZero**: Add "Review Status" as a health score component; accounts that have left reviews show 40-60% lower churn — factor into CSM prioritization
- **Salesforce**: Create a "Social Proof Influence" field on Opportunity to track deals where G2/review assets were shared; feed into closed-won attribution
- **Zapier / Make**: Connect G2 webhook to Slack alert to Airtable review database to Google Sheets social proof library — a 4-step automation that eliminates manual review tracking
- **Notion / Confluence**: Maintain a "Review Quote Library" database tagged by use case, persona, outcome type, and competitor mention — make searchable for sales team
- **Canva / Figma**: Build a template for "Customer Quote Cards" that auto-populate from the review library for social media and event collateral

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Low review conversion rate (under 8%) despite sending requests.
**Solution:** Check suppression logic — most teams are sending to the wrong accounts. Audit the last 50 requests: what percentage went to accounts with health score below 70? Also check your deep link — a broken or non-personalized G2 link drops conversion by 60%. Test the link yourself before every campaign launch.

**Problem:** Reviews are generic and don't mention specific outcomes or use cases.
**Solution:** Add a "review prompt" to your email — include 2-3 specific questions: "What specific outcome have you achieved? What would you tell a peer who's evaluating us?" This guides reviewers toward more specific, quotable content without violating G2 terms.

**Problem:** G2 marks reviews as "unverified" or removes them.
**Solution:** Ensure you're using G2's official review link (from your G2 profile dashboard) rather than a generic URL. G2 requires account validation for reviews. If using G2's API, make sure you're using the correct review invitation endpoint, not self-submission. Never incentivize reviews in a way that violates G2 terms (no direct payment or discounts contingent on review submission).
