## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Free tier conversion rate is below 3% after 60 days.**
Solution: The free tier is likely too generous — users are getting full value without upgrading. Run a cannibalization audit: identify the 1-2 features most correlated with paid conversion and move them behind the paywall. Alternatively, your upgrade triggers may be appearing at the wrong moments; use product analytics to find the exact in-app events that precede upgrades and move prompts to those moments.

**Problem: High free signup volume but <15% activation rate (users signing up but not returning).**
Solution: The onboarding experience isn't delivering value fast enough. Shorten time-to-first-value: reduce required setup steps, pre-populate the account with demo data or an example dashboard, and trigger the "aha moment" within the first session. Rewrite Day 1 email to include a direct deep-link to the highest-value action, not generic product education.

**Problem: Paid plan customers complaining that free users get "almost everything."**
Solution: Conduct a feature differentiation audit using the Paid Plan Protection Scorecard from Deliverable 5. Identify 2-3 high-visibility, high-utility features that are currently in the free tier and migrate them to paid. Communicate changes with 60-day notice to free users, framing it as "expanding our paid plan value." Offer affected free users a 3-month discounted upgrade path.
