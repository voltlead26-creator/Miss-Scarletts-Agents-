## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Very high CPL ($800+) with no MQL conversion**
Fix: Your audience is too broad or your creative is not resonating with decision-makers. Run the following diagnosis: (1) Check video completion rate — if < 15%, the hook is wrong; rewrite the first 3 seconds to be more specific to your ICP's pain. (2) Check if form fields are too generic — add a qualifying question ("Company size" or "Current solution") to filter out non-ICP leads before they enter CRM. (3) Narrow audiences from interest-based to lookalike-only until you have cleaner ICP signals.

**Problem: Strong video views and clicks, but zero form submissions**
Fix: The issue is in the post-click experience, not the ad. Check: (1) Is your landing page loading in < 2 seconds on mobile? TikTok traffic is 95%+ mobile — a slow page kills conversion. (2) Is the landing page experience jarring vs. the TikTok ad's native feel? Test sending TikTok traffic to a mobile-optimized landing page that mirrors TikTok's visual style. (3) Switch from website conversion to TikTok Instant Lead Forms — eliminate the landing page entirely for initial testing.

**Problem: TikTok reports 80 leads but only 20 show up in CRM**
Fix: This is a common integration gap, not a lead quality problem. Diagnose: (1) Check Zapier/integration error logs — failed pushes from form field mismatches are common. (2) Verify TikTok Lead Form field names exactly match your CRM field API names. (3) Add an email notification step to your Zap as a backup — catch leads that fail CRM push via email to a monitored inbox. (4) Audit your CRM deduplication rules — TikTok leads may be merging into existing contacts without creating new MQL records.
