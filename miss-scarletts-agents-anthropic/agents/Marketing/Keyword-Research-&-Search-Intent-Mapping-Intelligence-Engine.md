## Troubleshooting

**Issue: AI generates generic keyword ideas that don't reflect real search behavior**
Fix: Provide more specific use case language, actual product feature names, and real competitor brand names in your inputs. The more specific your "core capabilities" and "key pain points" fields, the more precise the output. Also explicitly ask the AI to generate keywords in the style of how your target buyer would actually type a Google search — not formal product category language.

**Issue: Quick-win keywords produce pages that never rank despite low competition**
Fix: "Low competition" for AI-generated estimates assumes average content quality. For pages in the DA 30–40 range, you need topical depth (1,500–2,500 words minimum), strong internal linking from the pillar page, and at least 3–5 external backlinks within 60 days of publishing. Re-run the Advanced prompt with explicit instruction to factor in your domain authority and add a "required backlinks to rank" estimate per keyword.

**Issue: Content cluster architecture is too broad and doesn't differentiate from existing content**
Fix: Run the prompt a second time with your existing content URLs listed explicitly and instruct the AI to identify gaps only — keywords not addressed by any existing page. This prevents recommending content you already have and focuses the architecture on true white space.
