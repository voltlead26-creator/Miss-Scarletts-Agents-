## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot:** Use LinkedIn Ads native HubSpot integration to sync Lead Gen Form submissions directly to contacts; create LinkedIn-specific lead source properties and lifecycle stage workflows; set up LinkedIn Insight Tag via HubSpot's tracking code manager
- **Salesforce:** Deploy LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms via Salesforce native connector; create LinkedIn Campaign Member object to track prospect engagement; use Salesforce Campaigns to measure LinkedIn-influenced pipeline and closed-won revenue
- **6sense / Bombora:** Export in-market account lists from intent platforms → upload to LinkedIn as Matched Audiences (Company List) → layer with job title targeting for highest-intent demand capture campaigns
- **Chili Piper / Calendly:** Embed scheduling link in LinkedIn Lead Gen Form thank-you URL to convert high-intent leads to booked demos within minutes; track show-rate by LinkedIn campaign to optimize for quality over quantity

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High impressions but very low CTR (below 0.3%)**
Solution: Your creative is not stopping the scroll — the hook isn't resonating with the audience. Run a creative diagnostic: check if the intro text first 150 characters contain a specific data point or provocative claim. Replace generic benefit statements ("Drive more pipeline") with specific, audience-relevant provocations ("Why 73% of Series B CFOs missed Q1 board prep deadlines"). Also check audience-message match — are you serving enterprise-focused copy to SMB audiences?

**Problem: High CPL (2x+ above benchmark) despite good CTR**
Solution: You have a landing page or lead gen form quality problem, not an ad problem. If using Lead Gen Forms: reduce form fields to 4-5 maximum, use pre-filled LinkedIn data, and A/B test the form headline and CTA copy. If driving to website: check page load speed (LinkedIn mobile users have low patience), ensure the landing page headline matches the ad headline exactly, and add social proof (customer logos, a specific stat) above the fold. Also audit your audience targeting — broad audiences drive cheaper CPLs but lower MQL rates, which inflates effective CPL when you factor in sales qualification time.

**Problem: LinkedIn leads not appearing in CRM or UTMs are broken**
Solution: This is the #1 operational failure in LinkedIn programs. Audit in this order: (1) confirm LinkedIn Insight Tag is firing on all pages via browser console; (2) check that Lead Gen Form thank-you URL contains UTM parameters `utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=paid-social&utm_campaign=[campaign-name]`; (3) verify your CRM form handler is preserving hidden UTM fields; (4) for Salesforce, confirm the LinkedIn native connector is syncing on the correct lead object; (5) for ABM attribution, ensure LinkedIn Company Pages are matched to CRM accounts via the LinkedIn Company Match feature in Campaign Manager.
