## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Review velocity stalls after initial launch**
Fix: Shift from generic "leave us a review" asks to specific, outcome-based asks tied to recent wins. Send from your CSM's personal email (not marketing automation) with a pre-written review draft the customer can edit and submit in <2 minutes. High-effort ask = low completion. Make it frictionless.

**Problem: Press pitches getting ignored despite strong data**
Fix: Journalists don't cite data they can't verify. Publish your benchmark report as a dedicated landing page with methodology, sample size, and raw data download before pitching. Lead with the counterintuitive finding, not the self-serving one. "Sales teams that record fewer calls close more deals" gets a response. "Narrativ AI customers close 23% more deals" does not.

**Problem: AI SOV not improving after 60 days despite executing the playbook**
Fix: LLM training data has a lag — most models update training data every 6–12 months. Your earned media from Month 1–2 may not be reflected in ChatGPT/Perplexity answers until a model update cycle. Shift your 60-day measurement to Perplexity and Google AI Overviews (real-time RAG systems) rather than ChatGPT (training-data dependent). Also verify that your new press mentions and reviews are being indexed by checking for direct citations in Perplexity searches with site-specific operator queries.
