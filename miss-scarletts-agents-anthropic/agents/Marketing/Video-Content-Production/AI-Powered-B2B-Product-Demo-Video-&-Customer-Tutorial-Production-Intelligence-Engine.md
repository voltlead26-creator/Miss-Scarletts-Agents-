## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Script sounds like a product brochure, not a human conversation**
Solution: Read every line aloud. If you can't say it in one breath or you'd never say it to a colleague, cut it. Replace "leverages our proprietary AI infrastructure" with "our AI does the math you don't have time for." Use contractions. Add pauses. Write for the ear, not the eye.

**Problem: Demo video gets high view count but zero CTA conversions**
Solution: Your CTA is either too late, too vague, or too high-friction. Move the first CTA to the 50% mark. Replace "learn more" with a specific, low-commitment offer ("see a 2-minute version with your industry's data"). Add social proof within 10 seconds of the CTA — a customer logo or stat — to reduce friction.

**Problem: Tutorial video completion rate below 40%**
Solution: Audit your chapter structure. Tutorial viewers are task-oriented — they're trying to do ONE thing. If your 8-minute tutorial covers 6 features, split it into 6 two-minute micro-tutorials. Add chapter markers so viewers can skip to exactly what they need. The goal isn't one long video — it's a searchable, modular library where every video delivers value in under 3 minutes.
