## Success Metrics

- **Hook retention rate:** >40% of viewers watch past the first 3 seconds (platform benchmark varies; optimize this first)
- **Video completion rate:** >20% for 30-second ads (cold traffic), >35% for warm/retargeting audiences
- **CTR:** >1.0% for LinkedIn video, >1.5% for Meta video, >0.8% for YouTube TrueView
- **CPL/CPA improvement:** >25% reduction in cost per result versus static image ads
- **Creative freshness score:** No ad set running same creative >4 weeks without a hook refresh
- **Testing velocity:** Minimum 2 new creative variants tested per month per platform
- **A/B test statistical validity:** 95% confidence before declaring a winner, minimum 50 conversions per variant

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Video ads have high view rates but low clicks — creative is engaging but not converting.**
Solution: Audit your CTA placement and offer clarity. Most engagement-without-conversion issues come from: (1) CTA appearing too late in the video, (2) mismatched funnel stage (awareness creative asking for a demo), or (3) vague offer ("Learn more" vs. "Start free trial — no credit card"). Apply the BOFU script framework and move CTA to appear at both the 50% and 100% mark of video length.

**Problem: Creative fatigues within 1-2 weeks on Meta, CPMs spiking rapidly.**
Solution: Your audience is too narrow for your budget — frequency is compressing your performance window. Either (1) expand targeting with Advantage+ Audience to find lookalikes automatically, (2) implement the Tier 1 hook refresh weekly (new 3-second hook, same body), or (3) rotate between 5+ creative variants simultaneously so each individual ad reaches lower per-ad frequency.

**Problem: Can't produce enough creative volume to keep testing cadence.**
Solution: Implement the UGC-on-demand system: brief 3-5 customers or team members to record 60-second talking head videos answering "What problem did you have before? What does [Product] do? What result did you get?" Raw, unedited testimonial recordings often outperform polished studio production on TikTok and Meta. Add your hook text overlay in post and you have a new creative variant in 2 hours. For B2B, employee-generated content (EGC) from sales or product team members performs especially well on LinkedIn.
