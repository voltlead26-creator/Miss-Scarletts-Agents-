*Phase 2: Content Architecture Design*

- Apply the 3-second rule: the first frame must create a pattern interrupt

*Phase 4: Script Architecture*
- Apply the H.A.C.K. script formula:
  - **H**ook (0-3 sec): Pattern interrupt, promise, or controversy
  - **A**gitate (3-15 sec): Intensify the pain or desire — make it feel real
  - **C**ontent (15-50 sec): Deliver the promised value — specific, actionable, surprising
  - **K**icker (50-60 sec): CTA + teaser to the next video ("Follow for Part 2")
- For educational content: Add the "Aha moment" at second 30-40 — the single insight that makes sharing feel like a gift to their audience

*Phase 5: Distribution & Algorithm Optimization*

- LinkedIn Video: First 3 lines of caption must work without clicking "more", tag people not brands, post Tuesday-Thursday 8-10am

*Phase 6: Measurement & Optimization System*
- Weekly metrics review: View velocity (first 48 hours), completion rate (aim for 60%+), profile visits per 1K views, follower conversion rate
- Monthly audit: Top 3 performing videos — what's the common pattern? Repeat the hook, format, and topic structure.
- Quarterly pivot: Kill content pillars with <40% average completion rate; double down on 2 highest performers

## Success Metrics

**Platform Growth:**
- Week 1-4: 100+ views per video, >50% completion rate
- Month 2: 1,000+ followers per active platform, at least 1 video hitting 10K+ views
- Month 3: 3+ videos per month hitting 10K views, measurable profile visit → website traffic lift

**Business Outcomes:**
- Demo requests from "found you on TikTok/LinkedIn/YouTube" (track in CRM source field)
- Cost per lead from organic video vs. paid channels (target: 5-10x cheaper than paid)
- Email subscribers or lead magnet downloads from video CTAs

**Content Quality Indicators:**
- Average view duration >40 seconds on 60-second videos (aim for 65%)
- Save rate >2% (saves signal high-value educational content)
- Comment-to-view ratio >0.5% (engagement quality signal)
- Follower conversion rate per 1,000 views: >15 new followers

**Red Flags to Address Immediately:**
- Completion rate <30%: Hook needs revision; first 5 seconds not earning attention
- Zero saves despite views: Content is entertaining but not actionable enough
- Views but no profile visits: CTA not clear or compelling

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Videos getting 200-500 views but stuck there — not breaking out**
Fix: Your content is reaching followers but not getting distributed by the algorithm. This means completion rate is too low or shares/saves are minimal. Audit your hooks — they may be too niche or assume too much context. Rewrite your top 3 videos with more universal hooks that work for a cold audience who has never heard of your brand. Also check: are you posting at peak times? TikTok's sweet spot is 7-9am and 7-9pm local time; LinkedIn is Tuesday-Thursday 8-10am.

**Problem: Getting views and follows but zero demo requests or website traffic**
Fix: Your CTAs are too weak or buried. In short-form video, you have 3 seconds at the end to drive action — most creators waste it with "like and follow." Replace with a specific, benefit-driven CTA: "Link in bio for the free attribution audit template" or "Comment 'TEMPLATE' and I'll DM you the calculator." Use platform-native lead capture (LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms, TikTok Lead Generation Ads) to eliminate the click-to-website friction.

**Problem: Can't maintain 3+ videos/week — content production is taking too long**
Fix: You're producing, not batching. Implement shoot days: block one 2-hour session per week and record 4-5 videos in sequence. Pre-write all scripts using the Advanced Version prompt above (one session generates 5 complete scripts). Use talking-head format with good iPhone audio ($30 clip-on lavalier mic) — it's faster to produce than anything requiring B-roll assembly. If editing takes >30 min per video, you're over-editing: short-form audiences reward authenticity over polish.
