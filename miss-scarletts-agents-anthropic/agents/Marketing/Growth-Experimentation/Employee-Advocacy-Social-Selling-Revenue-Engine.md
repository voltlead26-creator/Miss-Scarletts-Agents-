## Integration Tips

**HubSpot:**
- Create a custom activity type "LinkedIn Social Touch" — log every meaningful DM, comment, or content share against the contact record
- Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator + HubSpot integration to sync profile views and connection activity
- Build a "Social Touched" contact property and use it in attribution reporting
- Set up a workflow: contact property "LinkedIn SSI engagement" → notify assigned AE

**Salesforce:**
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator for Salesforce app syncs engagement data directly to contact/lead records
- Create a custom field "Last Social Touch Date" on Opportunity to measure social-influenced deal velocity
- Build a report: Opportunities with social touches vs. without — compare close rate and cycle time
- Add social engagement to activity history on Accounts

**Slack (Internal Program Management):**
- Create #advocacy-wins channel where employees share post results
- Bot integration: auto-post weekly leaderboard every Monday morning
- Zapier workflow: when post hits 100+ likes → auto-celebrate in Slack

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Employees won't post consistently after Week 2 launch enthusiasm fades.**
Fix: Reduce friction — instead of asking employees to write posts, give them 3 options to choose from each week via Slack poll. Pre-approved, personalized, ready to copy-paste in 60 seconds. Add a monthly "top advocate" spotlight in all-hands to social-proof the behavior.

**Problem: Executive posts are getting engagement but not generating pipeline leads.**
Fix: Add conversion architecture to executive content — every 4th post should include a subtle CTA (poll that captures opinions, request for DMs, link to a relevant resource with UTM). Use Taplio or Shield Analytics to identify what content is attracting ideal ICP profiles in the comments, then engage those individuals directly.

**Problem: Sales team social selling feels forced and prospects aren't responding to DMs.**
Fix: The sequences are too pitch-forward or too generic. Audit the last 10 DMs sent — identify language that triggers defensiveness ("I wanted to reach out because..."). Replace with curiosity-based openers that reference something hyper-specific to the prospect. Require reps to spend 5 minutes on research before any outreach; this alone typically 3x response rates.
