## Overview

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: MER is above target but nCAC is rising — how to diagnose?**
This pattern usually means you're growing primarily through repeat buyers and organic, not new customer acquisition. Check: (1) Is repeat purchase rate up? If yes, retention is strong but acquisition is stalling. (2) Run a new-customer-only ROAS report in Meta — filter by "New Customers" using Shopify data in Meta's reporting. (3) If nCAC is rising despite stable blended ROAS, you have a creative fatigue problem — your best audiences have been saturated. Launch 5 new creative angles immediately.

**Problem: Subscription conversion rate won't move above 15% despite multiple email tests**
Subscription pitch is likely hitting the wrong moment in the customer journey OR the subscription value proposition isn't compelling. Audit: (1) Is the 20%+ discount compelling enough? DTC average subscription discount is 15-20%. (2) Are buyers experiencing product quality issues before Day 21? Check review sentiment + return rate. (3) Test subscription-first messaging on the product page itself, not just email — some buyers want to subscribe at point of purchase if the value is clear. (4) Consider a "pause" option — many customers don't subscribe because they fear lock-in; a "pause anytime" message on the subscription CTA lifts conversion 8-12%.

**Problem: TikTok ads drain budget with no conversions**
TikTok DTC requires creative-market fit before scaling. Diagnose: (1) Is your CPM on TikTok competitive with Meta? TikTok CPMs for consumer brands typically run $8-18 vs. Meta $12-28. If TikTok CPM is >$25, your audience targeting is too narrow — switch to broad. (2) Is your creative truly TikTok-native? Repurposed Instagram content fails on TikTok — hire 3 TikTok-native creators and give them full creative freedom for 30 days before judging the channel. (3) Are you using Spark Ads? Organic-style Spark Ads typically outperform regular in-feed ads by 30-40% CTR for DTC brands.
