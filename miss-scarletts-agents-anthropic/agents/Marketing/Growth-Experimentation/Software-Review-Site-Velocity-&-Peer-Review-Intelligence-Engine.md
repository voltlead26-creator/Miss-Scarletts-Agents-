## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot:** Create a "Review Readiness" contact property (score 0–100) using workflow enrollment triggers. Enroll contacts scoring 70+ into review request sequence automatically. Use custom events (feature milestone reached, NPS submitted) as sequence triggers.
- **Salesforce:** Build a Review_Requested__c field on the Contact object. Create a Process Builder flow that triggers review outreach 30 days post-renewal or 7 days post-expansion close. Route negative review alerts to the owning CSM via Chatter.
- **G2 Buyer Intent (G2 Market Intelligence):** Connect G2 intent signals to HubSpot/SFDC via native integration. When a target account visits your G2 page, auto-enroll the primary contact in a "warm outbound" sequence — review request + sales follow-up.
- **Clay.com:** Build a "Review Readiness Waterfall" table pulling from HubSpot usage data, Mixpanel events, and Intercom NPS scores. Export enriched contact list to Apollo or Outreach for sequencing.
- **Zapier:** Monitor new competitor reviews via G2 RSS feed → parse with GPT-4o → auto-populate competitive intel Notion database → weekly Slack digest to Sales and Product.
- **Gong.io:** Flag calls where customer mentions high ROI or specific feature praise → auto-tag contact in CRM as "Review Candidate" → trigger review request within 24 hours of call.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Low outreach-to-review conversion rate (under 8%)  
**Solution:** The #1 failure point is generic outreach. Personalize to a specific ROI moment or product milestone in the first sentence — not just "Hi [First Name]." Use HubSpot/Salesforce tokens to pull the customer's actual usage data (e.g., "You've created 47 reports this quarter"). Test subject lines referencing their company outcome vs. a generic product reference. A/B test direct G2 link vs. a pre-filled review form using G2's Campaign Builder tool (requires G2 license).

**Problem:** Reviews are getting flagged or removed by the platform  
**Solution:** Review platforms (especially G2 and Gartner) verify reviewer identity and employment. Ensure outreach targets only verified users with business email domains who actively use the product. Never offer cash incentives — gift cards and charitable donations require explicit platform approval (G2 has a formal incentive program). Space out outreach so you're not sending to 50 customers in one day — velocity spikes trigger fraud detection. Submit reviews via the platform's official campaign links, not direct profile links.

**Problem:** Competitor has 5x more reviews and is winning deals on social proof alone  
**Solution:** Execute a "review sprint" — dedicate 2 weeks to activating your top 20 power users simultaneously with a high-touch concierge approach (CSM sends personal video, offers 15-min walkthrough call to help write the review). Meanwhile, redirect paid budget temporarily to capture competitor review-site comparison traffic ("[Competitor] alternative" keywords on G2 and Capterra). Also activate your advisory board and Customer Advisory Board (CAB) members — they are pre-committed champions who will leave detailed, high-quality reviews.
