## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Low named account reach (below 50% of targets) after 30 days**
Fix: Your IP-to-account database match rate may be low. Check: (1) Verify your ABM platform is pushing fresh IP data to your DSP — enterprise IPs rotate quarterly. (2) Increase CPM bids 20-30% on Tier 1 lines to compete in premium auctions. (3) Expand title targeting to include Director-level in addition to VP/C-Suite — buying committees are larger than you think. (4) Add office locations (not just HQ) for large enterprise targets.

**Problem: High impression volume but zero pipeline influence showing in CRM**
Fix: This is almost always an integration failure, not a campaign failure. Check: (1) Confirm your DSP is passing exposure logs with IP addresses to your attribution vendor. (2) Verify your IP-to-account matching provider (Clearbit, 6sense, or Demandbase) is updating Salesforce/HubSpot account records with CTV exposure data. (3) Check view-through attribution window — if set to 1 day, extend to 7 days (industry standard for CTV). (4) Ensure customer suppression list is loaded correctly so you're not showing ads to existing accounts that inflate "influenced" numbers.

**Problem: Video completion rate below 88% despite CTV placement**
Fix: You likely have OLV (online video on mobile/desktop) mixed into your CTV line items. CTV is non-skippable and should deliver 92%+ VCR. Check: (1) Add "Connected TV only" environment filter in your DSP — exclude "In-Stream Video" and "Online Video" environments. (2) Review your supply path: FAST channel inventory (Pluto TV, Tubi) sometimes delivers OLV alongside CTV — add explicit publisher-level environment filters. (3) Verify you're not running in a fraudulent CTV environment — check IVT (Invalid Traffic) rate; if above 2%, add pre-bid fraud protection (DoubleVerify or IAS).
