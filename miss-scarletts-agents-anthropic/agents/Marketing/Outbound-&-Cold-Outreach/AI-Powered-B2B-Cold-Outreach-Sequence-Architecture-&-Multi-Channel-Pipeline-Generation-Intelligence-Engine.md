## Overview
Designs fully automated, signal-triggered multi-channel cold outreach sequences (email + LinkedIn + phone) with AI-personalized messaging for each prospect. Produces ready-to-deploy sequences that your SDR team or AI SDR tools (Apollo, Outreach, Salesloft, Instantly, Smartlead, 11x, Artisan) can execute without manual intervention. Use this when launching a new outbound motion, entering a new market segment, or rebuilding an underperforming cold outreach program.

# DELIVERABLE 6: COMPLIANCE & DELIVERABILITY FRAMEWORK

**Email deliverability protection:**
- Domain setup: Use a secondary sending domain (e.g., getdomain.com vs. domain.com) — configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC records before first send
- Warm-up protocol: New domains require 4–6 week warm-up using Instantly Warmup or Lemwarm — start at 5 emails/day, scale 5 per day per week
- Send volume limits: Cap at 40–50 emails/day per mailbox until 90-day domain reputation is established
- List hygiene: Verify all emails through ZeroBounce or NeverBounce before uploading to sequences — remove role-based addresses (info@, sales@, support@)
- Bounce rate threshold: Pause sequences immediately if bounce rate exceeds 3% in any 24-hour period

**Legal compliance (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL):**
- CAN-SPAM (US): Include physical address in email footer, one-click unsubscribe mechanism, honor opt-outs within 10 business days
- GDPR (EU/UK): Cold B2B email requires "legitimate interest" basis — document your legitimate interest reasoning; include privacy policy link; add unsubscribe to every email
- CASL (Canada): Requires express consent OR demonstrable existing business relationship for commercial email — consult legal before targeting Canadian prospects
- LinkedIn messaging: LinkedIn's terms prohibit automated connection requests — use Sales Navigator InMail where volume is a concern, or use tools that mimic human behavior and stay within LinkedIn's rate limits

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Open rates are below 30% despite targeting correct ICP**
Solution: Deliverability issue, not a messaging issue. Check domain health at mail-tester.com and MXToolbox. Verify SPF, DKIM, DMARC records are correctly configured. Pause sequences and run a 2-week re-warm using Lemwarm or Instantly Warmup at 20 emails/day. Check if your sending domain is on any blacklists. Do not attempt to fix with better subject lines until deliverability is confirmed healthy.

**Problem: Open rates are fine (45%+) but reply rate is below 3%**
Solution: Messaging resonance issue. The subject line is getting opens but the body isn't earning replies. Audit Touch 1 against these criteria: (1) Is the first sentence about THEM, not you? (2) Is the pain specific enough that they feel seen, not generic? (3) Is the CTA a micro-commitment, not a calendar request? Run a 2-week test of the "Pattern Interrupt" format (Touch 8 style) as your new Touch 1 — short, single question, no pitch.

**Problem: Getting replies but can't book meetings — prospects say "send more info" or "not now"**
Solution: Two separate fixes. For "send more info": implement the objection response from the library ("What's most relevant for you — competitor comparison, customer story, or ROI math?") to convert information requests into conversations.
