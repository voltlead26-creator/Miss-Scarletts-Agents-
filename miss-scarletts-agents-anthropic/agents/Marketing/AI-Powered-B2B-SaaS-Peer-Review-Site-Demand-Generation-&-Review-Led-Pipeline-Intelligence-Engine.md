## Integration Tips

- **G2 Buyer Intent → Salesforce/HubSpot:** Use G2's native Salesforce integration or Zapier to route intent signals as Salesforce Tasks/HubSpot Activities within 15 minutes. Tag accounts with intent signal type for SDR personalization.
- **HubSpot Workflow Automation:** Trigger review request emails via HubSpot Lifecycle Stage changes (Customer → Promoter) or custom NPS property updates. Use Smart Content to personalize by industry.
- **6sense / Bombora Integration:** Layer G2 intent data on top of 6sense account intelligence to prioritize intent signals by ICP fit score. Only activate SDR sequence for accounts with ICP score ≥70.
- **Gong / Chorus Call Intelligence:** Search call recordings for "G2" and "review" mentions to identify which customers verbally praised the product but never wrote a review — perfect outreach targets.
- **Customer Success Platforms (Gainsight, Totango, ChurnZero):** Create a "Review Request" playbook in your CS platform triggered by Health Score improvements, QBR completion, or Feature Adoption milestone milestones. This ensures CSMs never miss a high-conversion moment.
- **Slack Alerts:** Build a Slack bot that fires a notification to the relevant SDR channel the moment a target account (from your ICP list) appears in G2 Buyer Intent data. Include account firmographics and signal type in the alert message.
- **G2 Track:** Use G2 Track (G2's own analytics tool) to monitor category position, competitor review velocity, and profile conversion rates in a single dashboard. Export weekly to Google Sheets for CMO reporting.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Review request conversion rate is below 10%**
Solution: Audit the timing of your review asks — most low conversion rates come from asking too early (before value is realized) or too late (after the customer has mentally churned). Add a step before the review ask: a brief check-in call or email that surfaces a specific ROI metric ("Your team saved 4.2 hours per rep per week — did you know that?"). Customers who can articulate their ROI convert at 3–4x the rate of those who can't. Also test switching from generic email asks to CSM-personalized sends — personalized asks convert at 2–3x the rate of automated templates.

**Problem: G2 Buyer Intent signals aren't generating pipeline — SDR follow-up isn't converting**
Solution: The most common cause is SDR messaging that reveals the intent tracking too overtly ("I saw you were on our G2 profile...") which triggers a privacy response. Retrain SDRs to lead with value, not surveillance: "I'm reaching out because companies in [Industry] are evaluating [category] right now — here's a resource that's helped similar teams make the decision faster." Separately, check signal quality — profile views from existing customers, employees, and competitors are common false positives. Filter intent signals by account firmographic fit before routing to SDR.

**Problem: Competitor has 10x more reviews — hard to compete in category rankings**
Solution: Stop competing on volume and compete on recency and specificity. G2's algorithm weights recent reviews heavily — a campaign to get 30 reviews in 60 days will outperform a competitor's 200 stale reviews from 3 years ago. Focus on winning vertical badges (e.g., "Leader in Mid-Market" or "Leader in Financial Services") where the review threshold is lower and the buyer signal is more valuable than generic category rankings. Also invest in G2 Sponsored Content to appear in competitor comparison searches even when your review count is lower.
