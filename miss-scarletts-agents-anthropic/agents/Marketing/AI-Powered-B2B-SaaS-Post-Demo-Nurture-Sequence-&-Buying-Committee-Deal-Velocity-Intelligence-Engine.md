## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot:** Use Deal Stage enrollment triggers to fire sequences automatically when a deal moves to "Demo Completed" stage; use Contact Properties to route to persona-specific sequences; enable Sales Activity logging to suppress nurture when rep sends manual email within 24 hours
- **Salesforce + Pardot/Marketing Cloud:** Build Process Builder flows that enroll contacts in the appropriate journey based on Contact Role (Champion, Economic Buyer, Technical) on the Opportunity; use Engagement Studio branching for behavioral triggers
- **Outreach / Salesloft:** Sync marketing nurture sequences with sales cadences to prevent email collisions; use the "Prospect Active" signal from marketing email opens to trigger same-day sales tasks
- **6sense / Demandbase:** Trigger behavioral emails based on account-level intent signals (pricing page, competitor research) captured in the ABM platform — not just individual contact behavior
- **Slack (Sales Alerts):** Use Zapier or native integrations to push real-time alerts to the #deals channel when economic buyer opens email or champion engages with ROI calculator — this is the highest-converting signal for same-day sales follow-up
- **Calendly / Chili Piper:** Embed frictionless meeting links in all CTAs; track link clicks as a conversion event in CRM and as a trigger to pause nurture and notify sales immediately

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Champion is engaged but Economic Buyer never opens emails**
Solution: Do not rely on email alone for the economic buyer. After Day 7 with no opens, trigger a LinkedIn connection request from the AE referencing the champion ("Sarah suggested I reach out"). Simultaneously send a physical branded leave-behind via direct mail (Sendoso, Reachdesk) with the executive brief. Economic buyers at VP+ level respond to multi-channel, peer-validated outreach — not cold sequences.

**Problem: Sequence is too generic and prospects disengage after email 2**
Solution: The Advanced Version prompt requires you to input specific demo intelligence (exact features shown, objections raised, agreed next step). If your sales team isn't capturing this in CRM post-demo, implement a mandatory "Demo Debrief" field in Salesforce/HubSpot before the deal can advance. Without demo-specific personalization, post-demo nurture performs at commodity email benchmarks (8–12% open rate). With specific personalization, you should see 35–50%.

**Problem: Marketing and sales emails are conflicting and prospects are getting contacted 4–5 times per week**
Solution: Implement a suppression rule: if a sales rep logs any outreach activity (call, email, LinkedIn message) in CRM within 24 hours, pause all marketing automation for that contact for 48 hours. Add a "Marketing Cadence Active" field to the Contact record so reps can see and manually pause sequences. Set a maximum of 2 marketing touches per week per contact regardless of sequence enrollment.
