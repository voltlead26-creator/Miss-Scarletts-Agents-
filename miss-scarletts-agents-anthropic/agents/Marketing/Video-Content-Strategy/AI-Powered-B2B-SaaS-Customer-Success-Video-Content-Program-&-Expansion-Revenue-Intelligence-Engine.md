## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Low completion rates (<40%) on onboarding videos despite good watch starts**
Root cause: Videos are too long or lack role-specific relevance; Admin users watching End User content and vice versa.
Fix: Segment videos by persona at the trigger level — don't send Admin walkthrough videos to end users. Cap Tier 1 AI avatar videos at 4 minutes; add YouTube-style chapter markers so viewers can jump to their relevant section. A/B test a 90-second "teaser + full video" format against the full video alone.

**Problem: Video trigger automations fail to fire in Gainsight or HubSpot**
Root cause: Vidyard/Loom webhook events not mapping to the correct CRM contact record, or webhook authentication tokens expiring.
Fix: Audit webhook payloads in your CS platform's API logs — confirm that the video view event includes the contact email as the identifier. Use Zapier or Make.com as an integration middleware if native webhooks are unreliable. Set up a weekly automated test that fires a dummy video event and checks that the CRM activity is logged.

**Problem: Cannot cleanly attribute NRR lift to video program because CSM activities are correlated**
Root cause: Video engagement and CSM attention are confounded — high-touch accounts get both, making causal attribution impossible without a control group.
Fix: Design a 90-day holdout experiment at the next cohort of new customers: randomly assign 20% to a "CSM outreach only, no proactive video" control group. At 12 months, compare NRR, feature adoption breadth, and expansion rate using a two-sample t-test (significance threshold: p < 0.05). Present this as your board-ready Video ROI proof point.
