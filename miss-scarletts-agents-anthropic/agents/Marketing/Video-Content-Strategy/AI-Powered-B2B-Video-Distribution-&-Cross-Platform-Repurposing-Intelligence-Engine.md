### MODULE 3: SHORT-FORM VERTICAL CLIP PRODUCTION

For each clip (apply to all platforms):

**Hook Reengineering for Short-Form**:
The first 3 seconds determine 60% of watch time. Rewrite hooks using:
- The "Stolen Secret" pattern: "Most [job titles] don't know this about [topic]..."
- The "Controversial Claim" pattern: "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's the data."
- The "Specific Number" pattern: "[Exact number] [job titles] made this mistake last quarter."
- The "Before/After" pattern: "Before I show you [result], let me show you what [problem] actually costs you."

**Caption Strategy**:
- First line: replicate the spoken hook word-for-word (accessibility + algorithm signal)
- Lines 2–4: one-sentence context
- Line 5: CTA with link-in-bio or direct URL where permitted
- Never use more than 3 hashtags — use niche B2B tags (#b2bsales, #revenueoperations, #saasmarketing) over broad tags (#marketing, #business)

**Platform-Specific Adaptations**:

*LinkedIn Video*: 
- Optimal length: 45–90 seconds for thought leadership, 90–3 min for educational
- Must work without audio (65% of LinkedIn video consumed on mute)
- Add subtitles burned-in or via LinkedIn's auto-caption + manual correction
- Native upload only (no YouTube links — algorithm suppresses external links)
- Post as a personal post, not company page post (3x higher reach for personal)

*TikTok*:
- Optimal length: 30–60 seconds for B2B discovery, up to 3 minutes for deep educational
- Hook must be visual — add a B-roll overlay or text animation in first 2 seconds
- Trending audio increases discoverability by up to 40% for non-viral accounts
- Use the TikTok Creator Search Insights tool to validate topic demand before posting

*YouTube Shorts*:
- Vertical 9:16 only, under 60 seconds
- Add a chapter bookmark in the parent long-form video pointing to this exact segment
- Title should be a standalone search query (e.g., "How to fix pipeline forecast variance in 3 steps")
- Link Shorts to the parent video in the description to drive watch time on the full asset

*Instagram Reels*:
- Lower B2B pipeline conversion than LinkedIn/YouTube — treat as brand awareness only
- Best performing B2B Reels: behind-the-scenes, personal narrative, tactical "quick tip"
- Collab post with a relevant industry creator or customer account to double reach

## Integration Tips

- **Descript**: Use Descript's "Studio Sound" + auto-transcript to clean up audio and auto-generate subtitles. Export separate files for LinkedIn (square crop), TikTok/Reels (vertical 9:16), and YouTube (16:9).
- **Opus Clip**: Feed raw recordings to Opus Clip's AI — it detects high-engagement moments and auto-generates short-form clips with captions. Score clips by "virality score" before publishing.
- **HubSpot / Pardot**: Embed videos using Wistia (not YouTube) in email to enable view-time tracking in your MAP. Create a HubSpot workflow: "If contact watches >50% of video → enroll in bottom-funnel sequence."
- **Salesforce**: Append video engagement data (Wistia or Vidyard integrations) to lead records. Create a custom field "Video Engagement Score" to include in MQL thresholds and opportunity alerts.
- **Notion / Airtable**: Maintain a "Video Asset Vault" — every video + its derivative assets, UTM links, performance data, and repost schedule in one trackable database. This prevents content being used once and forgotten.
- **Zapier**: Set up a Zap — "New YouTube video published → Create LinkedIn draft → Create email draft → Notify SDR Slack channel." Reduces distribution lag from 5 days to same-day.
- **Later / Buffer**: Schedule all derivative social assets immediately after clip export. Map clips to a 30-day rolling distribution calendar so no video competes with itself for audience attention.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Short-form clips get low completion rates (<15%) despite strong source content.
**Solution**: The issue is almost always the hook — B2B audiences need a reason to watch within the first 2 seconds. Audit the clip's first frame: is there a text overlay visible before the presenter speaks? Does the verbal hook contain a specific number or provocative claim? Rebuild the hook using the "Specific Number" or "Controversial Claim" patterns above. Test 2 hook variants before assuming the topic itself is the problem.

**Problem**: YouTube long-form video gets views but zero pipeline attribution.
**Solution**: Video SEO drives views; CTAs drive pipeline. Audit these four areas in sequence: (1) Is the end screen CTA live and linking to a functional conversion page? (2) Is the pinned comment present with a direct CTA link? (3) Does the video description contain a UTM-tagged link in the first 150 characters? (4) Is the video embedded on a landing page with a form above the fold? If all four are in place but conversion is still < 0.5%, the offer itself needs testing — try a free tool or assessment instead of a generic demo request.

**Problem**: LinkedIn video posts on the company page underperform vs. benchmarks.
**Solution**: LinkedIn's algorithm heavily favors personal profiles over company pages for video content — typical personal post reach is 3–8x higher for the same content. Move distribution to the CEO, VP Marketing, or top-performing AEs' personal profiles. Have them post natively (not share the company post) with a personal perspective in the caption. Company page videos work best for retargeting paid campaigns, not organic reach.
