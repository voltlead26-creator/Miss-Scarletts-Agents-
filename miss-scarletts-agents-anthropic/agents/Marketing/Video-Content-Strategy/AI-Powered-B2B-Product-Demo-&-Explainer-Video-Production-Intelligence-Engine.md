## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI presenter looks/sounds "robotic" and ICP disengages by 0:15**
Fix: (1) Slow down avatar speaking pace to 115-120 WPM in HeyGen settings — most defaults are too fast. (2) Add [PAUSE 0.8s] tags after every third sentence in the script. (3) Enable "breathing" and "micro-expression" settings if available. (4) Test a real-person Loom recording for the homepage and use AI only for email variants — conversion data will tell you which performs better.

**Problem: Video-to-meeting conversion rate is below 4% despite good watch time**
Fix: Your CTA is almost certainly the problem, not the video. Run this diagnostic: (1) Is the CTA appearing only at the end? Add a mid-roll CTA chapter marker at the 50% mark. (2) Does the CTA ask for too much commitment? Replace "Book a demo" with "See it configured for your stack." (3) Is the post-click landing page consistent with the video promise? If the video promises "10 minutes to live data" but the landing page shows a 3-field form requiring a sales call, conversion will collapse.

**Problem: SDR team isn't using the video variants in their outreach**
Fix: This is a workflow adoption problem, not a video quality problem. (1) Build a one-click "Send Demo Video" button directly into your Outreach/Salesloft email templates using Vidyard's Chrome extension. (2) Create an SDR performance report that shows meeting-booked rate for reps who use video vs. those who don't — make it visible in the weekly team review. (3) Recognize the first SDR to book a meeting via video in Slack with the actual Wistia engagement screenshot — social proof drives adoption.
