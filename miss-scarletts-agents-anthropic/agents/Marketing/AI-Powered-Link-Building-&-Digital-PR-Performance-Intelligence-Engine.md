## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Outreach emails get no replies despite good prospect list quality.
**Solution:** Add a specific reference to a piece of content on their site in the first line — generic emails convert at 3-5%, hyper-personalized ones at 15-22%. Also A/B test subject lines; "noticed a gap" and curiosity-gap frames consistently outperform "I'd love to collaborate" openers.

**Problem:** DR isn't moving after 60+ new referring domains.
**Solution:** Check domain distribution — 60 links from 3 root domains count as 3 referring domains, not 60. Also audit anchor text: over-optimized exact-match anchors trigger algorithmic suppression. Diversify to branded and navigational anchors. Verify no major links are nofollowed or from deindexed pages.

**Problem:** CMO/leadership is skeptical of link-building ROI and wants to cut the program.
**Solution:** Use the ROI formula from Deliverable 6 to build a 12-month projection tied to specific pipeline numbers. Show a competitor authority gap chart — "Competitor X has DR 62, we have DR 28; closing to DR 45 unlocks these 47 keywords where they currently rank and we don't." Concrete keyword→traffic→revenue math converts skeptics faster than organic growth narratives.
