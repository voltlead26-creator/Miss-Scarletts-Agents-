## Overview

Architects a full-funnel B2B YouTube Ads program that combines TrueView In-Stream, Video Discovery, Bumper, and Connected TV ads to move enterprise buyers from first awareness to demo request — with every video impression and view mapped to pipeline in Salesforce or HubSpot. Use when entering a new market where buyers don't yet know your category exists, scaling beyond $15K/month in paid media and needing video to lower CPL, running account-based video advertising against a CRM target account list, or proving YouTube's pipeline contribution to a CFO who only sees brand spend with no revenue tie.

**Three Signals to Scale a Campaign:**
1. Cost-per-pipeline below channel average with 20+ qualified opportunities in 30 days
2. View-through conversion rate above 1.5% for Video Action campaigns to warm audiences

1. View rate below 15% after 2,000+ impressions (creative or audience mismatch)
2. Zero conversions from cold audience campaigns after $2,000 spend and 500+ views
3. Cost-per-demo exceeds 3x your target CPL for more than 14 consecutive days

## Integration Tips

- **Google Ads + Salesforce/HubSpot CRM**: Use Zapier or native integrations to automate GCLID capture from form fills to CRM contact records. This is the single most important technical prerequisite for offline conversion import — without GCLID, you cannot attribute pipeline to YouTube campaigns. Salesforce has a native Google Ads connector; HubSpot has built-in Google Ads conversion sync.
- **Google Analytics 4**: Link GA4 to Google Ads and import GA4 conversion events as secondary signals. Use GA4's Attribution reporting to compare YouTube-assisted vs. YouTube-last-click pipeline contributions. The difference reveals YouTube's role in the full consideration journey beyond the last-touch model.
- **Marketo / Pardot**: Capture UTM parameters and GCLID at every form fill and store in lead/contact records. Build a smart list/dynamic list of "YouTube Ad Sourced MQLs" for sales follow-up routing — these leads require different SDR messaging than inbound organic leads (acknowledge the content they watched).
- **Looker Studio / Tableau**: Build the YouTube Pipeline ROI dashboard using Google Ads API data + CRM opportunity data. Use GCLID as the join key between ad click data and CRM opportunity records. Publish a live dashboard to a shared link for CFO/CMO access without requiring them to log in to Google Ads.
- **Vidyard / Wistia**: If using gated video as a conversion mechanism (not just YouTube public videos), connect Vidyard/Wistia viewing data to HubSpot/Marketo contact records. A contact who watched 85%+ of your demo video before requesting a demo is a different conversation than one who submitted a form cold.
- **Salesforce Pardot + Google Ads**: Use the Pardot Google Ads connector to sync Pardot form completions as offline conversions directly — eliminates the need for manual GCLID capture and upload, significantly reducing implementation friction.
- **Notion / Google Sheets**: Use the Creative Brief template from Module 3 in Notion or Google Sheets as a shared production brief for your video agency or in-house team. Include the 5-second hook options, audience assignment, and performance benchmarks so creative teams understand the strategic context for each video asset.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Video Action Campaign serving zero conversions after $1,500+ spend and 500+ views.**
Solution: First, verify conversion tag is firing correctly — use Google Tag Assistant to confirm form-fill pages are triggering the conversion event. Second, check audience size: Customer Match and remarketing audiences below 1,000 users may not meet Google's serving threshold for Video Action Campaigns — expand by broadening audience lookback window from 30 to 90 days or combining remarketing tiers. Third, review the 5-second hook: if view rate is below 20%, creative is being skipped before the offer is communicated — revise hook using the Persona-Specific Mirror formula.

**Problem: YouTube reporting shows strong view counts and CPV, but no pipeline shows up in CRM with YouTube attribution.**
Solution: This is a GCLID capture failure. The most common cause is that your form (HubSpot, Marketo, Pardot) is not capturing the GCLID URL parameter and storing it in a hidden form field. Audit every demo/contact form: add a hidden "GCLID" field that auto-populates from the URL parameter `gclid`. Then confirm the CRM is storing this value on the contact record — it's required to tie a closed-won opportunity back to the originating YouTube click. Without this, all YouTube pipeline shows as "direct" or "unknown" in CRM reports.

**Problem: View-through conversions account for more than 60% of all YouTube attributed conversions, making pipeline ROI look artificially high.**
Solution: Reduce view-through conversion window from 30 days to 7 days for all campaigns. View-through conversions credit YouTube for a conversion that occurred after a user saw (but did not click) a YouTube ad — in long B2B sales cycles this can over-attribute. A 7-day window still captures genuine view-driven behavior while reducing coincidental attribution. Additionally, create a separate column in your pipeline report that shows only click-through conversions — this becomes your conservative, CFO-defensible YouTube attribution metric while view-through data informs creative effectiveness analysis.
