## Overview
This prompt audits your current marketing team's task landscape, maps automation potential for every function, and outputs a complete workforce restructuring plan — including new hybrid role designs, headcount ROI analysis, and a 90-day AI adoption roadmap. Use it when a CMO must restructure the marketing org to operate at 3-5x output with the same or reduced headcount.

Output a board-ready workforce restructuring proposal with supporting data.

## Integration Tips

**HubSpot:** Map the new AI-assisted workflows to HubSpot Workflows and Custom Properties. Use the "AI Operator" role to manage all HubSpot automation sequences. Set up custom dashboards tracking AI agent output volume vs. human-reviewed output.

**Salesforce:** Build a "Marketing AI Activity" custom object to log every AI-generated asset tied to campaign attribution. This lets you run AI content ROI reports inside Salesforce.

**Notion / Confluence:** Create a central "AI Playbook" page for each automated workflow — prompt templates, approval checklists, and quality rubrics. AI Operators own these pages and update them monthly.

**Zapier / Make (Integromat):** Use Zapier to wire triggers between your AI agents — e.g., when a new ICP company is added to Salesforce, trigger the Account Research Agent, which feeds the Personalized Outreach Agent, which queues a LinkedIn message draft for human approval.

**Google Sheets:** Build the headcount ROI model in Google Sheets using the financial template from Phase 4 above. Share with CFO and CEO before any headcount decisions.

**Rippling / Workday / BambooHR:** Coordinate role eliminations and new hires through your HRIS system with a 60-day notice period minimum. Tag restructured roles in the HRIS with "AI-Restructured: [date]" for compliance tracking.

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: The AI automation scores seem too optimistic — the prompt says "fully automatable" for tasks that still feel risky.**
Fix: Add this instruction to the prompt: "Apply a 2026 production-readiness filter — only mark a task as 'fully automatable' if you would bet your own performance review on the AI output requiring zero human review. When in doubt, classify as AI-Assisted."

**Issue: The restructuring plan recommends eliminating roles but doesn't account for institutional knowledge loss.**
Fix: Add to the company context: "We have [X] employees with deep institutional knowledge in [areas]. Flag any role elimination that would create a knowledge gap and recommend a documentation sprint (prompt-driven) before the role is eliminated."

**Issue: The financial model outputs don't account for ramp time — AI agents don't produce Day 1 results.**
Fix: Add this constraint: "Assume AI agents reach 80% of target output quality by Day 45 and 100% by Day 90. Model revenue/pipeline impact accordingly — do not credit full output gains until Month 4."
