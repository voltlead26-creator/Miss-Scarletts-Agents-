## Integration Tips

- **Salesforce / HubSpot → LinkedIn Matched Audiences:** Use native connectors (LinkedIn Insight Tag + CRM sync) to auto-refresh closed-lost and MQL audiences daily. Avoid manual CSV uploads — they introduce 24–72-hour lag and audience decay.
- **Google Customer Match:** Upload CRM lists of closed-lost contacts and demo no-shows as Customer Match audiences in Google Ads. Minimum 1,000 users required for activation. Refresh weekly via a scheduled CRM export + Google Ads API upload (Zapier or native HubSpot/Salesforce integration handles this).
- **Meta Custom Audiences:** Upload suppression lists (current customers, active pipeline) as "Exclusion" Custom Audiences. Use Meta's Customer List Custom Audience with email and phone for maximum match rates (typically 40–65% for B2B).
- **6sense / Bombora Intent Data → Account Retargeting Escalation:** Pipe weekly intent surge alerts into your CRM, then trigger LinkedIn Company Targeting escalation automatically via a Zapier workflow: Intent Surge detected → Add to "High-Intent Account" LinkedIn Audience → Increase daily bid cap by 30%.
- **Google Tag Manager:** Set up event-based pixel firing for each high-value page (pricing, demo, case study, ROI calculator) to create granular retargeting audiences. Each unique page-visit event becomes its own audience in Google Ads, enabling precise sequential retargeting.
- **Looker Studio / Tableau:** Build a unified retargeting dashboard pulling from LinkedIn Campaign Manager API, Google Ads API, and CRM pipeline data. Track incrementality test results alongside standard CTR/CPO metrics in one view.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Retargeting audiences are too small to activate (LinkedIn requires 300+ members, Google requires 1,000+ users)**
Solution: Broaden the pixel lookback window (extend from 14 days to 30 days for Tier 1 audiences, 60 days for Tier 2). For B2B with low traffic volume (<5,000 monthly visitors), consolidate granular audiences — combine "pricing page" and "demo page" abandoners into a single "High-Intent Page Visitors" audience. For CRM-based audiences, layer in contacts from related stages (e.g., combine Closed-Lost + Stalled SQL into one LinkedIn Matched Audience of 500+ to hit the threshold).

**Problem: Retargeting CPM is 3–5x higher than prospecting campaigns on LinkedIn**
Solution: This is expected for highly targeted B2B retargeting — your audiences are smaller and more competitive. The correct metric is CPO, not CPM. A $120 CPM retargeting audience that converts at 4% CPO = $3,000 CPO. A $30 CPM prospecting audience that converts at 0.2% = $15,000 CPO. If CPO is still unfavorable after 4 weeks, expand the audience (less specific job titles, longer lookback window) or shift budget to Google Display which offers significantly lower CPMs for similar retargeting intent.

**Problem: Incrementality test shows no statistically significant lift over holdout group**
Solution: Check three things first: (1) Is the holdout group truly excluded from all platforms, including organic social and direct email? Cross-channel contamination is the #1 cause of false-negative incrementality results. (2) Is the test running long enough? For B2B with 90-day sales cycles, a 30-day test captures only top-of-funnel conversion signals — extend to 60 days minimum. (3) Is the audience size large enough? You need at least 500 users in each group for statistical power at 80% confidence. If all three are validated and lift is still flat, the audience may not have sufficient buying intent — move budget to higher-tier audiences.
