## Overview

This prompt builds a comprehensive AI-powered system for generating demand across every organic surface where B2B buyers now research — Google AI Overviews, featured snippets, YouTube search, Reddit, LinkedIn search, Perplexity-style AI answers, and more — capturing brand impressions and pipeline from buyers who never click a link. Use it when your organic traffic is declining despite strong keyword rankings, or when you need to generate demand in an AI-first search environment.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** AI Overviews appear for target queries but consistently cite competitors instead of our brand.
**Solution:** The issue is almost always source authority, not content quality. Identify which publications AI tools are pulling from (they typically cite Gartner, G2, HubSpot, Forbes, industry-specific tier-1 publications). Build a targeted earned media program to get your company quoted in exactly those sources. Simultaneously, create an original research report with proprietary statistics — AI tools heavily favor original data sources. Timeline: 60–90 days to see citation improvement.

**Problem:** Reddit community participation gets flagged as self-promotional and posts are removed.
**Solution:** The rule is: give value first, 10x more than you take. For the first 30 days, contribute only answers to questions where you never mention your product — build karma and community trust. When you do reference your product, do it only when directly asked, with explicit disclosure ("I work at [Company] but genuinely believe..."). The best approach is to have employees who are genuine community members participate authentically, not a marketing team with a content calendar.

**Problem:** YouTube videos get organic views but zero conversion to demo requests.
**Solution:** The conversion path is usually broken, not the content. Fix: (1) Add a pinned first comment with a specific, low-friction CTA ("Download the template from this video: [link]"); (2) Include a verbal CTA at the 60% video mark, not just at the end; (3) Add info cards with product demo links; (4) Create a dedicated landing page for each video's CTA that matches the video's topic exactly, not your generic homepage; (5) Offer a high-value content upgrade specific to the video topic (e.g., "The exact automation template we built in this video").
