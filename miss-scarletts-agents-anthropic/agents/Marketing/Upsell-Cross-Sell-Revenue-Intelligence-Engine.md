## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Expansion score is high but the customer isn't responding to outreach**
Fix: Check if you're reaching the champion vs. an end user. Run Module 5's economic buyer outreach (Touch 5) earlier in the sequence — the champion may need air cover from above. Also verify the NPS score is recent; stale NPS data can give false-positive health signals on accounts that have since disengaged.

**Problem: Customer says "we're happy but not looking to expand right now"**
Fix: Shift the frame from "upgrade" to "protection." Use the consumption limit angle — position the expansion as insurance against performance degradation as they scale, not a discretionary investment. If API limits are genuinely not a concern, pivot to the Data Governance or SSO angle tied to their compliance posture post-funding.

**Problem: The ROI numbers feel generic and the customer pushes back**
Fix: Re-run Module 3 with more specific data — ask your CS team for actual hours saved, error rates before/after implementation, or revenue attributed to decisions made with your product. Pull a peer benchmark ("Companies your size in FinTech using our Enterprise tier see X% reduction in data pipeline incidents"). Specificity always beats generic ROI claims.
