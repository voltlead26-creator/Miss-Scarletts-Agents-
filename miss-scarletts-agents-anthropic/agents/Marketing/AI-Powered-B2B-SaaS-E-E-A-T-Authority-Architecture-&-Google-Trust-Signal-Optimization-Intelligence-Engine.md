## Overview

This prompt architects a comprehensive E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) program for B2B SaaS companies — diagnosing current trust signal deficiencies, benchmarking against top-ranking competitors, and producing a prioritized authority-building roadmap that directly recovers and protects organic rankings. Use it when content quality is high but rankings are suppressed, after a Google core algorithm update tanks organic traffic, or when building an organic search program that needs to outrank established incumbents.

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Leadership won't invest in E-E-A-T without clearer ROI proof**
- Fix: Run the prompt with your actual pre/post traffic data from Google core updates that impacted you. The output will calculate specific pipeline at risk using your organic CVR and ACV. Then add: "Ask the AI to model the incremental organic pipeline recovery if we move from Position 8 to Position 4 on our top 5 target keywords." Use this as the business case slide. Frame it as pipeline insurance, not SEO investment.

**Issue: We don't have internal experts willing to be named authors**
- Fix: Start with external contributors. Recruit 2-3 respected practitioners in your category on LinkedIn — offer a $300-500 honorarium per article plus promotion to their personal audience. This is faster to implement than building internal thought leadership from scratch, and external expert signals are often MORE powerful E-E-A-T signals than internal team members.

**Issue: Output recommends changes our engineering team won't prioritize (author schema, etc.)**
- Fix: Use the prompt output to generate a ticket-ready engineering spec: "Expand the author schema implementation section into a developer ticket with exact JSON-LD examples, implementation location in our [Next.js/WordPress/Webflow] template, QA test criteria, and a Google Rich Results Test validation step." This removes all ambiguity and makes approval trivial.
