## Overview
Designs complete, AI-personalized LinkedIn outbound sequences—connection requests, DM warm-up flows, and InMail cold campaigns—that convert cold prospects into booked meetings without burning your brand. Unlike generic multi-channel sequences, this engine applies LinkedIn-native psychology (social proof within the platform, mutual connection leverage, content engagement triggers) and respects LinkedIn's unique social norms so your outreach reads as a peer conversation, not a vendor blast. Use this when your email outbound is saturated, when your ICP lives primarily on LinkedIn, or when you want to add a LinkedIn-specific channel to your outbound mix with measurable pipeline attribution.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Connection acceptance rate is below 20%**
*Solution:* Your targeting or connection note is off. Audit in this order: (1) Check if your sender's profile is complete and active (no posts in 30+ days = lower acceptance). (2) Review your connection notes — are they leading with you or with them? Any mention of product, company, or a meeting ask in the note will tank acceptance. (3) Tighten your targeting — only connect with prospects who scored ≥ 15/25 on the Signal Scoring Matrix. (4) Test sending connection requests on Tuesday–Thursday between 8–10am in the prospect's timezone.

**Problem: Connections are accepting but not replying to DMs**
*Solution:* Your DM 1 is triggering the "vendor alarm." Review: (1) Does DM 1 mention your product, company, or ask for a meeting? If yes, rewrite to purely offer value or curiosity. (2) Is the message personalized to something they actually care about, or is it generic? Pull their last 3 LinkedIn posts and rewrite DM 1 to reference one. (3) Check send timing — DMs sent Friday afternoon or Monday morning get buried. Best window: Tuesday–Thursday, 7–9am or 12–1pm local time.

**Problem: LinkedIn is flagging or restricting your account**
*Solution:* You're sending too fast or LinkedIn's algorithm has detected non-human patterns. Immediately: (1) Pause all automation tools if using them — LinkedIn actively detects and bans accounts using bots. (2) Review weekly limits: Sales Navigator Core = 100 connection requests/week; free accounts = 20–25/week. Stay 20% below your limit. (3) Spread activity across the week with random intervals — don't send 30 connection requests in 2 hours. (4) If restricted, stop all outbound for 7 days, post 3x organically, then resume at 50% volume.
