## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Low account reach rate — only 40-50% of target accounts reachable on CTV**
Solution: CTV IP targeting coverage varies significantly by company type and geography. Remote-first companies, small firms, and non-HQ locations have sparse IP data. Expand coverage by: (1) adding a device graph provider (LiveRamp, Experian) to extend from office IP to home devices — this alone typically increases coverage by 15-25%, (2) supplement IP targeting with job-title and company-size audience targeting on platforms like Hulu or Peacock that offer demographic overlays, and (3) run MNTN alongside StackAdapt since each has a different IP database — overlap is typically 20-30%, giving you combined reach above either platform alone.

**Problem: CTV impressions are not showing up in Salesforce or DemandBase account records**
Solution: IP-to-CRM matching is the most common implementation failure in B2B CTV. First, confirm your target account IP list is current — IP addresses change, especially for companies that have moved offices or use cloud/SD-WAN routing. Re-pull IPs quarterly from ZoomInfo or Clearbit. Second, ensure your DSP is passing account-level impression events via webhook or API to your CRM — the native reporting UI almost never matches your ABM platform's account data without a deliberate integration. Work with your DSP's B2B team (MNTN and StackAdapt both have dedicated B2B customer success teams) to validate the data pipeline before scaling spend.

**Problem: CTV is generating impressions but no measurable lift in pipeline or sales engagement**
Solution: CTV is a long-cycle brand channel — expect 60-90 days before pipeline influence becomes statistically visible, especially with a 120-150 day sales cycle. If you see no lift at 90 days, diagnose in this order: (1) creative quality — is the ad communicating the core problem and brand clearly within the first 5 seconds? Test with a small focus group of ICP-matched individuals. (2) Frequency — are accounts receiving enough impressions to drive recall (minimum 8-12 per month for B2B brand awareness)? Check frequency report by account tier. (3) Sales-marketing coordination — are AEs actually following up differently on accounts with high CTV exposure? CTV generates awareness, not inbound leads — the sales team must treat CTV-exposed accounts as warmer and engage proactively. Without that coordination, CTV's impact is invisible.
