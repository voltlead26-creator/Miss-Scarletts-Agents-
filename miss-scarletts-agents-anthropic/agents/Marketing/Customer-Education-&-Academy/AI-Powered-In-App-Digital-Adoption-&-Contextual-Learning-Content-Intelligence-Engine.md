## Overview
Generates a complete in-app digital adoption content system for SaaS products — including product tours, contextual tooltips, behavioral walkthroughs, feature announcement modals, and NPS microsurveys — mapped to user segments, adoption stages, and revenue outcomes. Use this when feature adoption is below 40%, when new users fail to reach their "aha moment" within the first 14 days, or when your CSM team is spending more than 30% of their time on basic how-to questions that should be answered by the product itself.

## Integration Tips

- **Pendo:** Use Segment data from Pendo to export tooltip completion events into Salesforce as activity records — CS teams see in-app adoption data directly on the Account page without switching tools
- **Appcues:** Connect to HubSpot via Zapier — when a user completes the onboarding checklist, automatically move the contact to a "Activated" lifecycle stage and enroll in an expansion nurture sequence
- **WalkMe:** Integrate with Gainsight to surface in-app engagement scores as health score inputs — users with guide completion below 30% automatically trigger a CSM task
- **Intercom:** Use Product Tours completion webhooks to trigger Slack notifications to the CS Slack channel: "🎉 [User Name] at [Account] just completed the Power User Tour"
- **Segment (CDP):** Fire custom events from your DAP tool into Segment to unify in-app behavior with email, CRM, and product analytics — enables true multi-touch attribution of onboarding content to retention outcomes
- **Notion/Confluence:** Use AI to generate all tooltip copy and guide content in a Notion database first — review and approve as a team before deploying into DAP tool for governance at scale
- **Google Sheets:** Build an In-App Content Inventory spreadsheet (Guide Name | Segment | Trigger | Status | Completion Rate | Last Updated) to track content performance and prioritize optimization

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: In-app guides have low completion rates (< 20%) — users dismiss immediately**
Solution: Guides fail when they feel like interruptions, not help. First, audit trigger timing — guides shown on page load always underperform those triggered by a user's first interaction with a feature. Second, check copy: if your headline starts with "Did you know..." or the body is more than 2 sentences, rewrite with a benefit headline and one-sentence value statement. Third, add a progress indicator ("Step 1 of 3") — completion rates increase 40%+ when users know the endpoint.

**Problem: Can't segment in-app content by user role — DAP tool only has email, not role data**
Solution: Add a role-selection step to your welcome modal (3-4 options with icons) and store the selection as a custom user attribute in your DAP tool. Alternatively, use account-level attributes (plan tier, company size from CRM) as proxies for role-based segmentation. Most DAP tools support Salesforce/HubSpot sync to pull CRM fields into segmentation rules within 24 hours of setup.

**Problem: Engineering pushes back on DAP tool installation — concerned about page performance**
Solution: Modern DAP tools (Pendo, Appcues, Chameleon) add < 50ms page load overhead when loaded asynchronously. Run a Lighthouse audit with and without the snippet to quantify actual impact. For WalkMe, use the extension-based deployment for initial pilots before committing to a full JS snippet. Lead with the business case: if the DAP prevents one enterprise churn at $60K ARR, it pays for the annual platform cost in a single save.
