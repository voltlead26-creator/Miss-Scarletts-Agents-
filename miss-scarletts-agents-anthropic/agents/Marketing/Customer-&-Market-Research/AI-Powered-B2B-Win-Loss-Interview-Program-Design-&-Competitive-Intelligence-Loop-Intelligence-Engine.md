## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Buyers won't respond to interview requests — participation rate below 15%
**Solution:** Three fixes that typically 2x participation: (1) Use a third-party research firm email address — "from Veloxa" triggers defensiveness, from "CLM Market Research" triggers curiosity. (2) Lead with the deliverable, not the ask — "I'd like to share our 2025 CLM Benchmark Report with you as a thank-you" outperforms gift cards 2:1. (3) Have your champion or the AE's previous contact make the warm intro — warm intro interviews achieve 65%+ participation vs. 20% cold.

**Problem:** Win/loss data is collected but nothing changes — program becomes a reporting exercise
**Solution:** Assign a mandatory "activation owner" to every insight category at program launch. If a messaging miss is identified, the CMO owns the A/B test within 2 weeks. If a product gap appears in 3+ interviews, the CPO owns a response within 1 week. Publish a weekly "Win/Loss Actions Taken" digest to keep the program accountable.

**Problem:** Sales is providing biased win/loss reasons in CRM ("price" for everything) and it's corrupting the data
**Solution:** Remove free-text loss reason fields. Replace with a structured two-level taxonomy (max 8 Level 1 reasons, 3-5 Level 2 sub-reasons each). Require sales to select *two* reasons — primary and secondary — which forces more nuanced thinking. Audit quarterly: any AE with 80%+ "price" losses gets a coaching conversation with their manager. Price is almost never the only reason.
