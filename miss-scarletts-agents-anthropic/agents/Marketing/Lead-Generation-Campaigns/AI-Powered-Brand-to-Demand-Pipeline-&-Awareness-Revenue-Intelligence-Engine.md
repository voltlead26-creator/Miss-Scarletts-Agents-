## Troubleshooting

**Problem: "We can't prove brand drives pipeline — attribution shows 0% brand contribution."**
Solution: This is a data infrastructure problem, not a brand ROI problem. Implement these fixes: (1) Install UTM parameters on ALL brand content links (podcast promo codes, PR article links, event registration); (2) Add LinkedIn Insight Tag to capture organic social visitors; (3) Set up branded keyword tracking in Google Search Console as a proxy signal; (4) Create a holdout group test — pause brand investment in one geo for 60 days and measure pipeline decline vs control geos.

**Problem: "The lag between brand investment and pipeline makes it impossible to optimize in-quarter."**
Solution: Use leading indicators instead of lagging indicators for in-quarter decisions. Track weekly: branded search volume trend (+/- %), G2 category intent score, outbound reply rate delta in brand-targeted vs non-targeted accounts. These leading indicators move within 2-4 weeks of brand campaigns and predict pipeline 6-8 weeks out. Build a "Brand-to-Pipeline Lag Chart" showing your specific company's historical lag curve.

**Problem: "Sales team doesn't believe brand marketing generates pipeline — they think SDRs do all the work."**
Solution: Run a 90-day A/B test. Divide your ICP accounts into two groups: Group A receives brand touchpoints (LinkedIn retargeting, content, thought leadership) before SDR outreach. Group B receives only direct SDR outreach with no prior brand exposure. Track reply rates, meeting rates, deal velocity, and win rates. The data will show brand-aware accounts convert at 20-40% higher rates — this is your internal sales proof point.
