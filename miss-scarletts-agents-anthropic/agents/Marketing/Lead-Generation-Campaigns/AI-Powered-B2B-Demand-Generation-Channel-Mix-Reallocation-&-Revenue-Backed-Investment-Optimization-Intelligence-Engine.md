## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI recommends cutting too many channels simultaneously, creating execution chaos**
Fix: Add this constraint: "Do not recommend cutting or pausing more than 2 channels in the same 30-day window. Sequence cuts across the quarter to minimize disruption. If more than 2 channels qualify for exit, prioritize by lowest strategic risk to cut first."

**Problem: Pipeline projections seem unrealistically optimistic (e.g., doubling pipeline with same budget)**
Fix: Add: "Apply a 25% confidence haircut to all projected pipeline increases from scaled channels and a 40% haircut to any new untested channel projections. Show the haircut-adjusted number alongside the base projection and explain the discount rate."

**Problem: The output doesn't account for the sales team's capacity to work new leads generated from scaled channels**
Fix: Add this input before the channel table: "Sales capacity constraint: The sales team can handle a maximum of [X] new SQLs per month. Any channel scaling recommendation that would generate more total SQLs than this cap must flag the headcount requirement explicitly and adjust the pipeline projection to match current capacity."
