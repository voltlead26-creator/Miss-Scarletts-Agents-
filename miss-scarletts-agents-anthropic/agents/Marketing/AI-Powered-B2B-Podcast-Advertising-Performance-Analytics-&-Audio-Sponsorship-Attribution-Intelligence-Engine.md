## Overview
Transforms raw podcast sponsorship data — promo code redemptions, vanity URL traffic, download counts, and brand lift signals — into actionable pipeline attribution intelligence. Diagnoses show-level ROI, attribution gap size, optimal host-read vs. dynamic insertion mix, and produces a prioritized portfolio reallocation plan that maximizes cost-per-qualified-opportunity across your entire podcast advertising investment.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Promo code redemption rates dropped significantly for a show we haven't changed
*Solution:* Check three root causes: (1) The host may have changed their read style or enthusiasm — request the actual audio for a quality check; (2) The promo offer may have lost perceived value — test a new offer type (free trial vs. discount vs. bonus content); (3) Audience fatigue from overexposure — check frequency and consider a 4-week hiatus before returning at a fresh cadence.

**Problem:** Finance is threatening to cut podcast budget because direct attribution looks negative
*Solution:* Build the attribution gap case with triangulation data: Present the three-signal model (direct + self-reported + brand lift) to show the full picture. Request a 90-day holdout test: pause one show for 6 weeks and measure branded search volume, inbound volume, and pipeline in the paused window vs. active periods. A well-run holdout test will almost always demonstrate podcast contribution that direct attribution misses — and provides the data to justify continued investment.

**Problem:** Can't determine which shows are actually reaching our ICP
*Solution:* Use three verification methods: (1) Ask each show host/network for their Spotify Audience Network or Podtrac demographic data before signing; (2) Survey your own customers: "What podcasts do you listen to regularly?" — match your customer base podcast listening habits to show selection; (3) Run a test sponsorship on 2–3 episodes of candidate shows with unique promo codes and measure lead quality (not just volume) of respondents using your ICP definition.
