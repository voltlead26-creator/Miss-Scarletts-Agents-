## Troubleshooting

**Problem: ROAS looks artificially high for Google Brand campaigns, skewing blended metrics.**
Fix: Always segment brand vs. non-brand before analyzing blended ROAS. In the advanced prompt, note "separate brand/non-brand" in the constraints section. True paid media efficiency lives in non-brand ROAS.

**Problem: Meta Ads shows strong in-platform ROAS but pipeline from CRM doesn't match.**
Fix: This is iOS 14.5+ signal loss. Add "use CRM pipeline as source of truth for conversion volume; treat Meta in-platform conversions as directional only" to your business context. Run a Meta Conversion Lift test to calibrate the true incrementality.

**Problem: LinkedIn CPA appears extremely high compared to other channels.**
Fix: LinkedIn generates high-quality enterprise leads that close at higher ACV. Always include pipeline value (not just leads) in the data. A $1,200 CPA on a $50K ACV deal is a 40x ROAS — the prompt will correctly flag this as Tier 1 when pipeline data is included.
