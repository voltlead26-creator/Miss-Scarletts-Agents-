## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Comparison pages rank well but conversion rates are below 1%**
*Solution:* Run a CRO audit on the page structure. Common causes: (1) CTA placement too far down the page — test sticky sidebar CTA; (2) comparison table shows competitor favorably in key features — reframe criteria or add "Context" column; (3) "neutral" framing backfires — add more social proof and differentiate the recommendation box. A/B test two versions: one neutral-framing and one that explicitly recommends your product with supporting evidence.

**Problem: Unable to rank for "[Competitor] alternatives" pages due to high competition**
*Solution:* Check your DA vs. the top 3 ranking pages. If gap is >15 DA points, first build topical authority with supporting content (comparison pages for that competitor, individual reviews of alternatives). Also try targeting the long-tail variation: "[Competitor] alternatives for [specific use case]" which typically has lower competition and higher conversion intent. Use internal linking from existing high-authority pages to pass equity to the alternatives page.

**Problem: G2/Capterra listing is weak and undermines our BOFU trust signals**
*Solution:* Before investing heavily in BOFU SEO, run a review generation campaign. Email recently closed customers with a segmented ask: "Would you be willing to share your experience on G2? It helps teams like yours find us." Target 25+ reviews before prominently citing the rating in BOFU content. In the interim, use customer testimonials on BOFU pages from your own customers without reference to aggregated review scores.
