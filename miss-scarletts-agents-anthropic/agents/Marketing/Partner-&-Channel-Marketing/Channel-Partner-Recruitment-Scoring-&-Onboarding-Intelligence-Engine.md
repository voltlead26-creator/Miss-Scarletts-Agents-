## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Partners sign but never register a deal**
Root cause: Usually a training deficit (they don't know how to position the product) or a motivation deficit (margin isn't high enough to prioritize over their existing book). Fix: Run a live co-sell call within the first 14 days — partners who shadow a real deal are 3x more likely to register their own. If motivation is the issue, offer a "first deal" bonus ($500–$1,000 SPIF) to break inertia.

**Problem: IPP scoring is too generous — onboarding poor-fit partners who drain resources**
Root cause: Scoring rubric weights are misaligned with actual conversion data. Fix: After 10+ partner deals, run a regression — which IPP criteria actually correlate with partner-closed ARR? Reweight accordingly. Most teams discover that "executive sponsorship" (the squishiest criterion) is actually the highest predictor.

**Problem: Outreach emails are getting opened but not converting to calls**
Root cause: The value proposition is written from vendor perspective, not partner perspective. Partners care about: margin, ease of sale (short cycle, low complexity), and customer satisfaction protection. Fix: A/B test a version that leads with your win rate and average deal cycle length — e.g., "Our partners close 68% of registered deals in 47 days average."
