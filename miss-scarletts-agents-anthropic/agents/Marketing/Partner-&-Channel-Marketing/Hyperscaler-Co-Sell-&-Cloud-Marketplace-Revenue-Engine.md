## Troubleshooting

**Issue: AWS/Azure field reps aren't responding to co-sell invitations despite deal registrations.**
Fix: Cloud field reps receive dozens of ISV partner requests per week. What cuts through: (1) lead with the customer name and a specific use case relevant to the rep's territory — not a generic pitch; (2) attach a one-page "why this deal, why now, why us" note written specifically for that rep; (3) confirm the deal is over $25K ACV and the buyer has an active EDP/MACC — these are the deals reps care about. If still no response, escalate to your Partner Development Manager (PDM) at the hyperscaler — they exist specifically to facilitate these introductions.

**Issue: Marketplace listing generates traffic but zero conversions (no free trials, no contact form fills).**
Fix: Marketplace listings fail for three reasons: (1) hero messaging is feature-centric instead of outcome-centric — rewrite using Jobs-to-be-Done language ("I need to stop waking up at 3am because a data pipeline silently failed"); (2) no social proof — you need at minimum 5 verified reviews with specific metrics; (3) the free trial/demo CTA requires too much friction — create a one-click "deploy to AWS" button using CloudFormation or a Marketplace Sandbox environment, or offer a 14-day no-credit-card trial accessible directly from the listing. Run A/B tests on pricing visibility (show price vs. "Contact for pricing") — for mid-market ICP, showing price typically increases conversion by 20-40%.

**Issue: Finance team won't approve marketplace purchase even though buyer has EDP/MACC committed spend.**
Fix: This happens when procurement doesn't understand that marketplace spend counts toward their cloud commitment. Create a one-page "Marketplace Purchase vs. Direct Purchase — CFO Decision Brief" that shows: (1) EDP/MACC drawdown mechanics with a simple example; (2) net cost difference (marketplace = same or lower price + reduces AWS overage risk); (3) procurement process difference (marketplace PO vs. new vendor onboarding — marketplace is often 10x faster). Have your champion send this to their FinOps/cloud cost management team, not just procurement. In most cases, FinOps teams actively want to buy via marketplace to avoid EDP penalties.
