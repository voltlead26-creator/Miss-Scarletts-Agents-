# VAR/Reseller Channel Demand Generation & Partner-Sourced Pipeline Engine - Turn Your Channel Into a Self-Sustaining Revenue Machine

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Time:** 25 min | **Tags:** channel-marketing, partner-marketing, var, reseller, b2b, demand-generation, partner-sourced-pipeline, enterprise, channel-enablement

## Overview
Designs, launches, and optimizes a full-funnel demand generation system for VAR/reseller channel partners — enabling partners to generate, nurture, and close their own pipeline using AI-orchestrated co-branded campaigns, MDF programs, and partner-specific playbooks. Use it when you have an established reseller network generating <30% of its potential pipeline, when partner marketing is a spreadsheet exercise, or when you're launching a new channel program.

## Integration Tips

- **PRM (Allbound / Impartner / Zift / PartnerStack)**: Store all co-branded campaign kits in the partner portal as "Campaign Launchers" — a partner clicks, fills in their company name and logo, and downloads a fully co-branded campaign kit in <3 minutes. Gate MDF claim forms behind deal registration to enforce attribution discipline.
- **Salesforce / HubSpot**: Create a "Partner Ecosystem" pipeline stage and a custom "Partner Activation Score" field that auto-updates based on deal registrations, certifications, and portal logins. Build a Salesforce report for weekly CAM review: "Partners with 0 deals registered in 60 days" = reactivation list.
- **Crossbeam / Reveal (Account Overlap)**: Run account mapping with each Platinum partner quarterly — identify accounts in the partner's customer base that match your ICP but aren't in your CRM. This becomes the target list for Play 1. Removes cold outreach friction entirely.
- **Bombora / 6sense (Intent Data)**: License intent data at the vendor level and pipe relevant intent signals into the partner's CRM via API (or CSV weekly). Partners see "these 12 accounts in your territory are researching [compliance automation] this week" — removes targeting guesswork.
- **Gong / Chorus**: Extract co-sell call snippets where partner reps asked the best discovery questions or handled objections well. Package these as "Partner Win Moments" in the monthly Partner Revenue Review — peer-learning is 3x more effective than vendor training.
- **Marketo / HubSpot (MAP)**: Build co-branded email templates that auto-populate with partner's logo, rep name, and signature when pulled from the PRM. Partners import directly into their own email tool — no HTML editing required.
- **Slack / Teams**: Create a private Slack channel for Platinum partners only — share deal wins, competitive intel, and MDF pre-approvals in real time. Reduces CAM email volume by 40% and creates a community effect that increases partner engagement.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: "Partners say they'll run campaigns but never do — MDF goes unclaimed, playbooks go unread."**
Solution: The friction is too high. Audit the time required to actually execute Play 1 from zero. If it takes more than 3 hours from "download assets" to "send first email," simplify it. Create a 90-second video walkthrough for each play. Assign one CAM to co-pilot the first campaign execution for every Platinum partner — they run it together live on a screen share. Once partners see their first reply, adoption skyrockets. Also: introduce a "Partner Demand Gen Sprint" — a 2-week structured challenge where you run the campaign alongside 5–10 partners simultaneously, share results in a shared channel, and award a small prize to the top performer.

**Problem: "Partners are registering deals we already have in pipeline — causing channel conflict and sales team frustration."**
Solution: This is a CRM hygiene and rules-of-engagement problem, not a partner problem. Implement a strict deal registration timestamp rule: partner who registers first owns sourcing credit regardless of what stage the deal is in internally. Add a "Known Accounts" list that syncs to Crossbeam weekly — partners can check before registering whether the account is already in your CRM. Publish the conflict resolution policy in the Partner Agreement and PRM. Run a 30-minute training with both your direct sales team and top 10 partners on the rules — get sign-off from your VP of Sales and VP of Partnerships together.

**Problem: "MDF is being spent on activities that generate leads but never convert to registered deals."**
Solution: Add a mandatory "deal registration gate" to MDF reimbursement: partners must show at least one deal registration from the funded activity to receive reimbursement. This single policy change typically reduces MDF waste by 40–60%. Also implement a pre-approval review: before approving MDF, your Channel Marketing team scores the proposed activity on a 1–5 pipeline likelihood scale based on historical data. Activities scoring <3 get coaching before approval, not automatic denial — you want to help partners improve, not gatekeep.
