## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Partner recruitment emails get no response despite multiple follow-ups.**
Solution: The pitch is likely product-centric rather than partner-business-centric. Rewrite the opening paragraph to reference a specific achievement of the partner's company (a case study, a recent hire, a market they serve) before mentioning your product. Lead with THEIR business outcome, not yours.

**Problem: Partners sign up but don't activate — no deals registered within 60 days.**
Solution: The Day 7 email is the critical activation trigger. Add a specific joint account target ("I pulled 3 accounts from your client base that match our ICP — want to co-prospect these this week?"). Activation rate typically doubles when the first co-sell opportunity is named and specific, not generic.

**Problem: Affiliate program generating high volume but low-quality referrals with high churn from referred customers.**
Solution: Add a qualification checkpoint in the FAQ and application form — ask affiliates to describe their audience in 2 sentences. Filter out applicants whose audience doesn't match your ICP. High-volume, low-quality referrals usually indicate the commission structure is attracting incentive-motivated promoters rather than genuine advocates. Consider adding a 90-day customer retention requirement before commission payout vests.
