## Overview

This prompt runs a systematic competitor SEO intelligence and organic market share displacement program — mapping your competitors' full organic footprint, identifying their traffic-generating pages, pinpointing where their rankings are vulnerable, and producing a prioritized displacement campaign with content briefs and link acquisition targets. Use it when entering a competitive market, recovering from a traffic drop, or when you know competitors are capturing high-intent buyers before they reach you.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The AI identifies displacement opportunities but the content brief doesn't clearly differentiate from the competitor page — the recommendation is essentially "write a longer version of the same article."**
Solution: Push harder on the "Unique Angle" section by providing proprietary inputs the AI can work with: customer case study data points, benchmark statistics from your own product data, unpublished research findings, or expert quotes from your team. The displacement brief should always include at least one data point or proof element the competitor cannot replicate — this is what makes the content structurally superior rather than just longer.

**Problem: After publishing displacement content, pages index but do not move above position 30 after 8 weeks, suggesting the domain authority gap is too large to overcome with content alone.**
Solution: The link acquisition phase is non-optional for displacing competitors with DR 20+ points above your domain. Re-run Phase 5 with a target of 10–15 new referring domains specifically to the new page within 60 days of publication. Focus on resource replacement outreach (contact domains linking to the competitor's outdated page and offer your updated version) — this is 3x more effective than cold outreach and specifically targets the exact link equity you need to displace.

**Problem: The competitor has published a response to your displacement content — they've updated their ranking page after seeing traffic movement indicators.**
Solution: This confirms your content is working (competitors only update when they feel the threat). Run the SERP Feature Displacement section for that specific cluster: even if they refresh their article, you can still own featured snippets, PAA boxes, and AI Overview citations through superior structured content. Simultaneously, accelerate link outreach for that page — link velocity in the 30-day window after a competitor update is often decisive for who ultimately holds the top position.
