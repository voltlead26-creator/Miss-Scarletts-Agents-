- Use Contact Properties to pull [[USAGE_GROWTH_PCT]], [[ACTIVE_SEATS]], and [[HEALTH_SCORE]] from your product analytics integration (Segment, Amplitude, or Mixpanel via HubSpot custom properties)

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Champion is no longer in their role (champion churn) and renewal campaign goes cold**
Fix: Immediately pause the standard sequence. Route a brief, non-vendor-sounding introduction email from your VP of Customer Success to the new stakeholder — not a pitch, just a relationship opener with a clear "here's what your predecessor achieved" value summary. Treat this as a Day 0 restart: rebuild the relationship before advancing the commercial conversation. Extend the renewal runway by 30 days in your CRM to reflect the new stakeholder ramp time.

**Issue: Low email open rates on Emails 5-7 (under 35%) as renewal approaches**
Fix: Switch the primary communication channel for those emails. If email is not working at 30 days out, the AE should send a LinkedIn message instead of Email 5 — personal, short, and referencing a specific value data point. Consider a brief phone call attempt for Email 7. Some customers disengage from email near renewal because they associate vendor emails with contract pressure; a channel switch signals that this is a genuine conversation, not automated follow-up.

**Issue: Expansion email (Email 4) gets a "not the right time" response but renewal proceeds**
Fix: Accept the "not now" gracefully — reply with one sentence acknowledging it and confirming the renewal is the priority. Set a CRM task for 90 days post-renewal to restart the expansion conversation with fresh usage data. Never let expansion friction bleed into renewal risk; separate the motions clearly in your CRM pipeline and ensure the CSM and AE are aligned that renewal comes first.
