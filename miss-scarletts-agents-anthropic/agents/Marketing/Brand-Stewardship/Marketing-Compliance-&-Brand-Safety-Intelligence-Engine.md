# Marketing Compliance & Brand Safety Intelligence Engine - Automate Legal Review, FTC Disclosure, and Regulatory Risk Management for Every Marketing Asset

**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Time:** 20-30 min | **Tags:** compliance, brand-safety, legal, risk-management, b2b, b2c, enterprise, automation, ftc, gdpr, ai-governance

## Overview
Systematically reviews marketing assets — ads, emails, landing pages, social posts, influencer briefs, AI-generated content — against FTC guidelines, GDPR/CCPA requirements, industry-specific regulations, and your own claims substantiation standards. Use this when launching campaigns in regulated industries (healthcare, fintech, HR tech, legal), when scaling influencer or AI-generated content programs, or when a single compliance failure could trigger an FTC investigation, class action, or brand crisis.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI flags claims as violations but legal says they're fine**
Solution: This usually indicates the AI is applying consumer-facing FTC standards to B2B content, or applying US regulations to non-US campaigns. In your prompt, explicitly specify: (1) B2B vs. B2C audience, (2) geographic market, (3) whether your company has substantiation studies on file. Add: "Note: Our legal team has pre-approved claims supported by [study name/date]. Do not flag these." Build an approved claims whitelist into your prompt over time.

**Problem: Output is too long / too many findings for the team to action**
Solution: Add to the end of your prompt: "Prioritize your output. Show ONLY findings rated RED or CRITICAL. Limit output to maximum 10 findings. For each finding, provide only: (1) exact violating text, (2) regulation, (3) drop-in replacement text. Skip all findings rated YELLOW or GREEN." This creates a much faster action-oriented review for experienced marketing teams.

**Problem: Compliance review is slowing down campaign velocity**
Solution: Build a two-tier review system. Tier 1 (AI only, same-day): all standard campaigns using approved messaging from your brand guidelines. Tier 2 (AI + legal, 48-hour): any campaign with new performance claims, new industry/geography, influencer/creator content, or AI-generated content. Use this prompt to identify which tier each campaign belongs to — include the question: "Based on this asset, does it require Tier 1 (AI review only) or Tier 2 (legal escalation)? Explain why in one sentence."
