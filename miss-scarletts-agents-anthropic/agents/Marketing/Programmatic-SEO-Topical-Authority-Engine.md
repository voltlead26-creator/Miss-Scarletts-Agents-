## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Programmatic pages get flagged as thin content and deindexed.**
Solution: Each programmatic page must contain at minimum 600+ words, a unique data point or example per page, and a distinct H1 that differs from other pages in the set. Audit the pages Google deindexed using Search Console Coverage report — add a unique "local context" section (e.g., regional data, platform-specific screenshots, user testimonials) to each page. Consider noindexing the lowest-value pages until they're enriched.

**Problem: Pillar pages get traffic but don't convert to leads.**
Solution: The pillar page CTA is likely misaligned with search intent — informational searchers won't request a demo. Replace the hard CTA with a middle-of-funnel offer (free tool, ROI calculator, benchmark report, interactive checklist). Add an inline lead capture widget (not just a footer CTA). Test a sticky sidebar CTA vs. inline. Use Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity to see where users drop off.

**Problem: AI answer engines (Perplexity, ChatGPT) cite competitors but not my content.**
Solution: Check if your pages have FAQ schema markup — add it immediately. Ensure each answer to a key AI query is contained in a single, self-contained paragraph (2-4 sentences) near the top of the relevant section. Add structured definitions: "A customer data platform (CDP) is defined as..." — AI engines heavily cite definitional content. Build backlinks from authoritative domains (DA 60+) to the specific pages you want cited; backlink authority is a citation signal.
