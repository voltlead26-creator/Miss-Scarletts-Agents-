## Overview
Designs and automates a full account-based video marketing program — from AI-generated personalized outreach videos for named accounts to scalable 1:Few and 1:Many video campaigns — and closes the attribution loop by converting video engagement signals into pipeline triggers. Use this when you need to break through inbox noise with target accounts, accelerate stalled deals, or systematically convert video watch data into sales-qualified opportunities.

- Use Vidyard's "Video Messages" feature for rep-recorded Tier 1 videos — it generates a animated GIF preview that embeds in email and gets 3x more clicks than static thumbnails

**HeyGen / Synthesia (AI video generation):**
- Create a "brand avatar" that matches your company's visual identity — HeyGen allows custom avatar creation from a 5-minute video recording of a real team member

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Reps say creating personalized videos takes too long and aren't doing it consistently**
Solution: The bottleneck is almost always the scripting step, not the recording. Build a personalization brief template (see Deliverable 2) that auto-populates from CRM trigger data — when reps receive a brief with their first 10 seconds pre-written and the proof point pre-selected, recording time drops to under 5 minutes. Set a team standard: Tier 1 videos are ≤90 seconds, recorded in one take, no editing. Imperfect and human is better than polished and delayed. Pair each brief with a "script card" Slack message sent directly to the rep's phone — they can record from mobile on the way to their next meeting.

**Problem: Video watch rates are high but meeting conversion is low — prospects watch but don't book**
Solution: The problem is almost always the CTA, not the video. Audit all CTAs in active videos — if they say "let me know if you'd like to chat" or "feel free to book time," replace them with a specific, low-friction, high-value offer. "15-minute pipeline audit" outperforms "demo request" 4:1 in video context because it sounds like value delivery, not a sales call. Also verify your calendar link loads immediately from the video end screen — every extra click loses 30-40% of motivated viewers.

**Problem: AI-generated Tier 2/3 videos feel generic and prospects can tell**
Solution: The tell is almost always the intro — AI avatars default to formal, polished openers that sound nothing like how a human rep actually talks. Rewrite the first 15 seconds of every AI script to match the register your reps actually use (listen to 5 Gong calls and note the exact words they use when opening a cold call). Also ensure the script references at least one industry-specific term that shows you understand their world — "NRR" for CS leaders, "burn multiple" for CFOs, "sprint velocity" for engineering leaders. One correct industry term does more for perceived personalization than 10 generic compliments.
