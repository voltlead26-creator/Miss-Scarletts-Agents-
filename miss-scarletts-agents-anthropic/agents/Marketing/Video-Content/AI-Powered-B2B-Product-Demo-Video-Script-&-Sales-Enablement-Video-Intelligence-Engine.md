## Overview
Designs persona-specific, AI-scripted product demo videos and sales enablement video assets that compress the B2B sales cycle — turning a 45-minute live demo call into a library of self-serve, fully automated video assets that move buyers through the funnel 24/7 without a sales rep in the room.

SCENE 2 — THE VILLAIN (1:30-2:30):
Name the root cause. Not the prospect's fault — it's the system's fault.
Example: "The problem isn't your sales team. It's that your CRM shows you deal status — not deal health. It shows you what reps reported, not what's actually happening in the conversations."

c) Show the insight your product surfaces that the buyer wouldn't have otherwise

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Prospects watch the demo but don't book a follow-up call**
Solution: The CTA is too generic or too high-friction. Audit Scene 6 of every demo — replace "contact us" or "request a demo" with a specific, low-stakes offer ("Book a 20-minute data session," "Get a personalized benchmark report," "See your own data in 25 minutes"). The CTA must answer "what specifically happens next and how long will it take?" Also check if the Vidyard/Wistia CTA button is appearing at the right time — it should appear at the 80% mark, not at the end, when viewer attention is highest.

**Problem: Demo completion rates are below 40%**
Solution: The problem is almost always in Scene 1 or Scene 2 — the demo opens on product features instead of buyer pain. Rewrite the first 90 seconds to open on the buyer's world, not the product's world. The product should not be mentioned or shown until at least the 2-minute mark. Additionally, check demo length — Tier 1 demos over 4 minutes see sharp drop-off. Test a 2-minute cut of the same content and compare completion rates.

**Problem: AEs aren't using the demo video library — still defaulting to live demos for everything**
Solution: This is a change management problem, not a content problem. The fix is two-part: (1) Show AEs the data — pull a report showing pipeline velocity for deals where self-serve video was used in discovery vs. deals where it wasn't. Make the ROI undeniable. (2) Make it frictionless — create a one-click "Send Demo" button directly in Salesforce or HubSpot using the Vidyard Salesloft/Outreach integration so AEs can send a personalized demo link in 15 seconds without leaving their workflow.
