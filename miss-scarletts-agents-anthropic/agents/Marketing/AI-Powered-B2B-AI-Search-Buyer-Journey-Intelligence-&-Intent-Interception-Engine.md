## Overview
This prompt deploys an AI agent to reconstruct the full B2B buying journey as it unfolds inside generative AI systems — mapping the exact queries buyers ask at every funnel stage, identifying where competitors intercept them, and producing a prioritized content plan to capture in-market buyers before they reach a competitor-influenced decision. ## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The 60-query universe feels too large to audit meaningfully — the team doesn't have bandwidth to run this rigorously.
**Solution:** Compress to a 20-query "priority tier" using a simple scoring rule: Stage 4 queries (vendor evaluation) × HIGH commercial value score first, since competitive interception at this stage is the most expensive to lose. Run the full 60-query universe once per quarter as a strategic reset; run only the 20-query priority tier weekly. Automate as much as possible using the OpenAI/Anthropic API to query multiple models and capture responses in a Google Sheet.

**Problem:** AI model responses at Stage 1-2 (problem awareness) seem irrelevant to vendor competition — no brands appear, so competitive interception analysis isn't useful here.
**Solution:** Stage 1-2 is not about competitive interception — it's about category creation. Your goal at these stages is to appear as the authoritative source explaining the problem, which builds the neural association between your brand and the problem space before the buyer even knows what solutions exist. Track "brand + category association" rather than competitive position: is [Your Company] appearing as the educational authority on the problem, or is a competitor positioning themselves as the educator?

**Problem:** Content interventions aren't earning AI citations even after 60-90 days — published the comparison pages and G2 campaign but seeing no movement.
**Solution:** Most common cause is insufficient authority signal on the published content. AI models heavily weight content from high-domain-authority sources. If your comparison page lives on your own domain with a DA of 35 vs. a G2 comparison page with DA 92, the G2 page will win. Distribute your core comparison content to third-party sites (guest posts on industry publications, contributed articles on high-DA review platforms, seeding content into Reddit or industry Slack communities where AI models scrape discussions). The content needs to exist on sites AI models weight as authoritative, not just on your own domain.
