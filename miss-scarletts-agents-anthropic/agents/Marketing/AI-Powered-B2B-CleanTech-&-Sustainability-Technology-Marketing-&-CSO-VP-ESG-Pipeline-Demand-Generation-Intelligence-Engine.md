## Troubleshooting

**Problem: CSO-tier prospects engage with content but won't take demo meetings**
Solution: CSOs are skeptical of vendor-led demos after being burned by greenwashing vendors. Replace demo CTA with "ESG Audit Readiness Assessment" (AI-powered, 15 minutes, delivers personalized gap analysis). Convert assessment completions to demo using the assessment output as the demo agenda — CSO is now evaluating their own gap, not your product. Conversion from assessment to demo: 45–60%.

**Problem: Deals get stuck at CFO approval — CSO is champion but can't get budget released**
Solution: Activate CFO directly with "Compliance Cost Avoidance ROI Calculator" — input: current audit preparation hours + external consultant cost + regulatory fine exposure + ESG rating discount on cost of capital. Output: Net present value of platform investment vs. status quo. Present CFO with peer company benchmark (e.g., "Companies your size spend $280K–$450K/year on ESG audit preparation — platform ROI in Year 1"). Provide champion with CFO presentation template pre-built with their company's numbers.

**Problem: Sales cycle drags beyond 180 days — no urgency to close**
Solution: Tie deal to specific regulatory deadline. Identify the earliest applicable regulatory trigger for each prospect (SEC FY2026 filing = 18-month countdown, CSRD FY2027 = 24-month, SBTi near-term 2030 = Scope 3 baseline needed by Q1 to run full-year 2025 data). Frame close timeline as: "To have audit-ready 2025 baseline data for your [SEC/CSRD/SBTi] submission, implementation must begin by [specific date]. Implementation takes 8–12 weeks." Creates a natural, credible deadline without artificial urgency.
