## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The narrative pivot sounds defensive, not confident**
Solution: Apply the Judo Strategy filter — every message should make the disruptor's existence *strengthen* your position, not just acknowledge it. If your messaging says "we're still relevant because..." it's defensive. Reframe as "the disruption proves that [CATEGORY] is now mission-critical, which is exactly why companies need [YOUR DIFFERENTIATED CAPABILITY] not a commodity alternative."

**Problem: The 90-day roadmap is too ambitious for the team's current capacity**
Solution: Apply the Pareto filter — identify the 20% of actions that will protect 80% of revenue. Typically this is: (1) update sales objection handling scripts, (2) activate top 20 customer advocates, and (3) launch one well-funded thought leadership campaign. Cut everything else until the team is executing these three at high quality.

**Problem: The output treats all customers the same, not segmented by disruption risk**
Solution: Provide explicit segment data in your input prompt, including specific % of base in each risk tier. If you don't know this, run a win/loss analysis of the last 20 churned customers before using this prompt — the segmentation will be dramatically more accurate with real churn reason data.
