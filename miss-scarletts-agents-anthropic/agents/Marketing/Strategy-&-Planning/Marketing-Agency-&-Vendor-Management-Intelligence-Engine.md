## Integration Tips

**Notion:** Create an Agency Management database with properties for Agency Name, Type, Monthly Retainer, Contract End Date, QBR Score, and Status. Link to this prompt for each QBR cycle. Use the evaluation scorecard as a Notion table populated after each agency pitch.

**HubSpot:** Track agency-attributed pipeline by creating a custom deal property "Agency Source" and UTM taxonomy standard (`utm_source=agency-name&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=`). Pull this into the QBR performance dashboard automatically.

**Google Sheets:** Build the evaluation scorecard as a shared Sheet with drop-down scoring (1-5) per criterion, auto-calculated weighted totals, and conditional formatting (red/yellow/green) for instant visual comparison across agencies.

**Salesforce:** Create an Agency object in Salesforce (or use Accounts with Type = Vendor) to track spend, pipeline influenced, and contract terms. Build a report showing agency-attributed MQL→SQL→Closed conversion rates quarterly.

**Zapier / Make:** Automate the QBR data pull — connect Google Ads, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, and HubSpot to a monthly Sheets report. Trigger a Slack alert to the CMO if any agency KPI drops >20% week-over-week.

**DocuSign / PandaDoc:** Store finalized SOW red flag checklist as a contract review template. Run all new agency contracts through this checklist before signature.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Agencies submit proposals that don't address the business outcomes in the brief — they focus on deliverables (number of ads, blog posts) rather than pipeline impact.
**Solution:** Add a mandatory section to your RFP: "Provide 3 specific examples of how you would measure success for this engagement in terms of pipeline generated, not outputs delivered. Include the attribution methodology you would use." Grade any proposal that doesn't answer this below 50 on measurement rigor.

**Problem:** Agency performance looks fine on agency-reported metrics but internal pipeline data tells a different story.
**Solution:** Require direct platform access (never accept agency-only logins) and build your own reporting in HubSpot/Salesforce. Add a contract clause: "Agency reports are advisory only. Client-side attribution system is the source of truth for performance bonus calculations."

**Problem:** Agency resists 30-day termination clause, pushing for 90-day notice.
**Solution:** Counter with a 60-day "knowledge transfer period" framing rather than notice period — agencies accept this more readily. Alternatively, offer a 45-day notice with a 2% monthly performance bonus if they hit targets for 6+ consecutive months. This aligns incentives while protecting your exit rights.
