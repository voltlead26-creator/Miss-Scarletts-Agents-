## Troubleshooting

**Issue: LinkedIn restricting your account for automation**
LinkedIn's algorithm flags accounts sending >20 connection requests/day or >100 messages/day. Use a warm-up protocol: start at 5-10 requests/day, increase 10% per week. Use human-like delays (not instant sends). Prefer native LinkedIn tools or approved partners (LinkedIn Sales Navigator + Salesloft/Outreach integrations) over third-party scrapers that violate ToS.

**Issue: Connection accept rate below 25%**
Audit your profile first — a weak headline or no profile photo kills accept rates before the message is even read. Test message variants: try a version with NO ask (just a genuine observation) vs. a version with a soft CTA. Acceptance rate is 40% about the message, 60% about perceived profile credibility. Make sure you have 500+ connections, a professional photo, and a headline that speaks to the buyer's world — not your product.

**Issue: High reply rate but no meetings booked**
Replies are engagement, not pipeline. If prospects reply but don't convert to meetings, your bridge from "conversation" to "next step" is too abrupt. Insert a middle step: send a relevant case study or benchmark data before asking for a meeting. Create a low-friction ask ("I can send you our 2-page benchmark — want me to drop it here?") before proposing a calendar link. Meeting booking asks should come in Message 3 at earliest for cold contacts.
