## Overview

Automates deep account and prospect research at scale, synthesizing LinkedIn activity, tech stack signals, recent news, job postings, funding events, and intent data into outbound-ready intelligence packages. Use this when you need to turn a raw target account list into personalized outbound campaigns without burning SDR hours on manual research.

**2. BUY-READINESS SCORE (1-10 with rationale)**

**3. TECHNOGRAPHIC INTELLIGENCE**

Be specific — not "they need better forecasting" but "their recent CRO hire in Q4 + 3 open 'Revenue Operations Analyst' postings suggests they're trying to fix a broken pipeline visibility problem ahead of a likely Series C raise."

**7. PERSONALIZATION HOOKS (3 options)**
Provide 3 distinct opening hooks for outbound, each using a different trigger:
- Hook A: Recent company news/event
- Hook B: Tech stack pain point or gap
- Hook C: Org/hiring signal insight

**8. OUTREACH SEQUENCING RECOMMENDATION**
Recommended first touch (email vs. LinkedIn vs. phone), ideal timing, and 2-sentence email subject line + opening based on the strongest signal.

Use only publicly available signals. Flag any assumptions clearly with [INFERRED].

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** AI generates generic, non-specific hooks that sound like every other outbound email.
**Solution:** Ensure you provide at least one concrete, dated signal (funding announcement, specific job posting title, executive LinkedIn post text). The more specific the input signal, the more specific the hook. If no recent signals exist, downgrade the account to Tier 3 and pause outreach until a trigger event occurs.

**Problem:** Pain hypothesis doesn't match what prospects actually tell you in discovery.
**Solution:** After 10-15 discovery calls, audit your hypothesis accuracy by comparing predicted pain vs. disclosed pain. Update the SELLER CONTEXT section with your corrected pain hypothesis patterns per industry/persona segment. This feedback loop improves prompt output accuracy over time.

**Problem:** Researching 50+ accounts is too slow even with batch mode.
**Solution:** Use batch mode for Tier 2-3 accounts (condensed brief). Reserve the full 7-section package for Tier 1 accounts only. Alternatively, build a Clay workflow that runs AI enrichment columns in parallel across your account list — this prompt maps to a "AI Research" column prompt in Clay that can process 200+ accounts/hour.
