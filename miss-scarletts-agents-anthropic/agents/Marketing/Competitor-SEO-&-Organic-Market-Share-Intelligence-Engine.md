## Overview
Conducts a structured competitor SEO analysis to identify keyword gaps, SERP ownership opportunities, content cannibalizations, and backlink strategy insights that shift organic market share toward your domain. Use this when competitors consistently outrank you on high-intent commercial keywords, when you need data-driven justification for SEO investment, or when building a systematic organic acquisition program that reduces paid media dependency.

PHASE 2 — GAP ANALYSIS & OPPORTUNITY SCORING:

Keyword Gap Matrix:
For each identified keyword gap, score using this formula:
Opportunity Score = (Monthly Search Volume × Commercial Intent Score × Rank Probability) ÷ Keyword Difficulty

Where:
- Commercial Intent Score: Transactional = 1.0, Commercial Investigation = 0.8, Informational = 0.4, Navigational = 0.2
- Rank Probability: Based on our DR vs. competitor DR and current ranking (if any)
- Prioritize keywords where Opportunity Score > 50 and we have zero current presence

Segment gaps into four quadrants:
1. QUICK WINS: Low difficulty (KD <30), medium-high volume, we have existing content to optimize
2. STRATEGIC ATTACKS: High volume, high intent, high difficulty — requires new pillar content + link building
3. LONG-TAIL HARVESTS: Low volume individually but clusters of 50+ related queries to capture systematically
4. COMPETITIVE DISPLACEMENT: Keywords where competitor holds positions 1-3 but has weaker content quality than we can produce

## Integration Tips

- Export Keyword Gap report as CSV; paste top 200 rows into the Advanced prompt as context
- Use Semrush's "Organic Research → Position Changes" to track competitor ranking velocity in real-time
- Set up Semrush position tracking project for your 150 target keywords and share the PDF report weekly with the advanced prompt for AI-generated interpretation and action recommendations

**Google Search Console:**
- Export "Search Results → Queries" filtered by position > 5 and impressions > 100 — these are your "almost ranking" pages where quick optimization can move the needle within 30 days
- Feed this export directly into the Quick Copy-Paste prompt as your "current rankings context"

**HubSpot / Salesforce:**
- Create a custom UTM parameter (`utm_medium=organic&utm_source=google`) and a "First Page Viewed" property to track which organic landing pages drive the most pipeline
- Build a report: "Closed-Won by Original Source = Organic Search" broken down by landing page URL — this is your ROI proof for SEO investment

**Notion / Google Sheets:**
- Use the Competitor Keyword Gap Matrix output as a living document — paste into a Google Sheet with columns for status (Not Started / In Progress / Published / Ranking)
- Build a Notion content database with the 90-day content calendar, link targets, and owner assignments auto-populated from prompt output

**Zapier / Make:**

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI output is too generic — competitor analysis doesn't reflect actual market dynamics**
Solution: Provide more specific context: paste 5-10 actual URLs from each competitor's top-performing content, include your current GSC top-20 keywords with impressions and positions, and name 3 specific head-to-head keywords where you're losing. The more real data you give the model, the more precise the gap analysis.

**Problem: The 90-day traffic projections seem unrealistically optimistic**
Solution: Recalibrate using this heuristic: for a DR 30-50 domain, expect 60-90 days to see new content rank, and new rankings typically capture 20-30% of the estimated keyword search volume (not 100%). Adjust projections in the prompt with `assume CTR of 20% for position 3, 10% for position 5, 3% for position 10` to get conservative-but-credible forecasts for CFO conversations.

**Problem: Can't access paid SEO tools (Semrush/Ahrefs) to verify data**
Solution: Use free alternatives as input: (1) Google Search Console for your own domain data, (2) Moz Free Link Explorer for competitor DR estimates, (3) Google "site:competitor.com" searches to estimate indexed pages, (4) manually Google your 20 target keywords in incognito mode and record positions. Tell the AI in the prompt: "I don't have paid SEO tools — generate the analysis framework and content strategy based on these manually gathered data points" and provide what you do have.
