## Troubleshooting

- **"The output is too generic — it could apply to any company in our space."**
  Fix: The Advanced Version requires real discovery data. If you're getting generic output, your PAIN_POINT inputs are vague. Go back to your discovery notes and pull actual numbers, quotes, and named initiatives. The prompt performs proportionally to the specificity of the input.

- **"The ROI numbers don't feel credible to the prospect."**
  Fix: Show your math in the demo, not just the conclusion. Use the prospect's own numbers back to them — say "you mentioned 3.2% error rate — at 800 shipments per day that's 25.6 errors daily, at $340 average re-pick cost that's $8,704 per day or $3.2M annually." Ground every figure in something they said.

- **"The champion isn't using the internal deck we gave them."**
  Fix: Champions don't sell what they don't understand. Schedule a 15-minute "champion coaching call" 2 days after the demo — walk them through the deck slide by slide, roleplay the CFO conversation, and identify blockers. Use the FAQ cheat sheet from Deliverable 5C as the agenda.
