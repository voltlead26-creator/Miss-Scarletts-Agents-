## Integration Tips

**Salesforce:**
Create a custom "Content Used" multi-select field on Opportunity records. Have reps tag which content types were shared at each stage. Build a report correlating Content_Used with Opportunity.IsClosed = True and Opportunity.IsWon = True. Run quarterly regression analysis to identify content-win correlations.

**HubSpot:**
Use the Documents tool to track buyer engagement with shared assets. Set up a Workflow that automatically logs document engagement as Deal activity. Build a custom report comparing deal close rate for deals with high document engagement vs. low.

**Highspot/Seismic:**
Configure Content Scorecards to track: Content views per deal, share frequency per rep, buyer engagement time, and progression correlation. Set up monthly Governance Reports to flag assets with declining usage or low engagement scores.

**Gong/Chorus:**
Create a tracker for content mentions in sales calls ("I'll send you our ROI calculator" / "Did you have a chance to review the case study?"). Use AI summaries to identify when content conversations led to positive vs. negative buyer reactions. Feed insights back into content refresh priorities.

**Notion/Confluence:**
Build a Content Library database with all assets, last-reviewed dates, performance scores, and owner tags. Set up a quarterly review workflow that automatically pings asset owners when content is due for refresh based on age or product change triggers.

**Zapier:**
Automate: When Opportunity Stage = "Procurement/Legal" in Salesforce AND Content_Used doesn't include "Security Package" → Slack alert to AE + auto-attach security package to deal. Remove the manual step of reps remembering to share the right content at the right stage.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: "I don't have win/loss data to feed into this prompt."**
Solution: Start with qualitative signals. Ask 5 reps to complete a 5-minute survey: "Name the last 3 deals you won and what content you shared. Name the last 3 deals you lost and what content you wish you'd had." Combine with a quick Gong search for "content" mentions in deal calls. You'll have directional signal within 2 hours. Perfect data isn't required — directional patterns are enough to prioritize.

**Problem: "The output is too long and my leadership wants a one-pager."**
Solution: Add this to the end of your prompt: "After the full report, produce a one-page Executive Brief with: (1) Content Library Health Grade with one sentence rationale, (2) Top 3 revenue-blocking content gaps with dollar impact estimates, (3) Recommended Q1 investment (time + budget), and (4) Success metrics we'll review in 90 days."

**Problem: "Reps don't use the content even after we build it."**
Solution: Adoption is a change management problem, not a content problem. Add this module to the prompt: "Produce a Sales Content Adoption Plan for the top 5 assets we're building — including: launch email template, SKO/QBR talking points, Slack announcement copy, rep training script (2-minute enablement session format), and a 30-day adoption tracking checklist."
