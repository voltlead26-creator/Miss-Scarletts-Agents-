## Overview
This prompt engineers a complete AI-powered Digital Sales Room (DSR) for a specific account — generating a personalized buyer portal structure, curated content stack, mutual action plan, stakeholder-specific messaging, and deal-closing sequence. Use it when an opportunity moves to Stage 2+ to systematically reduce sales cycles and increase multi-threaded deal engagement.

Make every element hyper-specific to the company, buying committee, and active competitors. The DSR must feel like it was hand-built for this specific company, not templated.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Buyers open the DSR once but don't return.**
Fix: The content isn't personalized enough — buyers can tell when they're looking at a lightly customized template. Audit the DSR: does the hero message reference something specific from your last conversation? Does the ROI model use their actual numbers? Does the customer story feature a company in their exact vertical? Generic DSRs get ghosted. Also check: is the DSR too long? Aim for 6–8 sections max, each scannable in 90 seconds.

**Problem: Champion won't share the DSR with the rest of the buying committee.**
Fix: The champion doesn't feel confident enough to present internally — which means they haven't internalized the value yet OR they're protecting access while they build their internal case. Send the champion the "Internal Selling Toolkit" specifically: the 5-slide internal pitch deck, the CFO one-pager, and the Slack template. Make it effortless. Then ask directly: "What would make it easy for you to loop Sandra in this week?" Remove the blocker, don't just send another email.

**Problem: The CFO visited pricing 4x but no meeting has been scheduled.**
Fix: The CFO is self-educating — this is a high-intent signal. They're building a mental model without you in the room, which means they have a concern or comparison point they haven't voiced. Send a direct, short email: "I noticed you've been looking at the pricing section — I'd love 20 minutes to walk you through our ROI model for [Company] specifically and answer any questions. Here's my calendar: [link]." Don't wait for them to come to you.
