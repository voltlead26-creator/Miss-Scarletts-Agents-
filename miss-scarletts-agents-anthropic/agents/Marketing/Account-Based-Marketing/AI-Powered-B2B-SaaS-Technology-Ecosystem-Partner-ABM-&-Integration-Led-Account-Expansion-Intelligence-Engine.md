## Overview

Designs AI-powered ABM campaigns targeting accounts through technology integration signals, partner ecosystem data, and joint go-to-market programs. Use this when your SaaS product integrates with other platforms (Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Gong, etc.) and you want to systematically identify, score, and activate accounts that show integration-adoption signals or mutual partner relationships — the highest-conversion segment of any B2B SaaS TAL.

## Integration Tips

- **Crossbeam / Reveal**: Connect your CRM to partner CRMs for automated weekly account mapping. Configure alerts when a TAL account appears in a partner's customer list so your SDR can immediately request a co-sell introduction.
- **6sense / Bombora**: Layer technology intent signals as an overlay filter — target accounts showing intent on both your category AND your integration partners' categories simultaneously for the highest-propensity list.
- **Salesforce / HubSpot**: Build a custom "Ecosystem Fit Score" field that auto-populates via enrichment integration (BuiltWith → CRM). Create ABM campaign lists that auto-filter by score tier for each campaign track.
- **Outreach / Salesloft**: Build dedicated ecosystem ABM sequences with "integration signal" merge fields — your SDR outreach pulls the confirmed partner technology directly from the CRM field into the email body automatically.
- **Zapier / Make**: Automate the trigger from partner marketplace install to ABM campaign enrollment — when an account installs your app from HubSpot or Salesforce marketplace, auto-enroll in an onboarding-to-expansion sequence and alert the CSM.
- **Google Sheets + Crossbeam**: Build a shared partner co-sell pipeline tracker with partner team that syncs opportunity stage automatically — replace manual co-sell spreadsheets with a live view both teams can update.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Technology partner refuses to share account mapping data**
Fix: Start with Crossbeam's anonymous overlap feature — it shows you the COUNT of mutual accounts and mutual pipeline without exposing account names until both sides opt in. Show the partner a projected co-sell opportunity size ($X in mutual pipeline estimated from your Tier 1 account list) to create urgency for opting in. Offer to commit joint MDF for the first 90 days contingent on account mapping activation.

**Problem: Ecosystem Fit Scores don't predict pipeline conversion — high scores aren't converting**
Fix: Run a closed-won analysis on your last 20 new logos from partner-influenced deals. Which specific signals appeared in those accounts 60-90 days before opportunity creation? Remove signals with no predictive correlation and upweight the high-correlation signals (typically "uses Tier 1 partner technology" + "in active partner co-sell" predicts far better than engagement signals alone). Recalibrate scoring model quarterly.

**Problem: Partner AEs agree to co-sell but don't follow through with introductions**
Fix: Remove all friction from partner participation — create a 60-second referral form (Typeform or HubSpot) where partner AEs submit a referral with 3 fields and get a confirmation that your team will respond within 4 business hours. Add a direct financial incentive (spiff or gift card) visible in the form confirmation. Replace async communication with a shared Slack channel for each Tier 1 partner where you post co-sell opportunities in real-time and partner AEs can claim them instantly.
