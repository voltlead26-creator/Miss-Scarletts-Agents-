Generates a concise, actionable daily intelligence brief for each target account — surfacing fresh triggers, buying signals, and specific outreach angles that sales and marketing can act on immediately. Use every morning before reaching out to a tier-1 account or when an account surges in intent data.

## Integration Tips
- **6sense / Bombora / G2 Buyer Intent**: Export weekly intent surge reports for your target account list, paste into this prompt's intent data field. Run every Monday morning for Tier 1 accounts.
- **ZoomInfo / Apollo / Clay**: Use these to pull recent job posting data and technographic stack changes. Build a Clay table that auto-populates the intelligence fields and routes to this prompt via Zapier or n8n.
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**: Set up account alerts for each Tier 1 account. Every morning, check "Account News" and "Stakeholder Activity" in the Sales Nav feed and paste into the prompt.
- **Gong / Chorus**: After any call, extract the "customer mentioned" topics and use them to update the account intelligence context. Signals from past calls are often the strongest predictor of current priorities.
- **HubSpot / Salesforce**: Log the output of each daily brief as a note on the account record. Tag with the signal type so you can analyze which signal types convert to pipeline at highest rates.
- **Slack Automation**: Use Zapier to post the daily brief output to the #abm-intelligence Slack channel each morning, tagged to the AE or SDR who owns that account.

## Troubleshooting
- **No signals found for a target account**: Don't force outreach on silence. Instead, use this as a content-based nurture opportunity — share an insight or piece of content relevant to their industry without referencing their company specifically. Re-run the brief in 2 weeks. If still no signals after 30 days, deprioritize from Tier 1.
- **Too many conflicting signals (some positive, some negative)**: Rank signals by recency (last 7 days > last 30 days), then by directness to your ICP pain. A recent executive hire is a caution flag even if intent data is surging — hold 30 days and monitor before outreaching.
- **The personalized opener sounds too clever or try-hard**: Simplify. Remove the "gotcha" framing. Replace with a genuine question: "Are you tackling [X] yet or is that still on the roadmap?" Simple curiosity outperforms cleverness in B2B outreach.
