## Overview
Designs and executes end-to-end ABM campaigns that systematically target competitor customers — building account intelligence on competitor installs, crafting persona-specific displacement messaging, orchestrating multi-channel outreach across buying committees, and tracking switcher pipeline from first signal to closed-won. Use this when you need to grow pipeline by displacing competitors from their existing customer accounts rather than expanding the overall market.

- Track package delivery + meeting booked correlation — physical mail typically generates 4-8x higher response than email alone for cold-to-warm conversion

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Target accounts aren't responding to displacement outreach despite showing intent signals**
Solution: Check your empathy-to-product ratio. If your first 3 touches lead with product or features, you'll get ignored — the prospect doesn't trust you yet. Ensure the first 2 touches are 100% about their pain (competitor's known issues, renewal timing, peer validation) with zero product pitch. Reserve product conversation for Touch 3+ when you've earned trust. Also check: are you reaching the right persona? Intent signals often surface at the researcher level — make sure you're also reaching the economic buyer who controls the budget and renewal decision.

**Problem: Win rate on displacement campaigns is lower than greenfield**
Solution: This typically means your messaging isn't addressing switching risk adequately. The #1 reason competitive displacement deals stall is fear of migration. Audit your mid-funnel assets: Do you have a detailed, risk-free migration roadmap? A committed migration timeline? References from companies who completed migrations in similar timeframes? Customer testimonials that specifically address "the migration was easier than I expected"? If not, build these first. Also ensure you have a "parallel run" offer — letting prospects run both solutions simultaneously for 30-60 days dramatically reduces switching risk perception.

**Problem: Competitor launches counter-campaign or their sales team starts reaching out to your customers**
Solution: This is a signal your displacement campaign is working. Activate your customer retention program immediately: schedule QBRs with your top 20 customers, have CSMs proactively reach out to all customers in segments the competitor is targeting, and brief your champions with competitive talking points so they can defend the relationship internally. Simultaneously, accelerate displacement outreach — competitor counter-campaigns create fear in their customer base, which actually increases displaceability for the next 60-90 days.
