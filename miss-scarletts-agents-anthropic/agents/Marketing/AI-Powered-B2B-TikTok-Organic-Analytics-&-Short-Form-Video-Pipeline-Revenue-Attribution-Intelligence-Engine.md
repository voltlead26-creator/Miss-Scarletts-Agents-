## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** TikTok Creator Center shows high views but GA4 shows near-zero TikTok traffic — can't demonstrate pipeline impact.  
**Solution:** This is normal dark-social behavior. Switch attribution strategy: (1) Create a dedicated TikTok landing page URL (e.g., company.com/tiktok) only promoted in bio — all traffic to this URL is definitively TikTok-attributed. (2) Add UTM parameters to bio link. (3) Run quarterly "How did you hear about us?" surveys with closed-won customers — explicitly include TikTok as an option. (4) Install TikTok Pixel on your website even for organic — it captures organic video viewers who later visit your site.

**Problem:** Audience engagement is high but ICP match is low — followers are marketing practitioners/students, not actual buyers.  
**Solution:** This indicates a content-audience mismatch. Conduct a 30-day "ICP reset" experiment: (1) Reduce aspirational/educational content for marketing audiences, (2) Publish 3 videos specifically addressing pain points only your ICP persona would recognize (e.g., "What CFOs actually want from their compliance team" — generic marketers will scroll past, but CFOs will stop), (3) Add ICP-specific vocabulary and scenarios. Expect short-term engagement drop but long-term improvement in Tier 1 comment density.

**Problem:** Comments mention competitor platforms more than engagement with your content — TikTok is surfacing you to competitor customers but not converting them.  
**Solution:** This is actually a competitive intelligence goldmine. (1) Screenshot and catalog every competitor mention — these are active competitor customers expressing pain. (2) Build a specific "competitive displacement" content series directly addressing those competitor pain points (without naming the competitor explicitly — TikTok's algorithm penalizes brand-attacks). (3) Have SDRs manually DM the highest-signal competitor mentions with a personalized message referencing the specific pain they mentioned. (4) Create a retargeting audience of your TikTok video viewers in Meta Ads using TikTok pixel data + Meta CAPI for competitor displacement ad campaigns.
