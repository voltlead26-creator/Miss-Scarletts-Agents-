## Troubleshooting
- **Problem:** Outreach to defense prime contacts goes unanswered — LinkedIn and email both show low response rates.
  **Solution:** Defense prime employees are trained to be cautious with unsolicited vendor contact. Shift strategy to conference-first engagement — secure a meeting at SmallSat, AUSA, or Space Symposium before cold outreach. Alternatively, engage via their supplier portal (Lockheed's EXOSTAR, Raytheon's supplier diversity program) as a registered vendor, which legitimizes future outreach. Have a mutual contact from your advisory board or existing customer introduce you via email before you reach out cold.
- **Problem:** Government contacts engage at industry days but deals stall — no follow-on procurement activity.
  **Solution:** Government procurement stalls are almost always caused by budget cycle timing or requirements not yet finalized. Map every opportunity to the government fiscal year (Oct 1 start) and the specific program's FYDP (Future Years Defense Program) funding profile. Build a 90-day re-engagement sequence aligned to the FY budget submission cycle (February–April for President's Budget request). Stay visible through quarterly technical updates, not sales calls. When a Sources Sought drops, respond within 7 days with a capability statement — it signals to the program office that you're actively engaged.
- **Problem:** Defense prime teaming conversations stall at the BD director level — no path to a formal teaming agreement.
  **Solution:** Defense prime BD directors manage hundreds of potential teaming partners. You need to create pull-through from the program technical team, not push from the BD side. Get your technical solution evaluated by their program engineers by requesting a 30-minute technical exchange meeting (not a sales call). If their engineers validate your capability, the BD director will prioritize formalizing the relationship. Offer to fund a joint whitepaper or technical demonstration — this creates a documented collaboration that justifies a formal teaming agreement.
