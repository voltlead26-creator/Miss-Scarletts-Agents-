## Overview
Continuously mines customer reviews, support tickets, sales call transcripts, NPS responses, and community posts to extract revenue-grade proof points — then auto-generates sales-ready case study snippets, ROI claims, and objection-crushing testimonials segmented by buyer persona, vertical, deal size, and sales stage.

## Integration Tips
- **Gong/Chorus:** Set up keyword alerts ("ROI", "time savings", "switched from", "used to") to auto-flag call segments for evidence extraction. Pipe weekly transcript summaries into this prompt.
- **G2/Capterra:** Use the G2 API or weekly review digest emails. Batch 20-30 reviews per run. Tag new reviews in your CRM with evidence category.
- **Salesforce/HubSpot:** Create a custom "Customer Evidence" object. Each DEPLOYMENT READY proof point becomes a record tagged by vertical, persona, use case, and deal stage — queryable by SDRs via Copilot or Einstein.
- **Seismic/Highspot:** Auto-populate content cards with metric callouts from this prompt output. Set quarterly refresh cadence triggered by new evidence batches.
- **Notion/Google Sheets:** Build a living Evidence Library. Use Zapier to route new proof points from this prompt into the correct sheet tab based on vertical/persona tag.
- **Outreach/Salesloft:** Map specific proof snippets to email sequence templates — trigger insertion of the right testimonial based on prospect industry detected in CRM enrichment.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI extracts vague proof points with no specific metrics (e.g., "saved a lot of time").**
Solution: Add this instruction to your prompt: "If no quantified outcome is present, attempt to extract a directional signal (faster/cheaper/safer) and mark it as NEEDS VERIFICATION with a suggested follow-up interview question to obtain specifics." This preserves qualitative signals for future qualification rather than discarding them.

**Problem: Proof points are relevant to only one or two personas, leaving evidence gaps for economic buyers.**
Solution: Add a "Gap-Fill Mode" instruction: "For each non-CFO proof point, generate one speculative financial framing (e.g., 'If this team's time savings were valued at fully-loaded cost, the annualized impact would be approximately $X'). Flag these as INFERRED — not for direct attribution, but for discovery conversation framing."

**Problem: Sales reps aren't using the evidence library — they revert to generic claims.**
Solution: Move from a passive library to active "Proof of the Week" delivery. Pipe this prompt's output into a weekly Slack digest (via Zapier) formatted as a sales hook: "This week's proof: [Metric callout] — perfect for [persona] at [deal stage]. Copy-paste: [mini case study]."
