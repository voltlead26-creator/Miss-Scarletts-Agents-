## Success Metrics

- **Specificity test**: Every script, question, and objection response is specific to the product/persona — no generic B2B filler

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The discovery questions feel generic and reps aren't using them.**
Fix: Re-run the Advanced version with 2–3 verbatim quotes from real customer interviews in the context block. The model needs buyer language, not your internal product language, to generate questions that resonate. Pull quotes from your win/loss interviews or Gong recordings.

**Problem: The Challenger insight narrative sounds like a pitch, not an insight.**
Fix: The insight must challenge a belief the buyer currently holds — not explain your product. If it starts with "our product..." it's a pitch. The insight should start with "here's something unexpected about [their function/industry]..." Rerun with the instruction: "The insight must make the buyer say 'I hadn't thought about it that way' — not 'tell me more about your product.'"

**Problem: Objection responses are too long — reps won't memorize them.**
Fix: Ask the model to also produce a "30-second version" of each objection response — 2–3 sentences maximum. Use the long version for training and the short version as the rep-facing card. The validation + reframe + advance structure should still be present but compressed.
