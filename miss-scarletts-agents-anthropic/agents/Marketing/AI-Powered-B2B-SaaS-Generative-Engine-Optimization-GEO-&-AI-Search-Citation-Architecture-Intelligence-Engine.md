## Overview

This prompt architects a complete Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) program for B2B SaaS companies — auditing current AI search citation coverage, identifying high-value query gaps where AI engines are answering your buyers without mentioning your brand, and producing a content and entity authority roadmap that earns systematic citations in Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, and Claude. ## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Tested 30 queries and your brand is cited in 0 of them**
Fix: Start with entity authority before content. AI engines won't cite brands they don't recognize as authoritative entities. Priority order: (1) Get to 75+ G2 reviews with keyword-rich customer descriptions, (2) Claim and complete Google Business Profile, (3) Implement Organization schema with all sameAs links, (4) Get 3 mentions in indexed publications (even small industry blogs). Return to content optimization after entity foundation is established.

**Problem: AI engines cite your content but attribute it to the wrong company**
Fix: Entity disambiguation issue. Ensure your company name appears in the first 100 words of every content page, implement Organization schema with your exact legal company name, add "by [Company Name]" to all author bylines, and check that your Wikipedia article (if it exists) clearly distinguishes you from similarly named companies.

**Problem: Google AI Overviews shows your content but ChatGPT/Perplexity don't cite you**
Fix: Bing indexation gap. Submit your sitemap to Bing Webmaster Tools immediately, check for Bingbot crawl blocks in robots.txt, and build 5+ backlinks from Bing-indexed publications (Bing's link graph lags Google's by 3-6 months). Also increase Reddit and Quora presence — Perplexity heavily cites these platforms, and ChatGPT Search uses community content as social proof signals.
