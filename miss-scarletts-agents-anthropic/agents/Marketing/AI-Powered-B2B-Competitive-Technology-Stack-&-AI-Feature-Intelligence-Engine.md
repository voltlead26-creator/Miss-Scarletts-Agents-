## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The AI invents competitor feature details not in the provided intelligence.
**Fix:** Explicitly prepend your prompt with: "Base all claims ONLY on the intelligence snippets I provide. If you cannot verify a claim from the provided sources, flag it as [UNVERIFIED] rather than stating it as fact." Review confidence ratings before sharing any battlecard with sales.

**Problem:** Battlecard talking points feel generic or don't resonate with reps.
**Fix:** Add 3–5 actual verbatim objections your reps have heard in the field (pull from Gong call recordings or Slack messages from the sales team). Ask the model to rewrite battlecard responses specifically addressing those exact phrasings.

**Problem:** Analysis takes too long to produce and is outdated before it's useful.
**Fix:** Split the prompt into two tracks: (1) a weekly "early warning" run using only job postings and changelogs (Sections 1 and 6 only), and (2) a monthly deep-dive covering all sections. Automate the weekly version via a Make.com scenario triggered every Monday.
