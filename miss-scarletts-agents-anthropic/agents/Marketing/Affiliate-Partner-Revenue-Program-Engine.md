## Success Metrics

**Program Health Indicators:**
- Partner activation rate: >40% of recruited partners make at least 1 sale within 90 days (industry average: 20%)
- Partner-sourced CAC vs. blended CAC: partner CAC should be ≤80% of blended CAC within 6 months
- Revenue concentration: top 10% of partners driving <50% of revenue (avoid single-partner dependency)
- Partner NPS: >50 (measured via quarterly survey)

**30-Day Validation Gates:**
- Week 1: Platform live, first 10 partner invitations sent
- Week 2: First 5 partners accepted and logged in
- Week 4: First affiliate-tracked click recorded; at least 1 partner created content

**12-Month Targets:**
- 200+ active affiliates (at least 1 sale in trailing 90 days)
- 15%+ of new ARR attributed to affiliate channel
- Partner CAC ≤$2,500 (vs. $4,200 blended)
- Top affiliate case study published and used for recruitment

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Partners sign up but never promote (low activation)**
*Fix:* The #1 cause is a weak onboarding sequence. Specifically, most programs fail to give partners a "quick win" — a piece of content that's proven to work. Add a "steal this" resource: a complete, ready-to-publish review template or comparison article your top affiliate already used. Remove creative burden by 80% and activation rates jump from 20% to 40%+.

**Problem: High clicks, low conversion rate (<1%)**
*Fix:* Either partner audiences aren't qualified (wrong partner type) or the landing page isn't converting affiliate traffic. First, check if the affiliate is sending the right traffic (survey their audience). Second, create a dedicated affiliate landing page that continues the conversation from the affiliate's content ("As [Partner Name] mentioned..."). Conversion rates typically improve 30-50% with a personalized landing page vs. homepage.

**Problem: One or two partners driving 80%+ of revenue (concentration risk)**
*Fix:* You have a star partner problem, not an affiliate program. Actively recruit competitors to your top affiliate's format (if it's a YouTube reviewer, recruit 10 more YouTube reviewers). Also, formalize the partnership with your top performers — offer co-marketing, exclusives, or a reseller arrangement. This protects the relationship while you diversify.
