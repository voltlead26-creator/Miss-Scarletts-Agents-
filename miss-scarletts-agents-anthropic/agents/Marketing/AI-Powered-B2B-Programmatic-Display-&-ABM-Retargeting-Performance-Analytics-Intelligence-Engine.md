**Difficulty:** Advanced | **Time:** 25-35 min | **Tags:** programmatic, display-advertising, abm-retargeting, paid-media-analytics, b2b, demand-side-platform, attribution, pipeline-roi, dsp, account-based-marketing

## Overview
This prompt deploys an AI analytics agent to perform a comprehensive performance audit of B2B programmatic display and ABM retargeting campaigns — diagnosing spend efficiency by audience segment, surfacing account-level engagement patterns invisible to standard dashboards, and producing a prioritized optimization playbook that connects every impression to pipeline influence. Use it monthly for program health reviews or whenever CPL/CPO metrics are trending in the wrong direction.

## Success Metrics

- **TAL Coverage Rate:** Tier 1 ≥90% / Tier 2 ≥70% / Tier 3 ≥50% of accounts receiving impressions per month
- **Cost-per-Influenced-Opportunity:** Should decline quarter-over-quarter as optimization compounds
- **Pipeline Influence Rate:** % of open opportunities with programmatic exposure in the prior 90 days (benchmark for well-run programs: 40-60%)
- **Audience Efficiency Score:** Top 20% of audiences driving ≥60% of pipeline influence
- **Creative Freshness:** No creative running longer than 6 weeks without a CTR-trend review
- **Identity Resolution Match Rate:** ≥45% CRM-to-DSP sync rate (investigate if below 35%)
- **View-Through Attribution Quality:** Click-through conversions should represent ≥25% of total attributed conversions (if lower, incrementality testing is needed)
- **Frequency Balance:** <15% of budget going to accounts with >25 impressions/month and zero engagement signals

## Integration Tips

- **The Trade Desk / DV360:** Export "My Reports" with account-level reach and frequency data. Use the CRM Onboarding feature to sync Salesforce/HubSpot contact lists for proper match rate measurement. Schedule automated report exports to Google Sheets weekly.
- **6sense / Demandbase:** Pull "account engagement score" by target account and overlay against programmatic reach data in a spreadsheet join (use Account domain as the join key). High 6sense score + low programmatic reach = priority bid increase.
- **Salesforce:** Use Salesforce Campaigns to tag opportunities influenced by programmatic (view-through and click-through). Create a custom field "Programmatic Display Influenced" and set up a Pardot/Marketing Cloud automation to populate it when contact record is cookied.
- **Google Looker Studio / Tableau:** Build a unified dashboard pulling The Trade Desk API + Salesforce SOQL for account-level pipeline influence. Key dimensions: Account Name, Tier, Impressions (60-day), Pipeline Stage, Days-to-Close.
- **Bombora / TechTarget:** Pull weekly intent surge reports and configure bid multipliers in your DSP for accounts showing surge events within the last 7 days. Most DSPs support audience-level bid adjustments.
- **HubSpot:** Use HubSpot's LinkedIn Ads integration as a cross-validation tool — accounts engaged in LinkedIn + programmatic simultaneously should see priority treatment and accelerated sales follow-up.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: View-through attribution shows high pipeline influence but click-through shows nearly zero — leadership is skeptical of the ROI claims.**
Solution: Run a matched market or holdout incrementality test. Divide your TAL into two groups matched by firmographic profile, intent score, and pipeline stage. Suppress ads for the control group for 60 days. Compare pipeline creation rates. If influenced group shows ≥15% higher pipeline creation rate, you have statistically defensible proof of programmatic impact. Most DSPs (Trade Desk, DV360) support holdout testing natively.

**Problem: CPM is skyrocketing and budget runs out by mid-month, leaving accounts unserved in the second half.**
Solution: Set daily budget caps per audience segment rather than monthly totals. Enable "Even Pacing" in your DSP (not "ASAP delivery"). For Tier 1 priority accounts, set a hard CPM ceiling — you will lose some impressions but protect budget for the full month. Check if you're overbidding in premium inventory environments (LinkedIn Publisher Network, premium news sites) for audience types that would perform equally well in mid-tier inventory.

**Problem: CRM-to-DSP audience match rate is only 20-25%, meaning most of our TAL is unreachable through retargeting.**
Solution: Layer multiple identity approaches: (1) IP-based targeting using your account list with a provider like Bombora or 6sense, (2) Use LinkedIn Matched Audiences as a complement (higher B2B match rates but higher CPM), (3) Upload personal emails (not just work emails) from contacts — personal email match rates in programmatic DSPs run 2-3x higher than work email. (4) Add company domain targeting as a catch-all. Accept that for B2B, a 35-45% match rate is the realistic ceiling for cookie-based audiences; IP targeting fills the gap.
