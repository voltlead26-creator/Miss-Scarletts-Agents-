## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Partners won't write content or post on LinkedIn — "that's not how we get clients."
**Solution:** Never ask partners to CREATE content. Ask them to REVIEW content and provide one quote. Ghost-write everything, get their approval in 5 minutes. Show a single data point: "Partner X's one LinkedIn post generated 2 inbound calls last quarter." Track individual partner marketing contribution to pipeline to build the internal business case over time.

**Problem:** Case studies reveal confidential client information.
**Solution:** Use the composite method: combine 3 similar engagements into one anonymized case ("a PE-backed mid-market manufacturer in the midwest"). Lead with the problem and outcome data, not company names. Build a client permission program: post-engagement NPS survey → satisfied clients (9/10) receive a "reference request" offering three graduated levels — anonymous composite, named reference call for prospects only, or logo use with approval.

**Problem:** Generated marketing qualified leads aren't actually qualified — they're too small or wrong buyer type.
**Solution:** Add a client intake screener to the website contact form (company size, type, engagement type, timeline, budget range). Gate flagship research behind a company email address + 3-question screener. Configure CRM to auto-score leads and only alert partners when both company fit score and engagement fit score exceed threshold. Quarterly audit: review all leads against signed engagement characteristics and refine targeting.
