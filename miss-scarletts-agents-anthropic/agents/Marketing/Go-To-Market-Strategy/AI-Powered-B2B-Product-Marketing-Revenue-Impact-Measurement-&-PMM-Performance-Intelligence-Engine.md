## Integration Tips

- **Salesforce**: Create a custom object "PMM Attribution" with lookup to Opportunity; add picklist fields for PMM_Tier (Sourced/Influenced/Enabled), PMM_Asset_Used, PMM_Campaign_Type. Use Salesforce Flows to auto-stamp when Highspot content is accessed mid-deal.
- **Gong**: Create a Tracker category "PMM Messaging" with keywords from your messaging framework. Monitor how often sales reps use approved messaging vs. improvised language. Export weekly to a shared Google Sheet for PMM review.
- **Highspot**: Set up "Pitch Scorecards" for each PMM content asset to track engagement depth (pages viewed, time spent), sharing behavior (external views), and deal correlation. Export monthly to Tableau for ROI modeling.
- **Amplitude/Pendo**: Instrument a "PMM Launch Funnel" event group for each product launch — Feature_Awareness_Email_Click → In-App_Feature_Tour_Start → Feature_Used_First_Time → Feature_Used_5+ Times. Set PMM as the "owner" funnel in product analytics.
- **Tableau/Looker**: Build a PMM Revenue Dashboard with 4 views: (1) weekly PMM OKR status, (2) monthly competitive win rate by competitor, (3) quarterly content ROI table, (4) cumulative PMM-attributed ARR vs. quarterly target. Embed in the CMO's executive dashboard.
- **Notion/Confluence**: Host the PMM Measurement Playbook — methodology docs, OKR definitions, launch tiers — so RevOps and Finance can audit the approach at any time without pulling PMM into a meeting.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: RevOps or Sales leadership disputes PMM attribution, claiming the deals "would have closed anyway."**  
Solution: Run a controlled comparison before claiming impact. For battlecard content, compare win rates for Stage 2+ deals of the same ACV range and buyer segment where battlecard was (a) accessed and (b) not accessed. Present the methodology to RevOps and agree on it before publishing numbers. Use language like "PMM-correlated win rate lift" rather than "PMM caused these wins" — intellectual honesty builds more credibility than inflated claims.

**Problem: Highspot/Showpad shows low content utilization — sales reps aren't using PMM assets.**  
Solution: This is a distribution problem, not a measurement problem. Present low utilization data to Sales leadership as evidence that current content isn't meeting reps' needs. Run 5 Gong call reviews to understand what reps actually say in competitive deals vs. what the battlecard says. The gap between ideal messaging and actual rep behavior IS the PMM roadmap. Fix content before blaming adoption.

**Problem: PMM can't get clean win/loss data because Salesforce opportunity records don't have consistent loss reason tagging.**  
Solution: Implement a "Win/Loss Required" Salesforce validation rule that blocks Stage 6 (Closed Lost) transitions until a Loss Reason picklist is populated. Work with Sales Ops to implement this in 1 sprint. In the interim, use Gong's deal intelligence to extract loss reasons from call recordings using keyword search and AI call summaries — this gives 90-day retrospective data while the CRM hygiene improves.
