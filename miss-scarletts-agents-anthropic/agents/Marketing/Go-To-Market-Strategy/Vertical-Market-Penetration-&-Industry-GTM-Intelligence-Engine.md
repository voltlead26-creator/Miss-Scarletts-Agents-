- **ABM Platform (6sense / Demandbase / RollWorks):** Build vertical-specific intent audiences using the keyword clusters from Section 4. Set up vertical buying stage alerts to trigger outbound sequences automatically.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Vertical positioning feels generic — prospects say "sounds like every other SaaS vendor."**
Fix: You haven't gone deep enough on vertical vocabulary and commercial insight. Go back to Section 5 of the Advanced version and apply the Challenger Sale framework. Find the counterintuitive truth — the thing that contradicts what this vertical currently believes. Run 5 discovery calls with vertical prospects asking only "what do you believe about [problem domain] that most vendors get wrong?" The pattern that emerges is your commercial insight.

**Problem: Outbound open rates are low and replies are near-zero from vertical prospects.**
Fix: The messaging is still product-centric rather than vertical-problem-centric. Replace any mention of your product in the first two emails with an industry-specific insight, benchmark, or provocative question. The first email should be indistinguishable from a message sent by an industry analyst, not a vendor. Test subject lines that reference the vertical's specific regulatory context, a recent industry event, or a shared peer company by name.

**Problem: The vertical is generating discovery calls but deals are stalling in evaluation.**
Fix: You likely have a vertical champion but no economic buyer engagement. Revisit Section 2's mobilizer/blocker analysis. Build a "champion toolkit" — one-page executive summary, pre-built business case template, ROI calculator pre-loaded with vertical benchmarks — designed for your champion to sell upward internally. Also check whether compliance or legal review is an undisclosed blocker; in heavily regulated verticals, a security questionnaire or SOC 2 report delivered proactively can unblock deals stuck for weeks.
