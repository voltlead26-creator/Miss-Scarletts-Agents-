## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Our marketplace listing gets low traffic but we're getting good organic traffic on our website.**
Solution: Marketplace search algorithms weight customer reviews and transaction velocity heavily. In the first 90 days, proactively ask 5–10 existing customers to leave a marketplace review, even if they're on a direct contract. Also optimize your listing title to include the exact category keywords buyers search (e.g., "CSPM" not "Cloud Security Management"). Run a small sponsored listing budget ($2K–$5K/month) on AWS Marketplace to build initial visibility while organic ranking develops.

**Problem: Our AEs resist routing deals through marketplace because they're worried about losing commission credit or deal control.**
Solution: Fix this at the policy level before launch — never leave commission treatment ambiguous. Write and publish the internal marketplace deal policy with explicit quota credit rules (e.g., "AE receives full OTE credit for all marketplace-transacted deals. Marketplace is a procurement path, not a different channel."). Run a 15-minute FAQ session with the sales team where leadership explicitly confirms commission treatment. Create a single Slack channel (#marketplace-deals) where AEs can get rapid answers from the alliances manager without slowing down their deals.

**Problem: We enrolled in the co-sell program but cloud AMs don't engage with our referrals or respond to our outreach.**
Solution: Cloud AMs have 200+ ISV partners competing for their attention. Differentiation comes from mutual customer specificity — never send a generic "let's partner" message. Instead, identify 3–5 accounts where both companies have confirmed presence (you have an AE assigned, they have an AM assigned), and lead with a specific account and a specific joint use case. Attend the hyperscaler's regional partner summit or QBR event in person — 1 in-person meeting converts to 10x more engagement than portal messages. Also ensure your listing has 10+ customer reviews and >$50K in marketplace transactions before aggressively pushing co-sell — cloud AMs prioritize partners with proven marketplace traction.
