Use when launching a competitive push, entering a new segment, or responding to competitive losses.

- **Salesforce / HubSpot CRM:** Tag all competitive displacement opportunities with a "Displacement" deal type field. Track win/loss reasons with structured picklists so you can measure campaign ROI over time. Auto-enroll contacts where Competitor field = target competitor into displacement nurture sequences.
- **Outreach / Salesloft:** Build a dedicated competitive displacement sequence folder. Use dynamic fields to pull in trigger signal data (e.g., "[COMPETITOR_G2_COMPLAINT]") for hyper-personalization at scale.
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** Build a saved search of decision-makers at companies where company keywords include the competitor name (job postings, LinkedIn description). Export weekly for SDR sequencing.
- **Bombora / G2 Buyer Intent:** Set up intent topic alerts for competitor brand name + "alternative" or "review" queries. Route high-intent accounts to AE sequences, lower intent to SDR nurture.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Outreach sequences get low open rates (<25%) despite personalization
**Solution:** The subject line is doing the wrong job. For competitive displacement, subjects that name the competitor ("CaptivateIQ alternative") often underperform versus insight-led subjects that name the pain. A/B test 3 subject line approaches: (1) curiosity gap about the pain, (2) social proof ("How [Company Like Theirs] saved $X"), (3) direct trigger reference ("Re: your Q3 renewal"). Also check send-time optimization — competitor customers in ops roles often respond best to early morning sends (7-8am their timezone).

**Problem:** Comparison landing page gets traffic but low conversion
**Solution:** Comparison page visitors are skeptical by nature — they're doing research, not ready to buy. The CTA must be lower-friction than a demo request. Offer: a migration assessment, an ROI calculation personalized to their company size, or a 15-minute "is switching even worth it?" call. Conversion lift of 30-50% is typical when moving from "book a demo" to "get your migration cost analysis." Also ensure the page directly addresses the #1 switching objection in the first scroll (usually: "it's too much work").

**Problem:** SDRs go off-script and start feature-dumping vs. competitors in outreach
**Solution:** The displacement sequence only works when reps lead with insight and empathy, not comparison. Run a 30-minute roleplay specifically for competitive calls. Enforce a "no feature comparison in first contact" rule. The battle card should include explicit language on what NOT to say (e.g., never say "[Competitor] is bad at X" — instead ask questions that surface that pain from the prospect).
