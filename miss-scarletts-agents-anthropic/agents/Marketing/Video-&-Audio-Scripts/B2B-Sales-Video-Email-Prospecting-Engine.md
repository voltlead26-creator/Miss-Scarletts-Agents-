   - One sentence on what you do and who you help

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Scripts sound stiff and scripted when recorded — reps struggle to deliver them naturally.
**Solution:** Instruct the AI to add `[pause]` markers and write the script in spoken fragments, not complete sentences. Have reps record 3 takes and pick the most natural — imperfection is authenticity. Tell reps: "You're not reading a script, you're having a conversation you prepared for."

**Problem:** Personalization feels forced — the trigger observation in the opener feels shoehorned.
**Solution:** Only use triggers with genuine signal value (job postings, exec hires, funding, earnings call quotes). Avoid triggers that feel like surveillance (e.g., "I saw you visited our pricing page"). If no strong trigger exists, use industry-level insight instead: "Companies at your stage typically run into X right now — I wanted to address that directly."

**Problem:** High play rate but low reply rate — prospects watch but don't respond.
**Solution:** Diagnose the CTA. "Book a demo" kills momentum. Softer alternatives drive more replies: "Worth a quick reaction?" or "Does this sound familiar?" Lower-friction asks produce higher reply rates even if the eventual meeting takes one more touch to book. Also review the P.A.I.D. framework — if Insight/Value is too product-heavy too early, rework to lead with pain longer before introducing the solution.
