## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The script feels like a feature tour, not a story.**
*Fix:* For every feature you describe, ask: "So what?" Add the outcome. Then ask "Who proves it?" Add the proof. If the answer to either is vague, cut the feature entirely or replace it with one of your three Wow Moments. Only show features that create an emotional shift in the buyer.

**Problem: The demo is too long — watch completion drops below 30% in testing.**
*Fix:* Apply the "first 90 seconds" rule — if the buyer doesn't see the core pain resolved in 90 seconds, everything after is wasted. Cut the Reframe section in half. Remove any screen that requires explanation. If you're demoing more than 3 features, cut to 2. Every second of runtime must earn its place by raising buyer confidence.

**Problem: Our product has too many features and we can't decide what to show.**
*Fix:* Run a "win/loss interview" filter — ask your last 10 won deals: "What was the one thing in our demo that made you say 'I need this'?" The feature that surfaces in 6+ of those interviews is Wow Moment #1. Everything else is context or a post-sale education problem, not a demo problem.
