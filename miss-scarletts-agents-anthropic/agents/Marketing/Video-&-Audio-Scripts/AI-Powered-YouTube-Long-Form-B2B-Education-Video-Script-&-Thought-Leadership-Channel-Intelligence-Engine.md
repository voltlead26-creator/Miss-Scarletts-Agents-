## Success Metrics

- **Hook retention rate:** At least 70% of viewers should still be watching at the 2-minute mark (check in YouTube Studio → Audience Retention)
- **Average View Duration (AVD):** Target 40–50% AVD for a 15-minute video (6–7.5 minutes average watch time)
- **CTR from impressions:** 4–8% click-through rate on thumbnail/title (check in YouTube Studio → Reach)
- **Comment-to-view ratio:** 0.5–1.5% — indicates genuine engagement vs. passive watching
- **Pipeline attribution:** Track UTM-coded CTA link clicks (demo bookings, content downloads) in your CRM for 90 days post-publish
- **Search ranking:** Target page-one ranking for primary keyword within 90 days using Google Search Console
- **Subscriber conversion rate:** 2–5% of viewers should subscribe per video (indicates content-channel fit)

## Integration Tips

- **YouTube Studio:** Paste chapter markers directly into the video description to activate YouTube's native chapter navigation — improves search ranking and viewer retention
- **HubSpot / Salesforce:** Add UTM parameters to all CTA links (e.g., `?utm_source=youtube&utm_medium=video&utm_campaign=attribution-framework`) so every demo booking, download, or trial signup is tracked back to the specific video in your CRM
- **Notion / Airtable (Content Calendar):** Log each video as a card with fields: Topic, Primary Keyword, Target Viewer Persona, CTA Offer, Publish Date, AVD Target, 30-day CTR, Pipeline Attributed — build a video content dashboard to see which topics drive the most revenue
- **Descript / Opus Clip:** After publishing, upload the raw transcript to Descript for automated short-form clip extraction, or use Opus Clip to AI-select the highest-retention 60-second moments for social distribution
- **Rev / Castmagic:** Generate SEO-grade transcript and auto-populated show notes from the final audio file — feed the cleaned transcript back into the AI-Powered Definitive Guide prompt to create a companion blog post within minutes of video publication
- **Google Search Console:** Track keyword impressions and clicks 30 days post-publish to measure search authority lift; if the video is ranking for related queries but not the primary keyword, use that data to refine the title in a pinned comment or description edit

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The narration sounds stiff or written-to-be-read, not natural when spoken aloud.
**Solution:** Read the script aloud while recording a voice memo. Any sentence that makes you stumble or pause unnaturally needs rewriting. Prompt the AI: "Rewrite this narration at a 9th-grade reading level in casual, confident spoken English — remove all subordinate clauses and passive voice."

**Problem:** The video has high initial CTR but audience retention falls below 30% at the 3-minute mark.
**Solution:** This is a hook-to-content mismatch — the title promised something the content doesn't deliver fast enough. Check your "Audience Retention" report in YouTube Studio. Add an explicit segment at the 2–3 minute mark that directly delivers the core promise from the title. Use the open loop from the hook to keep viewers waiting for the payoff.

**Problem:** The video generates views but zero pipeline or CTA clicks.
**Solution:** The commercial offer isn't integrated into the educational flow — it's landing as an ad break. Rewrite the mid-roll commercial mentions using the "Teaching Moment Tie-In" formula: [educational insight] → [why this is hard to do manually] → [what Mosaic/your product does automatically] → [back to education]. The CTA should solve a problem the viewer just realized they have because of the video.
