## Integration Tips

- **Gong:** Configure Tracker Groups for each major competitor. Set Smart Alerts to notify competitive PMM within 24 hours of a displacement-signal call. Use Gong's Deal Intelligence to auto-surface deals with competitive mentions.
- **Salesforce:** Add a "Displaced Competitor" picklist field and "Switch Trigger" multi-select to the Opportunity object. Build a "Competitive Wins" report type filtered to Closed Won + Displaced Competitor ≠ blank. Use Salesforce Flow to trigger the content collection workflow automatically.
- **Zapier/Make:** Chain: Salesforce closed-won trigger → Notion win story page creation → Slack AE notification → HubSpot competitive sequence update → Highspot folder routing. Use Zapier Tables as a staging area for in-progress win stories.
- **Highspot/Seismic:** Create a "Competitive Proof" Smart Page per competitor with auto-populated assets. Use Spot analytics to track which win stories reps are using in competitive deals and correlate to win rate.
- **HubSpot:** Add competitive proof snippets to the relevant competitor-named email sequences. Tag contacts with their current competitor tech stack so sequences auto-personalize.
- **Notion:** Use a Notion database as your win story CMS with properties for competitor, vertical, ACV, switch trigger, and content status. Create database views filtered by competitor for each PMM or sales rep.
- **Loom/BombBomb:** Embed the AE async video question script into a Loom template so reps record their win story in a single structured session immediately after the DocuSign.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** AEs don't fill out the competitive fields in Salesforce or record their win story video.
**Solution:** Gate the "Closed Won" stage advancement with required Salesforce fields for any deal where the Opportunity includes a competitive tag. Add the win story recording to the AE's commission confirmation email: "Your commission is processing — while it clears, record your 10-minute win story and earn a $50 gift card." Track completion in the monthly competitive win report shared with sales leadership.

**Problem:** Legal won't approve using the competitor's name in published case studies.
**Solution:** Build two versions of every asset from day one: named (with competitor, for internal use and Highspot) and anonymized (with "a leading [category] platform" language, for public web and LinkedIn). The anonymized version can reference "per-transaction pricing models" and "90-day implementation timelines" without naming anyone. SEO value shifts from competitor-branded searches to category-level searches ("accounts payable automation alternatives"), which often convert better anyway.

**Problem:** Win stories are collected but never activated — they sit in a Google Drive folder nobody opens.
**Solution:** Activation requires friction removal. Three fixes: (1) Add the 3 most recent competitor-specific win story bullets directly into the CRM deal record as a custom Salesforce field populated by automation — reps see proof without leaving their workflow. (2) Set a weekly Slack digest that posts the latest win story asset to #sales-enablement every Monday morning. (3) Require competitive deal call prep (in your sales methodology) to include citing one specific win story — and track whether reps do it via Gong topic analysis.
