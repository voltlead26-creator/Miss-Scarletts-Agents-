## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Customers decline to participate or approvals take weeks**
Fix: Create a tiered approval program — Tier 1 (logo + quote only, 2-minute approval), Tier 2 (short case study, email approval with Docusign), Tier 3 (full case study + video). Start with Tier 1 for reluctant customers. Offer named attribution alternatives: "a leading [INDUSTRY] company" lets you publish without naming. Embed approval into CSM QBR: get verbal consent and draft approval in the same meeting.

**Problem: AI-generated first drafts don't match the customer's voice or exaggerate outcomes**
Fix: Always include the exact verbatim quotes from Gong in your extraction — never paraphrase. Add a verification pass: instruct the AI to flag any claim it derived (not directly quoted) with [DERIVED — VERIFY]. Send customers the draft with an explicit note: "We only want to publish what's accurate — please edit freely." Keep a quote library in Notion with verified, approved quotes per customer for reuse.

**Problem: Sales team doesn't use the case studies even after publishing**
Fix: Make case studies searchable by objection, not just by vertical. Tag every case study: "Use when prospect says: '[specific objection]'" in your sales enablement tool. Create a weekly Slack digest: "New proof assets published this week — here's when to use each one." Add case study deployment as a MEDDPICC coaching checkpoint: AEs should confirm which case study is in the deal at the Economic Buyer meeting stage.
