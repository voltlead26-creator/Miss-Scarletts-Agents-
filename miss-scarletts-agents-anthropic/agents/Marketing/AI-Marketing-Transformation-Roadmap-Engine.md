## Integration Tips

- **HubSpot:** Use HubSpot's Breeze AI agents and AI Content Assistant as Phase 1 foundation — no API needed, zero IT friction, works inside existing workflows
- **Salesforce:** Map roadmap milestones to Salesforce Campaigns so ROI tracking is automatic; use Einstein GPT for sales-marketing alignment use cases
- **Notion:** Build your AI transformation tracker as a Notion database with phase, owner, status, and ROI fields; Notion AI can auto-summarize weekly progress for your CEO update
- **Zapier/Make:** Use as the no-code integration layer for all Phase 1 and Phase 2 automations before investing in custom API work — most quick wins can be live in under 4 hours
- **Slack:** Create an #ai-wins channel where the team shares prompt discoveries and workflow breakthroughs — organic adoption compounds faster than any formal training program
- **Google Sheets:** Build the financial model in Sheets and connect it to GA4 and HubSpot via native connectors so ROI tracks automatically, reducing board-prep time from 4 hours to 20 minutes

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The roadmap output is too generic and doesn't feel specific to our industry or team size.
**Solution:** In the Advanced Version, add 2-3 sentences about your specific ICP, sales motion (PLG vs. sales-led), and the 1-2 channels that drive 80% of your pipeline. The model anchors all recommendations to your actual growth engine rather than a generic B2B template.

**Problem:** The financial model projections are hard to defend to a skeptical CFO.
**Solution:** After generating the model, add this follow-up: "Now steelman the CFO's objections to each ROI line item. For each, give me the conservative case (50% of projected) and what assumptions would need to hold for the base case to be valid." This produces a defensible range and pre-handles skepticism in the room.

**Problem:** The team is resistant — the roadmap feels threatening to their jobs.
**Solution:** Re-run Section 5 with this additional instruction: "For each role on our team, describe specifically how their job evolves (not disappears) over the 12-month roadmap. What does a day in the life look like for our content writer in Month 12 vs. today? Make it inspiring, concrete, and tied to career growth." Share this with the team before the roadmap itself.
