## Troubleshooting

**Problem: X Ads Manager shows 3x more conversions than CRM/GA4 — which number is right?**
Solution: X Ads Manager over-reports due to broad view-through attribution windows (especially 30-day view-through on awareness campaigns). Your CRM/GA4 number is more accurate for direct response. To reconcile: (1) set X Ads Manager to 1-day click, 0-day view-through attribution window, (2) add X UTMs to all landing page URLs, (3) compare the resulting X Ads Manager number against GA4 — the gap should close to < 15%. Use the blended number (CRM + assisted) for true channel value.

**Problem: Brand safety score keeps coming back High Risk despite adding exclusions — ads still appearing next to controversial content.**
Solution: X's brand safety controls operate at three layers, and most advertisers only configure one. Fix all three: (1) in X Ads Manager Campaign Settings → Brand Safety & Suitability, enable IAS pre-bid filtering and select all relevant exclusion categories; (2) add a negative keyword list at the ad group level (X keyword targeting exclusions prevent your ad from appearing in searches/feeds containing those terms); (3) for Amplify Pre-Roll specifically, whitelist only Tier 1 verified publishers rather than allowing open network placements. If running without IAS or DoubleVerify, manual controls alone will not achieve Low Risk status at scale — invest in a brand safety tool.

**Problem: Follower lookalike campaigns (targeting followers of competitor accounts) have low CTR and high CPA compared to interest targeting.**
Solution: Follower lookalike quality degrades at scale.
