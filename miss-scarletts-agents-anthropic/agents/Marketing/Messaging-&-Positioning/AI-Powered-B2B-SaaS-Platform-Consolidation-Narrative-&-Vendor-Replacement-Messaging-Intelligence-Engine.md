## Overview
Builds a complete platform consolidation messaging system for B2B SaaS companies positioning their suite as the replacement for 3–7 point solutions — enabling PMMs and sales teams to win budget consolidation conversations with CFOs, IT leaders, and procurement teams. Use it when you're expanding from a point solution to a platform and need messaging that makes the "replace your stack" case financially, operationally, and strategically irresistible.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: CFO doesn't believe the TCO numbers — dismisses them as "vendor math."**
Fix: Anchor every TCO line item to an auditable source — use the prospect's own Coupa/Zip/Vendr procurement data during discovery ("Can you pull your actual spend on these vendors from your finance system?"). The moment they fill in their own numbers, it's no longer vendor math. Also include a "conservative case" TCO using 50% of your claimed soft savings — even the conservative number should still show net savings.

**Problem: IT leader is a champion for one of the point solutions (e.g., they implemented Gong and own that relationship).**
Fix: Don't position it as replacing — position it as "consolidating under a single security posture and SLA." Use the Integration Teardown proof asset (Module 5) to show IT the operational complexity they currently own and frame platform consolidation as reducing their risk surface and maintenance burden, not eliminating their work. Give IT a defined role in the migration project (they become the integration lead, not the person being replaced).

**Problem: End users revolt because they love a specific point solution (e.g., "We won't give up Gong").**
Fix: Use the "upgrade, not replace" language from Module 2D. Never frame it as taking away a tool — frame it as giving end users everything they love about that tool PLUS cross-platform capabilities they don't currently have. If possible, demo the capability head-to-head with a real user's workflow in the room. User sentiment almost always shifts after a live demo when they see their exact workflow works — and works faster.
