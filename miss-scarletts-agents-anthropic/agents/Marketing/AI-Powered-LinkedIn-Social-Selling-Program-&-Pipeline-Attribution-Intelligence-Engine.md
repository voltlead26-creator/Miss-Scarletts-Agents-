- Use "Buyer Intent" feature (Advanced Plus) to surface accounts researching your category

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Reps aren't posting consistently — content creation feels too time-consuming.**
Solution: Implement an AI content calendar agent that generates a full week of posts every Monday morning (use the automation prompt in Section 5.2). Reduce posting frequency for Tier 2/3 reps to 1x/week. Provide a 30-post swipe file of proven templates specific to your product category so reps can post with minimal editing. Run a "First 5 Posts" challenge with a small prize for the first rep to complete their initial 5 posts.

**Problem: Can't attribute LinkedIn pipeline accurately — reps don't log LinkedIn activity in CRM.**
Solution: Simplify attribution to a single required field on every new contact: "How did you first engage with this person?" with LinkedIn as a dropdown option. Add a Slack reminder every Friday: "Log your LinkedIn meetings from this week." Implement the Zapier automation so activity is auto-logged when possible. For the first 30 days, accept imperfect attribution and focus on building the habit — perfect data beats no data.

**Problem: LinkedIn connection acceptance rate is below 20% — outreach isn't converting.**
Solution: Audit the last 50 connection requests sent — most low acceptance rates come from generic messages, approaching cold accounts with no prior engagement, or targeting titles too senior without a warm signal. Fix: (1) Engage with prospect content for 5-7 days before sending a connection request, (2) Always personalize with a specific signal ("saw your post on [topic]"), (3) Lower the ask — connect first, pitch never in the request message. Target acceptance rate: 35%+.
