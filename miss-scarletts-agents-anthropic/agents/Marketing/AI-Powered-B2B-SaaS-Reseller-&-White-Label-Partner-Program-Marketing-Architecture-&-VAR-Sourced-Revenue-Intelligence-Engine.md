## Integration Tips

- **PartnerStack / Impartner**: Auto-enroll newly activated partners in the 90-day email enablement kit. Use deal registration webhooks to trigger the partner pipeline scorecard in Slack.
- **Crossbeam / Reveal**: Feed account mapping data into the QBR template automatically. Flag shared accounts as ABM targets in HubSpot for co-sell activation.
- **HubSpot CRM**: Tag all partner-sourced contacts with `partner_source = [PARTNER_ID]` at creation. Build a partner attribution dashboard using deal source + influence fields.
- **Salesforce + Alliances**: Map `Partner_Account__c` lookup on all partner-registered opportunities. Build a partner leaderboard report visible in partner portal.
- **Zapier / Make.com**: Trigger: New deal registered in PartnerStack → Action: Create opportunity in Salesforce + notify CAM in Slack + add partner to co-sell email sequence.
- **Notion / Confluence**: House the Partner Marketing Activation Kit as a linked database. Partners access their tier-specific kit via a gated partner portal page.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Partners register deals but don't engage with co-marketing campaigns — MDF goes unspent.
**Solution:** Reframe MDF as "use-it-or-lose-it" with a quarterly deadline. Assign a Partner Marketing Manager (or CAM) to personally call Gold/Platinum partners who have not submitted a campaign brief in the first 30 days. Offer a "starter campaign" fully built by your team that requires only their logo and a send-date approval.

**Problem:** White-label partners modify your approved messaging, creating compliance and brand risk.
**Solution:** Implement a content lockdown process — deliver all white-label assets as locked PDF or branded Canva templates where only designated fields (logo, partner name, contact) are editable. Add a contractual clause to your reseller agreement requiring written approval for any messaging changes. Audit quarterly.

**Problem:** Partner pipeline attribution is disputed — sales team claims partner "didn't source" the deal.
**Solution:** Establish a deal registration timestamp rule: any deal registered in the PRM before your direct team touches the account is partner-sourced. Use Crossbeam to create a neutral arbiter — if the account appears in the partner's "customers" list at the time of registration, it's partner-sourced, not influenced. Document this in your partner program agreement.
