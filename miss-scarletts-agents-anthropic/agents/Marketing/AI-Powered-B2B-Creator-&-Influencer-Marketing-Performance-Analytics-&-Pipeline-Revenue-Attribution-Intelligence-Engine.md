## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Near-zero direct attribution from podcast and YouTube creators despite positive sales team anecdotes about brand awareness.**
Solution: Promo code and vanity URL capture rates for podcast are typically 15–25% of true influenced conversions. First, add a "How did you hear about us?" open-text field to your demo request form and review 3 months of responses for creator mentions. Second, pull your last 50 won deals and have your CS or AE team ask "Where did this customer first hear about us?" in QBRs. Third, implement a matched market test: run one creator for 90 days in specific geographies or verticals while holding out control regions, then compare pipeline generation rates.

**Problem: LinkedIn thought leader sponsored posts show high impressions and engagement but zero trackable pipeline.**
Solution: LinkedIn's click-through rates for thought leadership posts are naturally low (0.3–1.5% typical) because the format drives brand familiarity and trust, not immediate conversion. The value is brand familiarity that shortens sales cycles and improves demo-to-close rates for prospects later encountered via other channels. Measure this by: (1) comparing demo show rates and close rates for prospects from LinkedIn-heavy accounts vs. control accounts, (2) checking Gong transcripts for prospects who engaged with the creator post months before becoming an opportunity, and (3) tracking whether target account contacts in your 6sense/Bombora intent platform showed engagement lift with your brand after the creator campaign.

**Problem: Multiple creators claim credit for the same pipeline opportunity, inflating total attributed pipeline beyond actual pipeline.**
Solution: This is a multi-touch attribution problem. Implement a consistent attribution rule across all creator tracking: use first-touch attribution for pipeline sourcing (which creator introduced the prospect to your brand) and multi-touch attribution for pipeline influence (all creator touchpoints that touched the deal before close). In HubSpot or Salesforce, set creator campaign member status to "Responded" only for the first creator touchpoint, and "Influenced" for subsequent creator touches on the same deal. Total your "creator-sourced" pipeline separately from "creator-influenced" pipeline in reporting to avoid double-counting.
