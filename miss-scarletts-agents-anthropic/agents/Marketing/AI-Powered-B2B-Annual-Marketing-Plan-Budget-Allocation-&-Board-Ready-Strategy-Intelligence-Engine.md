## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The AI produces vague budget percentages without dollar amounts.**
Fix: In your input, always specify total program spend as a dollar figure separate from total budget (including headcount). The model needs absolute numbers to produce an internally consistent financial model. Add: "Force all budget outputs to show both $ amounts and % of total program spend."

**Problem: OKRs come back as activities (e.g., "launch content program") rather than measurable outcomes.**
Fix: Add this constraint to your prompt: "Every Key Result must follow the format: '[Metric name] from [current baseline] to [specific number] by [specific date].' No KR may describe an activity or output — only a measurable result."

**Problem: The plan is strategically sound but lacks operational specificity — too high-level to execute.**
Fix: After generating the full plan, run a second prompt: "Take Section 6 (Strategic Bets) and Section 10 (30/60/90 Day Plan) and expand each into a project brief with: owner role, week-by-week milestone table, dependencies, blockers to resolve in week 1, and definition of done."
