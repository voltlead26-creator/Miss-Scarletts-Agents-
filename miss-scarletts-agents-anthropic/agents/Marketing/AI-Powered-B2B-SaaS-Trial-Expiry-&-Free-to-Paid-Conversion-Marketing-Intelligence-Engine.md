## Integration Tips

- **Customer.io:** Use `segment` calls from your product analytics to sync behavioral segments in real time. Set up a "trial_expiry_campaign" workflow with entry trigger `trial_end_date = today + 7`. Use liquid templating for `{{pipeline_count}}` and `{{last_feature_used}}` dynamic personalization.
- **HubSpot:** Create a custom lifecycle stage "Trial User" with sub-stages (Power / Activated / At-Risk / Dormant) synced via product analytics webhook. Use Workflows with enrollment triggers based on product event properties passed via the HubSpot API.
- **Salesforce:** Set up a Lead Score field that auto-increments based on trial behavior signals from your CDP or product analytics tool. Configure Process Builder or Flow to alert the SDR queue when score crosses the escalation threshold.
- **Intercom:** Layer in-app messages on top of email using Intercom's "Post" message type for Day 12 and Day 13 banners. Use Intercom's "Product Tours" for at-risk users who haven't activated — trigger a guided tour instead of just an email.
- **Pendo / Appcues:** Use Pendo Guides or Appcues flows to show a conversion CTA modal on pricing page visits during the final 48 hours. Pass the guide-completion event back to Customer.io/HubSpot to suppress email if user converted via in-app.
- **Amplitude / Mixpanel:** Build a "Trial Conversion Funnel" chart tracking Signup → Activation → Day 7 Engaged → Converted. Slice by segment to identify which behavioral cohorts convert best and prioritize those activation milestones in your onboarding flow.
- **Clay + Instantly:** For SDR escalation workflows, use Clay to enrich trial user firmographic data (employee count, funding round, tech stack) before the alert reaches the SDR. Route high-value accounts to Outreach sequences automatically.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Trial users open expiry emails but still don't convert — high open rate, low click rate.**
Solution: The subject line is working but the body isn't connecting to their specific situation. Add more behavioral personalization using product events (e.g., reference the exact feature they used, the specific data they uploaded, the integrations they connected). Generic "your trial ends tomorrow" body copy won't convert users who opened because they're curious — they need to see *their* progress reflected back at them to feel the loss aversion.

**Problem: Power Users are converting at low rates despite being highly engaged.**
Solution: Power Users are often evaluating multiple vendors simultaneously or waiting for budget approval — they're engaged but not unilaterally deciding. Deploy the champion enablement email variant: send them a pre-written business case template they can forward to their manager or finance team. Add a "send to your manager" CTA alongside the standard upgrade CTA. Power Users frequently need internal air cover more than persuasion.

**Problem: SDR team ignores escalation alerts because signal quality is low.**
Solution: Tighten the escalation threshold — fewer but higher-quality alerts. Add at least two qualifying signals before firing an alert (e.g., company size >200 employees AND pricing page viewed 2+ times AND activation event completed). Include the user's specific product usage data in the Slack notification (not just "high engagement") so the SDR understands why the account is worth calling. Run a monthly audit of SDR conversion rate from trial escalations vs. inbound — use the data to refine thresholds quarterly.
