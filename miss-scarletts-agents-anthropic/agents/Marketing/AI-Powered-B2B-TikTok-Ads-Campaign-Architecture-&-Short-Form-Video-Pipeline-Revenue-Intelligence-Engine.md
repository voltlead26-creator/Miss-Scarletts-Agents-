## Troubleshooting

**Problem: High impressions and decent ThruPlay rate but very low CTR and zero leads from TikTok Lead Gen Forms**
Solution: Your hook is working to hold attention but your CTA is not converting — this is a mid-creative breakdown, not an audience problem. Audit the transition point between content value and call-to-action: if your ad is purely educational with no natural handoff to a reason to act (offer, proof, urgency), viewers will watch but not convert. Rebuild your ad structure to include a "bridge" at the 70-80% mark: "If you want to see how [Company] can help you apply this in your workflow, the form below takes 30 seconds." Also check your Lead Gen Form setup — if your qualifying question feels like a survey rather than a step toward value, friction kills completion.
