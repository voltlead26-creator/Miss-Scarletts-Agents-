## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Adapted content sounds stilted or overly formal despite prompt instructions**
Fix: Add a "Voice Calibration" section with 3 sample sentences from the target market's leading trade publication in that language. Instruct the AI to match that publication's editorial register. Example: "Match the tone of Wirtschaftswoche (Germany) or Les Echos (France) in their tech coverage."

**Issue: AI generates inaccurate regulatory claims for target market (e.g., incorrect GDPR interpretation)**
Fix: Always include "FLAG any regulatory claims for human legal review — do not state compliance assertions as facts. Use language such as 'designed to support compliance with...' rather than 'is fully compliant with...'" This protects against hallucinated compliance claims that could create legal liability.

**Issue: Local keyword suggestions are generic and not competitive-intelligence-backed**
Fix: Supply the AI with 3-5 competitor URLs active in the target market and instruct: "Analyze the H1 tags, meta descriptions, and body copy from these local competitors to identify the keyword patterns and positioning language they're using, then build our adapted content to differentiate from and outposition them."
