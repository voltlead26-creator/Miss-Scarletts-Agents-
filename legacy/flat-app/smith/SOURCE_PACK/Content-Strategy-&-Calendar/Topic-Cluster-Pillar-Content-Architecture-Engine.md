## Integration Tips
- **Ahrefs / Semrush:** Export keyword data for your industry, paste volume and difficulty figures into the Advanced Version inputs for more accurate Priority Scores. Use Site Explorer to audit competitors' topic cluster gaps.
- **Notion / Confluence:** Paste the full cluster map into a Notion database with status fields (Brief / In Progress / Published / Indexed) to run it as a content production sprint board.
- **HubSpot CMS:** Use the Pillar Page + Cluster Page feature natively — tag each published piece with its topic cluster to auto-generate internal linking suggestions.
- **Google Search Console:** After publishing, monitor "Queries" for each cluster article monthly. Feed actual ranking keywords back into this prompt to refine the architecture at 90-day reviews.
- **Surfer SEO / Clearscope:** Feed the pillar page brief's LSI terms and entity list into your on-page optimization tool before writing.
- **Zapier / Make:** Automate a workflow: when a cluster article hits "Published" in Notion, trigger a Slack alert to the SEO lead to verify internal links are live.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** The engine suggests pillar topics that are too broad and competitive (e.g., "marketing automation" for a startup with DR 20).
**Solution:** Add your Domain Rating and specify "limit head keywords to under 2,000 monthly searches" in the constraints field. The engine will suggest sub-niche pillar topics where you can realistically compete within 90 days.

**Problem:** Cluster articles feel disconnected from the product — too informational, no conversion path.
**Solution:** Add this constraint to the Advanced Version: "Every cluster article must include a natural commercial bridge — a section explaining how [Product Name] solves the problem described in this article. Flag the placement." This forces product integration into the editorial brief.

**Problem:** Keyword volume estimates from the AI are inaccurate.
**Solution:** Use this prompt to generate the architecture and article titles, then verify volumes manually in Ahrefs, Semrush, or Google Keyword Planner before finalizing priority scores. The AI's relative prioritization logic is reliable even when absolute volume numbers vary.
