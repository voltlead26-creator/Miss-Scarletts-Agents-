## Overview
This engine designs, launches, and optimizes a structured B2B Creator Partner Program — a formal, ongoing relationship with independent B2B content creators (newsletter writers, podcast hosts, LinkedIn thought leaders, YouTube tech reviewers) who produce authentic content about your product for their established audiences, driving compounding organic pipeline that scales without paid media spend.

**2.2 Compensation Model Decision Framework**
Build a decision tree for choosing the right compensation model per creator:
- Fixed monthly retainer: When to use, risk/reward profile, negotiation anchors

- Equity/advisory compensation: Criteria for when to offer, vesting schedules, SEC compliance considerations

**2.3 Program Terms & Legal Framework**

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Creators aren't producing content on schedule (low compliance rate)**
*Solution:* Implement a content calendar with 2-week deadline visibility. Send a Loom video at the start of each month previewing the agreed content topics, re-energizing creators. Add a content compliance clause in agreements with a 30-day grace period before compensation reduction. Most compliance issues stem from unclear expectations, not creator disengagement — over-communicate deadlines and provide outlines when creators ask.

**Problem: Creator-attributed traffic isn't converting into pipeline (high bounce rate, low MQL rate)**
*Solution:* Run a content-to-landing-page audit. Often, creator content drives curiosity from their audience but your website isn't set up to convert "creator-warmed" visitors who aren't ready for a demo request. Add a "content hub" landing page for creator traffic with multiple conversion paths: newsletter sign-up, free tool, resource download. Creator audiences often need a lower-friction first conversion before becoming demo-ready.

**Problem: Top-performing creators are being poached by competitors with higher budgets**
*Solution:* Retention is built before the competitor call comes. Build non-financial lock-in: product advisory board seats, co-authored research reports with their name attached, conference speaking opportunities that enhance their personal brand. Increase total compensation package value through non-cash benefits. Have a quarterly retention conversation with Elite creators proactively to understand their satisfaction before they're approached by a competitor.
