## Troubleshooting

**"My CFO says brand is unmeasurable and won't approve the budget"**
Use the holdout test methodology: run brand campaigns in 2 markets/regions while withholding in 2 comparable ones. After 90 days, compare branded search growth, inbound lead velocity, and pipeline creation rates between groups. Even a 10% difference in inbound lead velocity demonstrates measurable brand lift. Present this as a "brand investment test" rather than a full program — lower barrier to approval.

**"The sales team says brand doesn't generate leads and wants the budget back"**
Pull your CRM data to show the correlation between deal close rate and brand familiarity at first contact. Use the question in discovery calls: "Had you heard of us before this call?" Track deals where answer is "yes" vs. "no" — typical brand-aware deals close 20-35% faster and at 15-25% higher win rates. Present this data to sales leadership before the budget conversation.

**"We're a Series A company — is brand investment premature?"**
At Series A ($1M-$5M ARR), brand investment should be founder-led thought leadership only (free) and 1-2 targeted sponsorships in your exact niche (<$50K total). Do NOT run broad awareness campaigns yet — you need to nail ICP precision first. This full playbook applies to Series B+ companies with validated ICP, repeatable demand gen, and budget to sustain a minimum 12-month brand investment horizon.
