## Troubleshooting

**"The content passes the voice test but gets zero ICP engagement"**
The voice is right but the angle is wrong. Audit your last 5 posts — are they about the executive's world or the buyer's world? Every post should make an ICP say "this is my situation" not "this person is impressive." Run a 2-week experiment where every post starts with a buyer frustration, not an executive insight.

**"Executive keeps editing everything into corporate-speak"**
This is a trust problem, not a voice problem. The executive doesn't yet trust that direct, specific, personal content is safe to publish. Show them 3 competitor executive posts that are performing well and are more direct than their comfort zone. Also: have them approve a content brief (angle + hook concept) before the ghostwriter writes — reduces full rewrites.

**"Getting engagement from practitioners but not from decision-makers"**
Your content is too tactical. Decision-makers engage with content about strategic tradeoffs, organizational decisions, and outcomes — not execution tips. Audit your content mix: you likely have too much "how-to" and too little "what I'd do differently" or "the real reason this initiative failed." Shift 60% of content to strategic observations, not tactical advice.
