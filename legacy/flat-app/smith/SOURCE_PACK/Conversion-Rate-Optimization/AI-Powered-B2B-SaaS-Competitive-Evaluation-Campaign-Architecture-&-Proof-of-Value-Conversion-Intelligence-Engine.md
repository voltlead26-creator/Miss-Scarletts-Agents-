## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Champion is engaged but Economic Buyer is dark — no email opens, no ad clicks, no meeting attendance.
**Solution:** Pivot to an indirect influence strategy. Send Champion a "Board Presentation Template" specifically designed for their CFO conversation, pre-populated with industry benchmarks. Ask the AE to request an "Executive Sponsor" call — frame it as standard process for deals above your ACV threshold. Simultaneously activate LinkedIn message ads targeting the CFO by title + company with a customer proof piece from a peer CFO. Avoid cold outreach — let the Champion make the introduction.

**Problem:** Evaluation is stalling — the Champion keeps saying "we just need a few more weeks" and the decision date has been pushed twice.
**Solution:** This is typically a sign of internal politics or an undisclosed competitor gaining ground. First: run a "stall diagnosis" — ask the Champion directly "What would need to be true for you to move forward in the next two weeks?" Second: trigger the red-signal rescue sequence — executive sponsor outreach, a limited-time implementation offer (e.g., "If you sign by [date], our best Solutions Engineer is available to start immediately"), and a reference call with a customer who had a similar internal dynamic. Third: analyze LinkedIn to see if any new stakeholders from the account are suddenly engaging with competitor content.

**Problem:** The technical evaluator raises a new integration concern at Week 4 that kills momentum.
**Solution:** Late-stage technical objections are often proxies for something else (fear of ownership, internal political pressure). Respond in two tracks simultaneously: (1) Deliver a technical response package within 24 hours — specific integration documentation, a reference call with your Solutions Engineering team, and a customer in the same tech stack. (2) Brief the Champion that this may be a political signal and coach them to understand what's really behind the concern. Pre-build a "Technical Risk Mitigation" one-pager as a permanent evaluation asset so this response is ready to fire in <2 hours for any deal.
