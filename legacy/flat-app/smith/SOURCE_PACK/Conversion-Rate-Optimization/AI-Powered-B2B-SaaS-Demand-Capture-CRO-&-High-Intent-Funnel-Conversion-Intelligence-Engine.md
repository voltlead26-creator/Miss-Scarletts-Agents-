## Troubleshooting

- **Problem**: Tests are running for 6+ weeks and never reaching statistical significance
  **Solution**: You don't have enough traffic per variant. Consolidate tests by running one experiment at a time on your highest-traffic page, or increase MDE threshold from 10% to 20% to require smaller sample sizes. Consider a Bayesian testing approach that doesn't require fixed sample sizes.

- **Problem**: A/B test shows a conversion rate winner, but the winning variant produces lower-quality leads (lower MQL score, worse demo show rate)
  **Solution**: This is the quality-quantity tradeoff. Add a secondary metric of MQL score or demo attendance rate to every experiment. A 25% lift in form submissions that comes with a 40% drop in SQL rate is a net negative. Consider filtering experiment cohorts to show results only for leads that match ICP firmographics.

- **Problem**: Personalization rules are conflicting — a visitor from LinkedIn Ads who is also in the retargeting pool sees inconsistent messaging
  **Solution**: Define a clear personalization priority hierarchy: (1) CRM known-user personalization wins, (2) retargeting audience rules win over new visitor rules, (3) traffic source rules apply to net new visitors only. Build this logic as a decision tree in your CMS or personalization tool before going live.
