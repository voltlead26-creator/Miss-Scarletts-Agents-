## Troubleshooting

**Problem: CS team isn't using the cross-sell playbook — expansion pipeline is all AE-sourced.**
Solution: The playbook is likely too long or requires too much judgment. Create a 1-page "Cross-Sell Cheat Sheet" per product pair with three trigger-response scripts. Add a cross-sell touch as a mandatory milestone in every Success Plan at the 90-day mark. Include CSM cross-sell pipeline as a metric in their quarterly review.

**Problem: Messaging for Product B sounds identical to Product A — customers don't see the differentiation.**
Solution: You're messaging features, not outcomes. Rebuild your Product B messages using the "before/after" framework anchored to a role-specific job-to-be-done that Product A explicitly does NOT solve. If the JTBDs overlap, you have a product positioning problem that messaging alone cannot fix — escalate to product marketing leadership.

**Problem: New economic buyer (Product B budget owner) never engages despite personalized outreach.**
Solution: You're reaching them too late — they've already started evaluating standalone competitors by the time you outreach. Move earlier: target them with thought leadership content (via LinkedIn) 3–6 months before cross-sell readiness score hits threshold. Build awareness of your platform before they're in buy mode, so your AE call is a warm continuation, not a cold introduction.
