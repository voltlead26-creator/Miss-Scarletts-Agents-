## Troubleshooting

**Problem: The ICP definition is still too broad after running this prompt — output says "B2B SaaS companies 10-500 employees"**
Solution: Re-run Section 1 with your 3 best customers described in extreme detail (what problem triggered the purchase, what their tech stack looked like, what the champion's job title was, what the day before they found you looked like). Add the instruction: "Identify what the best 3 customers have in common that the worst-fit customers don't share — this is the real ICP signal."

**Problem: The recommended GTM motion doesn't feel right — seems too resource-heavy for current team**
Solution: Add your team bandwidth constraint explicitly: "We have [X hours/week] of founder time available for GTM activities, and no budget for agency or contractor support." This forces the model to recommend the minimum viable motion, not the ideal-state motion.

**Problem: The 90-day roadmap is too vague — Week 1 says "build your ICP list" without specifics**
Solution: Add the instruction: "For each week's action item, provide: (1) the exact tool or platform to use, (2) the specific output or deliverable that constitutes 'done', (3) the time estimate in hours, and (4) the decision to be made based on that week's output." This forces operational specificity.
