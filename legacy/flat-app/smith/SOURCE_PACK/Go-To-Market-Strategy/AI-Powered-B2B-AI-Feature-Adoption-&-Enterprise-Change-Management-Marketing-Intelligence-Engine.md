## Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Low AI Readiness Assessment completion rate (<20% of accounts respond)
**Solution:** Move the assessment inside the product as a native in-app survey (Pendo or Intercom) rather than an external email link. Add a $25 charity donation incentive for completion. Have CSMs personally send the assessment link via LinkedIn DM to their primary contacts rather than relying solely on marketing email sends. Target completion rate improves 3-4x with these three changes combined.

**Problem:** IT/Security contacts are unresponsive to email outreach about AI feature adoption
**Solution:** Stop emailing them about features — switch the message to a security partnership frame. Lead with your SOC 2 Type II report, your AI data processing addendum, and a "Security Architecture Briefing" calendar invite (not a sales call). IT leaders respond to peer-level technical conversations. Consider hosting a private "CISO Roundtable" webinar with 3-4 peer IT leaders from reference customers who can speak credibly about their security evaluation process. Response rates for this format typically run 4-6x higher than feature-focused email.

**Problem:** Internal champions are enthusiastic but can't get budget/permission to activate AI features for their team
**Solution:** The champion has a desire problem but an authority problem — shift your marketing support to help them build the internal business case rather than teaching them to use the feature. Provide a customizable "AI Activation Business Case" deck template with pre-filled ROI calculations for their industry and company size. Include an email template they can forward to their manager and IT director. Build a 10-minute "AI Activation for Your Leadership Team" video they can share without scheduling a call. Champions who receive this enablement kit convert their accounts at 2.3x the rate of champions left to self-serve the internal approval process.
