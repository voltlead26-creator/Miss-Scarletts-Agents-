## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Buyers keep asking "is this actually AI or just automation?"**
Cause: Positioning leads with AI technology, not AI outcomes. Also common if competitors have trained buyers to be skeptical.
Fix: Rewrite all external messaging to lead with the business outcome achieved without human effort ("40% of deals saved automatically") and only mention AI when explaining the mechanism. Add a "How Pipeline Prophet works" explainer that describes what the AI reads, decides, and does in plain English — no ML terminology.

**Issue: Enterprise procurement is blocking deals over AI governance concerns**
Cause: Trust architecture is underdeveloped or not surfaced early enough in the sales process.
Fix: Add a "Trust & Security FAQ" as a deal asset surfaced at first demo — cover: data residency, model training (is their data used to train?), human override controls, audit logs, and EU AI Act compliance tier. Schedule a dedicated IT/security call in the evaluation process rather than waiting for procurement to raise concerns.

**Issue: The "aha moment" isn't landing — prospects stay skeptical through the demo**
Cause: Demo is showing features rather than demonstrating autonomous AI value in real time.
Fix: Restructure demo to open with a live example of the AI taking a real action (not a static screenshot) — ideally using a sanitized version of the prospect's own data or a live sandbox that mirrors their CRM workflow. The aha moment must show the AI detecting a problem AND taking action, not just flagging a problem for a human to act on.
